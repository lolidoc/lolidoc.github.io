Question and Answer

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly hair every day?
A: It is generally not recommended to straighten your hair every day, as excessive heat can cause damage and dryness. It's best to give your hair regular breaks from heat styling and use heat protectant products when straightening.

Q: How do I prevent frizz when using a straightener?
A: To prevent frizz when using a straightener, make sure your hair is completely dry before styling, use a heat protectant spray, and finish with a smoothing serum or oil.

Q: How often should I clean my straightener?
A: It is recommended to clean your straightener every few weeks to remove any product buildup. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for cleaning and maintenance.

Q: Can I use a straightener on wet hair?
A: No, it is not safe to use a straightener on wet hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a straightener to avoid damage.
