Tips for Using the Best Drugstore Conditioner for Aging Hair

To get the most out of your best drugstore conditioner for aging hair, follow these tips:

1. Start with clean, wet hair

Before applying the conditioner, make sure your hair is clean and wet. This will help the conditioner to penetrate the hair shaft and provide maximum hydration.

2. Use the right amount

Use the recommended amount of conditioner for your hair length and thickness. Applying too much can weigh down the hair and make it appear greasy.

3. Focus on the ends

Concentrate the conditioner on the ends of your hair, as this is where it tends to be driest and most prone to breakage. Avoid applying too much conditioner to the roots, as this can lead to a greasy scalp.

4. Leave it on for a few minutes

Allow the conditioner to sit on your hair for a few minutes before rinsing. This will give it time to penetrate the hair shaft and provide maximum hydration.
