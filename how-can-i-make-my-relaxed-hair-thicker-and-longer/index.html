How Can I Make My Relaxed Hair Thicker and Longer: Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you make your relaxed hair thicker and longer:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. This will help prevent breakage and minimize damage.

2. Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can cause damage and breakage. If you must use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

3. Incorporate hair masks or deep conditioning treatments into your hair care routine. These treatments can help hydrate and nourish your hair, promoting growth and thickness.

4. Protect your hair while sleeping by using a satin or silk pillowcase or wrapping your hair in a satin or silk scarf. This will help prevent friction and minimize breakage.
