A: While cutting hair cannot directly prevent or reverse hair loss, it can create the illusion of thicker and fuller hair. By using cutting techniques that add volume and movement, a stylist can help senior women achieve a hairstyle that boosts their confidence and makes their hair appear thicker.

Q: Are there any specific products or tools recommended for cutting senior women's hair?
