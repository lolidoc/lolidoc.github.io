Are you tired of spending hours trying to style your medium thick wavy hair every morning? Do you struggle to find easy hairstyles that work with your natural waves? If so, you're not alone. Many women with medium thick wavy hair face the same challenges when it comes to styling their locks. But fear not, because in this blog post, we'll explore some easy hairstyles that are perfect for medium thick wavy hair, and help you achieve a gorgeous look in no time.

Medium thick wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it has volume and texture, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Finding hairstyles that are easy to do and still look great can be a challenge. But with the right techniques and a little bit of practice, you can create stunning hairstyles that showcase your natural waves and make you feel confident and beautiful.

So, what are some easy hairstyles for medium thick wavy hair? Let's dive in and explore a few options.

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a timeless and effortless hairstyle that works well with medium thick wavy hair. To achieve this look, start by applying a salt spray or texturizing spray to damp hair. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage natural waves, and let it air dry or use a diffuser to speed up the process. Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling wand or a flat iron to touch up any areas that need a little extra definition. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a versatile option that works well with medium thick wavy hair. To create this look, start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom. Use a comb or your fingers to create a clean part, and gather the top section into a ponytail or a bun. You can leave a few face-framing pieces loose for a softer look. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or a special occasion.

3. Braided Crown

The braided crown hairstyle is a boho-inspired look that is perfect for medium thick wavy hair. To achieve this style, start by parting your hair down the middle and creating two equal sections. Take a small section of hair from one side and begin braiding it towards the back of your head. Once you reach the back, secure the braid with a bobby pin. Repeat on the other side. Once both braids are secure, you can gently pull on the edges to create a fuller and more voluminous look. Finish with hairspray to keep everything in place.

4. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that works well for medium thick wavy hair. To create this look, start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape, and secure it with bobby pins. Pull on the edges of the bun to create a messy and undone look. You can also leave a few pieces of hair loose around your face for a softer and more romantic look. This hairstyle is perfect for a lazy day or when you're short on time.

Conclusion

Styling medium thick wavy hair doesn't have to be a challenge. With the right techniques and a little bit of practice, you can create easy and beautiful hairstyles that showcase your natural waves. Whether you prefer beachy waves, a half-up half-down style, a braided crown, or a messy bun, there are plenty of options to choose from. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you and your hair type. Embrace your natural waves and have fun styling your medium thick wavy hair!
