What Hairstyles Suit Me: Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I determine my face shape?

To determine your face shape, start by pulling your hair back and looking in the mirror. Pay attention to the widest part of your face and the shape of your jawline. Common face shapes include round, square, oval, heart, and diamond.

2. Can I pull off a short hairstyle if I have a round face?

Absolutely! While there are some general guidelines for different face shapes, it's important to remember that personal style and confidence play a big role in pulling off any hairstyle. If you feel confident and beautiful in a short hairstyle, go for it!

3. What should I do if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you're not happy with your new hairstyle, don't panic. Hair grows back, and there are always ways to style and change your hair to make it more to your liking. Communicate openly with your hairstylist and work together to find a solution.

4. How often should I change my hairstyle?

There are no set rules for how often you should change your hairstyle. It ultimately depends on your personal preferences and how often you feel the need for a change. Some people prefer to stick with one style for years, while others like to switch it up every few months.
