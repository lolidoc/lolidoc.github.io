To cut older men's hair with scissors only, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair thoroughly. Wet hair is easier to work with and allows for more precision. Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using hair clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work on one section at a time and ensure even cutting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin by trimming the hair at the desired length. Hold the scissors vertically and make small cuts, gradually working your way around the head. Take your time and be cautious not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After cutting a section, release it and check for any unevenness. If needed, make small adjustments to achieve a more balanced look. Remember to keep the scissors vertical for a precise cut.

In summary, to cut older men's hair with scissors only, follow these steps: prepare the hair, section it, start cutting, and check for evenness. Taking your time and being patient will ensure a satisfactory result.
