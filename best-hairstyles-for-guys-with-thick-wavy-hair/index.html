Are you a guy with thick wavy hair? Do you struggle to find the best hairstyles that work well with your hair type? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore the best hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair and give you some tips on how to style and manage your waves.

Having thick wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, your hair has natural volume and texture, which can create a unique and stylish look. On the other hand, it can be difficult to control and style, leading to frizz and unruly waves. But fear not, there are plenty of hairstyles that can enhance your thick wavy hair and make you look your best.

When it comes to choosing the best hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair, it's important to consider your personal style, face shape, and hair length. Whether you prefer a short, medium, or long hairstyle, there are options that will suit you.

Short hairstyles like the buzz cut or the textured crop are great choices for guys with thick wavy hair. These styles help to manage the volume and keep your hair looking neat and tidy. For those with medium-length hair, a classic pompadour or a messy quiff can add some flair and definition to your waves. And if you're blessed with long hair, consider a loose and layered style or a man bun to showcase your waves.

Best Hairstyles for Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

Now let's dive deeper into some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair:

1. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a popular choice for guys with thick wavy hair. This style keeps your hair short and low-maintenance, while still allowing your natural waves to show. It's a great option for those who want a clean and polished look.

To achieve the buzz cut, ask your barber to use a shorter guard on the clippers and cut your hair evenly all over. This will help to control the volume and keep your waves in check. You can also experiment with different lengths to find the perfect buzz cut for your face shape.

2. The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that works well with thick wavy hair. This style adds texture and definition to your waves, creating a messy yet stylish look. It's a great option for those who want to embrace their natural hair texture.

To achieve the textured crop, ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and back, while leaving some length on top. This will create contrast and allow your waves to stand out. You can also use a texturizing product to enhance the texture and hold of your hair.

3. The Classic Pompadour

If you're looking for a more polished and formal hairstyle, the classic pompadour is a great choice. This style adds height and volume to your hair, while keeping your waves under control. It's a timeless and sophisticated look that works well for both casual and formal occasions.

To achieve the classic pompadour, ask your barber to cut your hair shorter on the sides and back, while leaving more length on top. This will allow you to style your waves into a pompadour shape. Use a strong-hold pomade or gel to slick back the sides and create height on top.

4. The Messy Quiff

If you prefer a more casual and effortless look, the messy quiff is a great option. This style adds volume and texture to your hair, while giving you a laid-back and cool vibe. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

To achieve the messy quiff, ask your barber to cut your hair shorter on the sides and back, while leaving some length on top. This will allow you to style your waves into a messy and textured quiff. Use a styling product with medium hold to add texture and hold to your hair.

5. The Loose and Layered Style

If you have long hair and want to embrace your waves, the loose and layered style is a great choice. This style adds movement and dimension to your hair, while allowing your waves to flow freely. It's a low-maintenance and natural look that works well for those with thick wavy hair.

To achieve the loose and layered style, ask your barber to add layers to your hair, focusing on the ends to create movement. This will help to reduce bulk and enhance your natural waves. Use a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to define and control your waves.

Tips for Styling and Managing Thick Wavy Hair

Now that you know some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair, here are some tips to help you style and manage your waves:

1. Use the right products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for thick wavy hair. Look for products that provide moisture, definition, and hold to help control your waves and reduce frizz. Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

2. Don't overwash your hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip away its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Instead, try to wash your hair every other day or every few days to maintain its natural moisture and texture. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

3. Embrace the natural texture

Instead of fighting against your waves, embrace them and work with them. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair and enhance its natural texture. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

4. Get regular trims

Regular trims are essential for maintaining healthy and manageable hair. Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking fresh. This will also help to prevent your hair from becoming too heavy and weighed down.

Conclusion of Best Hairstyles for Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

Having thick wavy hair doesn't mean you have to settle for a boring hairstyle. There are plenty of options that can enhance your natural waves and make you look your best. Whether you prefer a short, medium, or long hairstyle, there is a style that will suit your personal style and face shape. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the ones that work best for you. Embrace your waves and rock your thick wavy hair with confidence!
