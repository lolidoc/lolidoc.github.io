Best Short Hairstyles with Beard: The Perfect Combination

One of the most popular short hairstyles with a beard is the classic fade haircut. This hairstyle features short sides and back, with longer hair on top that is gradually tapered. The fade haircut works well with a beard as it creates a clean and polished look, while the beard adds a touch of ruggedness and masculinity. To achieve this style, ask your barber for a high or low fade, depending on your preference, and ensure that the length on top is long enough to style.

Another popular option is the undercut hairstyle with a beard. This style features shaved or closely cropped sides and back, with longer hair on top that is usually styled into a slicked-back or textured look. The undercut hairstyle is a great choice if you want to achieve a modern and edgy look, while the beard adds a touch of masculinity and depth. To achieve this style, ask your barber for a disconnected undercut with a fade on the sides and back, and leave the length on top long enough to style.

For those who prefer a more casual and laid-back look, the textured crop hairstyle with a beard is a great option. This style features short and textured hair all over, with a slightly longer fringe at the front. The textured crop hairstyle is perfect if you want a low-maintenance yet stylish look, while the beard adds a touch of ruggedness and masculinity. To achieve this style, ask your barber for a textured crop with a short fringe, and ensure that the hair is slightly longer at the front for added dimension.

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I still rock a short hairstyle with a beard if I have thinning hair?

A: Absolutely! There are many short hairstyles that can work well with thinning hair, such as the buzz cut or the crew cut. Pair it with a beard to add depth and dimension to your look.

Q: How do I maintain my short hairstyle and beard?

A: Regular trims and grooming are key to maintaining your short hairstyle and beard. Invest in quality hair products and beard oils to keep them looking their best.

Q: Can I grow a beard if I have patchy facial hair?

A: Yes, you can still grow a beard if you have patchy facial hair. Opt for a shorter beard style, such as a stubble or a goatee, to minimize the appearance of patchiness.
