How to Style Hairstyles for Over 50 with Round Face and Curly Hair

Styling hairstyles for over 50 with round face and curly hair can be relatively simple with the right techniques. Here are a few steps to follow:

1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair to enhance your natural curl pattern.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your curls gently. This will help to prevent frizz and maintain the shape of your curls.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose or frizzy curls. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before styling.

5. Finish with a hairspray or lightweight styling cream to hold your curls in place and add shine.
