Hairstyles For Guys With Long Thick Wavy Hair

Hairstyles for Guys with Long Thick Wavy Hair

Introduction

Having long, thick, and wavy hair can be both a blessing and a challenge for guys. While it allows for versatile styling options, it can also be difficult to manage and style on a daily basis. However, with the right techniques and hairstyles, you can embrace your natural hair and create a stylish look that suits your personality. In this article, we will explore some trendy hairstyles for guys with long, thick, wavy hair in the year 2023.

1. The Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle for guys with long, thick, wavy hair. This style involves keeping the hair on the top long and letting the waves flow freely, while the sides and back are cut short or shaved. This creates a stylish contrast and adds definition to your overall look. To maintain this style, regular trims are necessary to keep the sides and back tidy.

2. The Man Bun

The man bun is a classic hairstyle that works well for guys with long, thick, wavy hair. This style involves gathering all of your hair at the crown of your head and tying it into a bun. It not only keeps your hair out of your face but also adds a touch of sophistication to your appearance. You can experiment with different variations of the man bun, such as a low bun or a half-up half-down style.

3. The Messy Textured Crop

If you prefer a more laid-back and effortless look, the messy textured crop is a great choice. This hairstyle involves cutting your hair into short layers and adding texture to enhance the natural wave pattern. To style, simply apply some texturizing product to damp hair and tousle it with your fingers for a messy yet stylish finish. This hairstyle is low-maintenance and perfect for guys who want a casual and trendy look.

4. The Long and Loose

If you want to embrace the full length of your hair and let your waves shine, the long and loose style is a great option. This hairstyle involves growing your hair out and keeping it long and loose. Regular trims are essential to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance. To enhance the natural waves, you can use a curl-enhancing product or scrunch your hair with your hands while it's damp.

5. The Side Swept

The side swept hairstyle is a versatile choice for guys with long, thick, wavy hair. This style involves sweeping your hair to one side and letting it flow naturally. You can use a styling product to add texture and hold to your waves, or simply let them fall freely. This style works well for both formal and casual occasions and can be easily achieved with a comb or your fingers.

6. The Tapered Fade

If you prefer a more modern and edgy look, the tapered fade is worth considering. This hairstyle involves gradually fading the hair on the sides and back while keeping the top long and wavy. The contrast between the short sides and the long, thick waves creates a bold and stylish appearance. Regular trims are necessary to maintain the faded sides and to prevent the hair from becoming too unruly.

7. The Surfer's Flow

The surfer's flow is a popular hairstyle for guys with long, thick, wavy hair who want a carefree and beachy vibe. This style involves growing your hair out and letting it flow naturally. You can add some salt spray or texturizing product to enhance the beachy texture and create a windswept look. This hairstyle is perfect for guys who want a relaxed and effortless appearance.

8. The Braided Man Bun

If you want to add some flair to your man bun, consider incorporating braids into the style. The braided man bun involves braiding a section of your hair before tying it into a bun. This adds a unique and stylish element to your hairstyle and allows you to showcase your creativity. You can experiment with different braid styles, such as a French braid or a fishtail braid, to create a personalized look.

9. The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that works well for guys with long, thick, wavy hair. This style involves sweeping the hair back and away from the face, while keeping the top voluminous and wavy. You can use a styling product to add height and hold to the pompadour, or simply rely on the natural volume of your waves. This hairstyle exudes confidence and sophistication.

10. The Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a trendy and versatile choice for guys with long, thick, wavy hair. This style involves gathering the top half of your hair and tying it into a small ponytail or bun, while leaving the rest of the hair loose. This creates a stylish and effortless look that can be worn for various occasions. You can experiment with different placement and styling options to find the perfect half-up half-down style for you.

Conclusion

When it comes to hairstyles for guys with long, thick, wavy hair, the options are endless. Whether you prefer a clean and polished look or a more relaxed and casual vibe, there is a hairstyle that suits your taste and personality. By embracing your natural hair texture and experimenting with different styles, you can achieve a stylish and confident appearance that will turn heads in the year 2023.
