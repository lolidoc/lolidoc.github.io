Best Products for Aging Hair 2019: History and Myth

Throughout history, people have been searching for ways to combat the effects of aging on their hair. From ancient civilizations to modern times, there have been countless remedies and potions claiming to reverse the signs of aging and restore youthful vitality to the hair. While many of these claims may be exaggerated or unfounded, there is some truth to the idea that certain ingredients and treatments can help to improve the health and appearance of aging hair.

One common myth about aging hair is that cutting it regularly will help it grow faster. While regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, they do not actually affect the rate of hair growth. Hair grows from the hair follicles in the scalp, not the ends, so cutting the ends will not make it grow faster. However, keeping the ends of the hair trimmed can help to prevent further damage and breakage, which can make the hair appear thicker and fuller.
