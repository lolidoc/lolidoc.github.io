Layered Haircuts for Thick Curly Hair with Bangs: How to Style and Maintain

Styling and maintaining layered haircuts for thick curly hair with bangs can be relatively easy with the right products and techniques. Here are a few tips:

1. Start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for curly hair to keep your locks hydrated and frizz-free.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz. Scrunch your hair gently with your hands to encourage the curls to form.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume.

4. Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling wand or curling iron to touch up any loose curls or add more definition to your bangs.

5. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to set your style and keep your curls in place throughout the day.
