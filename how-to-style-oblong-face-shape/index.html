Are you struggling to find the right hairstyles for your oblong face shape? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people with oblong faces find it challenging to style their hair in a way that enhances their features. But fear not, because in this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style oblong face shape to help you look your best.

One of the biggest pain points for people with oblong face shapes is finding a hairstyle that doesn't elongate their faces even more. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds width and volume to the sides of your face, balancing out its length.

So, how can you style an oblong face shape? The first step is to consider the length of your hair. If you have long hair, try adding layers or waves to create the illusion of width. Shorter hairstyles with volume on the sides, such as bobs or pixie cuts, can also work well for oblong faces.

In summary, when it comes to styling an oblong face shape, the goal is to add width and volume to the sides of your face to balance out its length. This can be achieved through layering, waves, or shorter hairstyles with volume. Now, let's dive deeper into how to style oblong face shape and explore some specific techniques and recommendations.

How to Style Oblong Face Shape: Techniques and Recommendations

When it comes to styling an oblong face shape, there are several techniques and recommendations that can help enhance your features and flatter your face shape. Let's take a closer look.

1. Haircuts and Hairstyles

The right haircut and hairstyle can make a world of difference for oblong face shapes. Here are some recommendations:

  • Layered cuts: Adding layers to your hair can create movement and volume, making your face appear wider.
  • Side-swept bangs: Bangs that are swept to the side can help break up the length of your face and add width.
  • Bob hairstyles: Bobs that hit at or above the chin can be flattering for oblong faces, especially when they have some volume on the sides.
  • Pixie cuts: Shorter hairstyles with volume on the sides can also work well for oblong faces.

Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you. Consider consulting with a hairstylist who can provide personalized recommendations based on your face shape and features.

2. Volume and Texture

Adding volume and texture to your hair can help create the illusion of width and balance out the length of your face. Here are some tips:

  • Use volumizing products: Try using volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products to add body and lift to your hair.
  • Teasing or backcombing: Gently teasing or backcombing the roots of your hair can create volume and height at the crown.
  • Curl or wave your hair: Adding curls or waves to your hair can also help create the appearance of width and add texture.

Play around with different styling techniques and products to achieve the desired volume and texture for your hair.

3. Parting and Styling

The way you part and style your hair can have a significant impact on how it frames your face. Here are some tips:

  • Side part: A deep side part can help create asymmetry and add width to your face.
  • Soft, face-framing layers: Opt for soft, face-framing layers that can help soften the angles of your face and add dimension.
  • Avoid center parts: Center parts can elongate the face, so it's best to avoid them if you have an oblong face shape.

Experiment with different parting and styling techniques to find what suits you best. Don't be afraid to try new things and have fun with your hair!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oblong face shape?

A: Absolutely! Straight hair can look great on oblong face shapes. Just make sure to add volume and width to the sides of your face to create balance.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have an oblong face shape?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, hairstyles that add length to your face, such as long, straight hair, may not be the most flattering. It's best to opt for styles that add width and volume instead.

Q: How can I make my face appear shorter if I have an oblong face shape?

A: Adding volume and width to the sides of your face can help create the illusion of a shorter face. Consider trying layered cuts or hairstyles with volume on the sides.

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, you can! Side-swept bangs can be particularly flattering for oblong faces, as they help break up the length of the face and add width.

Conclusion of How to Style Oblong Face Shape

Styling an oblong face shape doesn't have to be a challenge. By choosing the right haircut, adding volume and texture, and playing with different parting and styling techniques, you can enhance your features and flatter your face shape. Remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin. Embrace your unique features and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles!
