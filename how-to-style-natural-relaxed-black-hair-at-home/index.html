How To Style Natural Relaxed Black Hair At Home

Written by Vannesa Dec 08, 2022 ¬∑ 3 min read

Having natural relaxed black hair can be a beautiful and versatile canvas for various hairstyles Whether you prefer sleek and straight voluminous curls or elegant updos there are plenty of styling options that you can try at home In this article we will provide you with tips and techniques to style your natural relaxed black hair effortlessly

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having natural relaxed black hair can be a beautiful and versatile canvas for various hairstyles. Whether you prefer sleek and straight, voluminous curls, or elegant updos, there are plenty of styling options that you can try at home. In this article, we will provide you with tips and techniques to style your natural relaxed black hair effortlessly.

Preparation

Before styling your hair, it is essential to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to nourish and hydrate your strands. Afterward, apply a heat protectant product to shield your hair from heat damage.

Sleek and Straight

If you desire a sleek and straight look, you can achieve it by using a flat iron. Begin by sectioning your hair and working with small sections at a time. Apply a heat protectant spray and carefully glide the flat iron down each section, starting from the roots to the ends. Remember to use a low or medium heat setting to prevent excessive heat damage.

Voluminous Curls

To create voluminous curls, you can use flexi rods or perm rods. Start by applying a curl-enhancing product to damp hair. Then, take small sections of hair and wrap them around the rods, starting from the ends and rolling them up towards the roots. Leave the rods in overnight or use a hooded dryer to speed up the drying process. Once your hair is completely dry, carefully remove the rods to reveal beautiful bouncy curls.

Elegant Updos

For an elegant updo, you can try a sleek bun or a stylish top knot. Begin by brushing your hair upwards and gathering it into a high ponytail. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie and twist the length of the ponytail into a neat bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place and smooth any flyaways with a light holding gel or hairspray.

Maintenance and Care

To maintain your styled hair, it is crucial to protect it while you sleep. Consider wrapping your hair in a satin or silk scarf or using a satin pillowcase to prevent friction and minimize frizz. Additionally, avoid excessive heat styling and use heat protectant products whenever you do use heat tools.

Conclusion

Styling natural relaxed black hair at home can be a fun and creative process. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of looks that suit your personal style. Remember to take proper care of your hair to maintain its health and integrity. Experiment with different styles and have fun embracing the versatility of your natural relaxed black hair!
