The Hidden Secret of Choosing Colors for Medium Skin Tone

The secret to choosing the right color for medium skin tone lies in understanding your undertone. As mentioned earlier, medium skin tones can have warm or cool undertones. Determining your undertone can help you choose colors that will complement and enhance your complexion.
