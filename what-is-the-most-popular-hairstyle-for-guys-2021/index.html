The Most Popular Hairstyle for Guys 2021: Explained

The textured crop is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It is characterized by short sides and back, with longer hair on top that is styled with texture and movement. This hairstyle offers a modern and stylish look, while still being versatile enough to suit different occasions and outfits.

The textured crop is a great option for guys with straight or wavy hair, as it allows them to embrace their natural texture. It is also suitable for guys with thin or fine hair, as the added texture creates the illusion of thickness. For those with thick or curly hair, the textured crop can be a bit more challenging to achieve, but with the right styling products and techniques, it can still be a great option.

One of the reasons why the textured crop has become so popular is its versatility. It can be styled in various ways, depending on your personal preference and the look you want to achieve. You can wear it messy and tousled for a more casual and relaxed vibe, or you can style it with more structure and definition for a polished and put-together look.

Another reason why the textured crop is so popular is its low-maintenance nature. It requires minimal styling and can easily be maintained with regular trims to keep the sides and back short. The longer hair on top can be styled with a bit of product, such as a pomade or wax, to add texture and hold. This makes it a great option for guys who don't want to spend a lot of time on their hair each day.

Tips for the Most Popular Hairstyle for Guys 2021

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling the textured crop:

1. Start with clean, dry hair: The textured crop works best on clean, dry hair. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and make sure it is completely dry before styling.

2. Use a comb or your fingers to create texture: To achieve the textured look, use a comb or your fingers to create separation and texture in your hair. You can also use a bit of product to enhance the texture and hold.

3. Experiment with different partings: The textured crop can be styled with a side part, a middle part, or no part at all. Try different partings to see which one suits you best and complements your face shape.

4. Don't be afraid to trim: Regular trims are essential to maintain the shape and structure of the textured crop. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep the sides and back short and tidy.
