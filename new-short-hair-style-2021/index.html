Tips for Maintaining the New Short Hair Style 2021

While the new short hair style 2021 is low-maintenance compared to long hair, it still requires some care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining your short hair style:

1. Regular trims:

To keep your short hair style looking fresh and healthy, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair in good condition.

2. Use the right products:

Short hair requires different products than long hair. Look for styling products that are specifically designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays, pomades, and waxes. These products can help add volume, texture, and hold to your short hair style.

3. Experiment with styling:

One of the fun things about short hair is that you can experiment with different styles and looks. Play around with different techniques, such as curling, straightening, or adding accessories, to create a variety of different looks.

4. Protect your hair:

Just because your hair is short doesn't mean you can skip the heat protectant. Before using any heat styling tools, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
