Question and Answer

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! Long hair can still look great on round faces. Just make sure to choose a haircut that adds angles and lengthens your face, such as layered haircuts or a side-swept fringe.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face and long hair?

A: Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear even rounder. Also, steer clear of hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can widen your features.

Q: Can I wear a middle part with a round face and long hair?

A: While a middle part can work for some round-faced individuals, it is generally recommended to opt for a side part. A side part can create the illusion of a narrower face and complement a round face shape.

Q: How often should I trim my hair if I have a round face and long hair?

A: The frequency of hair trims depends on your hair's health and how quickly it grows. On average, it is recommended to get a trim every 8-12 weeks to maintain the health and shape of your haircut.
