Tips for Short Hairstyles 2020 Female Over 50 with Round Face

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling short hairstyles for women over 50 with a round face:

1. Add Volume on Top: To create a slimming effect, add volume on top of your head. Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add lift and height.

2. Avoid Heavy Bangs: Heavy bangs can make a round face appear even rounder. Opt for side-swept bangs or shorter bangs that frame your face.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture: Short hairstyles are all about embracing your natural texture. If you have curly hair, let your curls shine. If you have straight hair, add some texture with a curling iron or texturizing spray.

4. Play with Layers: Layers can add movement and volume to your hair. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that frame your face and create a more elongated shape.
