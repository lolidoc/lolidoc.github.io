Tips for Short Hairstyles for Over 50 and Overweight

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling short hairstyles for over 50 and overweight:

2. Add volume and texture

Short hairstyles for over 50 and overweight can benefit from added volume and texture. Use volumizing products and styling techniques to add height and fullness to your hair. This will make your hair appear fuller and more youthful.

3. Consider your hair texture

Take into consideration your hair texture when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have fine hair, opt for styles that add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, consider styles that thin out the hair and reduce bulk.
