One of the first steps I took to dress trendy over 60 was to declutter my wardrobe and get rid of any outdated or ill-fitting pieces. When it comes to choosing trendy pieces, I like to stay informed about current fashion trends through magazines, blogs, and social media. Another tip for dressing trendy over 60 is to invest in quality pieces that will stand the test of time. Instead of chasing every fleeting trend, I focus on building a versatile wardrobe with timeless basics such as well-fitting jeans, tailored blazers, and classic accessories. These pieces can be easily mixed and matched with trendier items to create stylish and age-appropriate outfits.
