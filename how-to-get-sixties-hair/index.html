Are you looking to achieve that iconic 60s hairstyle? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will guide you on how to get sixties hair. Whether you have a special event or simply want to embrace the retro look, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the perfect 60s hairstyle.

If you're struggling with flat, lifeless hair or want to add some volume and texture to your locks, then learning how to get sixties hair is the answer. The 60s was known for its big, glamorous hairstyles that exuded confidence and style. By following these steps, you'll be able to recreate that iconic look and turn heads wherever you go.

So, how exactly do you achieve the perfect sixties hair? It all starts with the right products and techniques. First, start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and lift. Next, apply a root-lifting spray or mousse to the roots of your hair, focusing on the crown area. This will give your hair that extra boost of volume.

Once your hair is prepped and ready, it's time to start styling. Begin by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. For extra lift, you can also use Velcro rollers or hot rollers to set your hair. Leave them in for a few minutes to cool before removing.

How to Get Sixties Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have the basics down, let's dive into a step-by-step guide on how to get sixties hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add body and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply Products

After washing your hair, apply a root-lifting spray or mousse to the roots of your hair. This will give your hair that extra volume and lift.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair to add volume and shape. Focus on lifting the roots and creating a smooth, polished look.

Step 4: Set Your Hair

If you want extra volume, use Velcro rollers or hot rollers to set your hair. Leave them in for a few minutes to cool before removing.

Step 5: Tease and Style

To achieve that classic 60s look, tease the crown of your hair to create height and volume. Gently backcomb small sections of hair at the roots and smooth out the top layer with a comb or brush. Finish off by styling your hair into your desired shape, whether it's a bouffant, beehive, or sleek updo.

And there you have it - the steps to achieving that iconic sixties hair! By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to recreate the glamorous styles of the 60s and embrace your inner retro queen. So don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and have fun with your hair!

Conclusion of How to Get Sixties Hair

Getting sixties hair may seem daunting at first, but with the right products and techniques, it's easier than you think. By following our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to achieve that iconic 60s hairstyle and turn heads wherever you go. So go ahead, embrace the retro look and rock that sixties hair!
