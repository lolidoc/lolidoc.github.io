Tips for Using the Best Shampoo for Thick Oily Hair

Here are some tips to get the most out of your best shampoo for thick oily hair:

  1. Use lukewarm water to rinse your hair before applying the shampoo.
  2. Massage the shampoo into the scalp using gentle circular motions.
  3. Leave the shampoo on for a few minutes to allow the ingredients to work their magic.
  4. Rinse thoroughly with cool water to seal the hair cuticles and add shine.
  5. Follow up with a lightweight conditioner on the lengths and ends of your hair.
