Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair Style for a Round Face

Here are a few tips for maintaining your short hair style for a round face:

1. Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for maintaining the shape and integrity of your short hair style. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products for your hair type and texture is important for maintaining your short hair style. Look for products that add volume and texture, and avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

3. Experiment with Styling Techniques

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to change up your look. Try using a curling iron or straightener to create different textures and styles, or use a volumizing spray to add height and volume to your hair.

4. Embrace Your Natural Texture
