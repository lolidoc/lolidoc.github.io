How to Use the Best Heated Rollers for Short Hair UK

Using the best heated rollers for short hair UK is simple and straightforward. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the desired results:

1. Start with clean, dry hair. It is best to wash your hair and allow it to air dry before using heated rollers.

2. Divide your hair into sections. For short hair, you may only need to divide your hair into two or three sections.

3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a heated roller. Secure the roller in place with a clip or pin.

4. Repeat with the remaining sections of hair, working your way from the bottom to the top of your head.

5. Allow the rollers to cool completely before removing them. This will help to set the curls and ensure that they last all day long.

6. Once the rollers are cooled, carefully remove them from your hair.

7. Use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to gently separate and shape the curls.

8. Finish with a light hold hairspray to help the curls last throughout the day.
