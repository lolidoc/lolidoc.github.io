2. Long Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A long bob, also known as a lob, is a versatile and stylish option for women over 50 with round faces. Pairing this hairstyle with side-swept bangs can help to create a more angular and elongated appearance. The length of the bob can be customized to suit your preferences, ranging from just above the shoulders to below the collarbone.

To achieve beachy waves, you can use a curling iron or opt for a heatless method such as braiding damp hair and allowing it to dry overnight.
