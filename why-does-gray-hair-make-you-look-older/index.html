Why does gray hair make you look older?

Finally, gray hair can also make the underlying skin tone appear paler, further contributing to an aged appearance.

A: Currently, there is no scientifically proven method to reverse gray hair. Q: Does plucking a gray hair make more gray hairs grow back?

A: The common myth that plucking a gray hair will cause more gray hairs to grow back is not true. Each hair follicle produces only one hair, and plucking a hair will not affect the production of future hairs. However, repeated plucking can damage the hair follicle and potentially lead to hair loss.
