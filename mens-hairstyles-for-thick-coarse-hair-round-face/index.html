Are you a man with thick, coarse hair and a round face? Finding the right hairstyle to flatter your features can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we will explore the best mens hairstyles for thick coarse hair round face. From classic cuts to modern styles, there's something for everyone. So, let's dive in and discover the perfect hairstyle for you.

Men with thick, coarse hair and a round face often struggle with finding a hairstyle that suits their unique features. Thick hair can be difficult to manage and style, while a round face shape requires a cut that adds length and definition. This combination can make it challenging to find a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type.

When it comes to mens hairstyles for thick coarse hair round face, the key is to choose a style that adds height and volume to the top of the head. This helps to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more balanced shape. Additionally, opting for shorter sides and back can help to slim down the face and create a more defined jawline.

In summary, the best mens hairstyles for thick coarse hair round face should have height and volume on top, with shorter sides and back to create balance and definition. Now, let's explore some specific hairstyles that fit these criteria.

Mens Hairstyles for Thick Coarse Hair Round Face

One popular hairstyle for men with thick coarse hair and a round face is the classic pompadour. This style features short sides and back, with longer hair on top that is swept back and styled with height. The pompadour adds volume to the top of the head, creating the illusion of a longer face shape. This style can be achieved with a good quality pomade or styling product.

Another option for men with thick coarse hair and a round face is the textured crop. This style is characterized by short, textured hair on top with even shorter sides and back. The textured crop adds height and volume to the top of the head, while the short sides and back create a more defined jawline. This style is low maintenance and can be styled with a matte styling product for a natural, textured look.

If you prefer a longer hairstyle, the messy fringe is a great option for men with thick coarse hair and a round face. This style features longer hair on top that is styled into a messy, textured fringe. The messy fringe adds height and volume to the top of the head, while the longer length helps to elongate the face. This style can be achieved with a texturizing spray or wax for added texture and hold.

For a more polished look, the side part is a classic choice for men with thick coarse hair and a round face. This style features a deep side part with longer hair on top that is slicked to one side. The side part adds height and volume to the top of the head, while the slicked back sides create a clean and polished look. This style can be achieved with a good quality pomade or styling gel for a sleek, sophisticated finish.

Conclusion

Finding the right hairstyle for men with thick coarse hair and a round face can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. By choosing a style that adds height and volume to the top of the head, with shorter sides and back, you can create a balanced and flattering look. Whether you prefer a classic pompadour, a textured crop, a messy fringe, or a sleek side part, there's a hairstyle out there that will suit your unique features. So, experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and stylish.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still wear longer hairstyles if I have thick coarse hair and a round face?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear longer hairstyles if you have thick coarse hair and a round face. Just make sure to add height and volume to the top of the head to create a more elongated face shape. Consider styles like the messy fringe or the side part, which add height and length to the face.

Q: How can I style my thick coarse hair to add volume?

A: To add volume to your thick coarse hair, try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Additionally, blow-drying your hair upside down can help to add lift and volume at the roots. Finally, use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture and hold to your hairstyle.

Q: Are there any hairstyles to avoid if I have thick coarse hair and a round face?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to hairstyles, there are certain styles that may not be as flattering for men with thick coarse hair and a round face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short all over, as this can make the face appear rounder. Additionally, hairstyles with too much volume on the sides can also make the face look wider.

Q: Can I still wear a beard with mens hairstyles for thick coarse hair round face?

A: Absolutely! A well-groomed beard can complement mens hairstyles for thick coarse hair round face. Consider a shorter beard style, such as a stubble or a short boxed beard, to create a more defined jawline and balance out the roundness of the face.

Conclusion of mens hairstyles for thick coarse hair round face

Finding the right hairstyle for men with thick coarse hair and a round face doesn't have to be a challenge. By choosing a style that adds height and volume to the top of the head, with shorter sides and back, you can create a balanced and flattering look. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and stylish. Remember, the key is to embrace your unique features and rock your hairstyle with confidence.
