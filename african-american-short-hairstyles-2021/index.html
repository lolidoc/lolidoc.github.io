Tips for African American Short Hairstyles 2021

If you're considering trying out African American short hairstyles 2021, here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

1. Moisturize regularly: African American hair tends to be dry and prone to breakage, so it's important to moisturize regularly. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

2. Protect your hair while sleeping: Invest in a satin or silk pillowcase or wrap your hair in a silk scarf before bed. This will help prevent breakage and frizz caused by friction with cotton pillowcases.

3. Avoid excessive heat styling: Heat styling tools can damage African American hair, so it's best to limit their use. If you do use heat, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray and use the lowest heat setting possible.

4. Experiment with different products: African American hair requires specific products that cater to its unique needs. Experiment with different shampoos, conditioners, and styling products to find what works best for your hair.
