So, is short or long hair better for fat faces? The answer is not so straightforward. It ultimately depends on various factors such as your personal style, hair texture, and face shape. While short hair can provide a more angular look, long hair can help soften the face.

In summary, whether short or long hair is better for fat faces depends on your personal preferences and the features you want to emphasize or downplay. Consider factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when making your decision.

Is Short Hair Better for Fat Faces?

Short hair can be a great option for those with fat faces. It can help create the illusion of a slimmer face by adding height and volume at the crown. Additionally, short hairstyles can provide a more angular look, which can help balance out the roundness of a fat face.

However, it's important to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape. A pixie cut or a short bob with layers can be flattering for those with fat faces. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume on the sides, as they can make your face appear wider.

Is Long Hair Better for Fat Faces?

Long hair can also be a great option for those with fat faces. It can help soften the face and create a more balanced look. Long layers or face-framing layers can help draw attention away from the roundness of the face.

However, it's important to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides, as they can make your face appear wider. Stick to hairstyles that create length and elongate the face, such as long, straight hair or loose waves.
