What is Thin 4c Hair?

Thin 4c hair refers to a hair type that is characterized by tight coils and a fine texture. It is the most fragile of all hair types and requires extra care and attention to promote healthy hair growth. Thin 4c hair is prone to breakage, dryness, and damage, which can hinder its ability to grow and retain length. Understanding the unique characteristics of thin 4c hair is essential for developing an effective hair care routine.

Recommendations for Growing Thin 4c Hair
