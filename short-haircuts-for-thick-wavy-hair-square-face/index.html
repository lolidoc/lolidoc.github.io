The Best Hairstyles for Square Faces

Having a square face shape with thick wavy hair can be a blessing. With the right haircut, you can enhance your features and bring out your natural beauty. If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, here are some short haircuts that are perfect for square faces with thick wavy hair.

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for square faces. This haircut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. It helps to soften the strong angles of a square face and adds a touch of femininity. For thick wavy hair, ask your hairstylist to add texture and layers to create movement and reduce bulk.

2. Bob with Side Swept Bangs

A bob haircut with side swept bangs is another great option for square faces with thick wavy hair. The length of the bob should be just above or below the jawline to create a flattering frame for your face. The side swept bangs help to soften the squareness of the face, while the wavy texture adds volume and dimension.

3. Shaggy Lob

If you prefer a longer haircut, a shaggy lob (long bob) can be a perfect choice. This haircut is slightly longer than a traditional bob and features layers throughout the hair to create movement and texture. For thick wavy hair, the shaggy lob adds an effortless, tousled look that suits a square face shape.

4. Textured Crop

A textured crop is a trendy and modern short haircut that works well for square faces with thick wavy hair. This haircut is short on the sides and back, with the top styled into textured layers. The textured crop adds volume and movement to your hair, creating a stylish and edgy look.

5. Layered Pixie

If you're looking for a shorter haircut, a layered pixie can be a great choice. This haircut features shorter layers on top and longer layers on the sides and back. The layers add texture and movement to your hair, while the pixie cut helps to balance out the strong angles of a square face.

6. Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is a modern and stylish haircut that suits square faces with thick wavy hair. This haircut is shorter on one side and longer on the other, creating an asymmetrical look. The wavy texture adds volume and dimension to your hair, while the asymmetry helps to soften the squareness of your face.

7. Curly Pixie

If you have naturally curly hair, a curly pixie can be a fantastic option for square faces. This short haircut embraces your natural curls and adds a touch of playfulness to your look. The curls help to soften the strong angles of a square face and create a feminine and stylish hairstyle.

8. Tousled Bob

A tousled bob is a versatile and chic haircut that works well for square faces with thick wavy hair. This haircut is slightly longer than a traditional bob and features tousled, textured layers. The tousled bob adds movement and dimension to your hair, creating a trendy and effortless style.

9. Layered Crop

A layered crop is a short haircut that suits square faces with thick wavy hair. This haircut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. The layers add texture and movement to your hair, while the crop cut helps to enhance your facial features and create a flattering look.

10. Messy Pixie

If you're looking for a low-maintenance and edgy haircut, a messy pixie can be a great choice. This short haircut features choppy layers and a tousled, undone look. The messy pixie adds a touch of effortless style to your appearance and suits square faces with thick wavy hair.
