Tips for Short Pixie Hairstyles for Over 50 Fine Hair 2018

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling and maintaining your short pixie hairstyle:

1. Use volumizing products: To add volume and texture to fine hair, use volumizing products such as mousse or texturizing spray. Apply these products to damp hair and use your fingers to tousle and lift the hair for added volume.

2. Embrace your natural texture: Short pixie hairstyles look great with natural texture. Embrace your natural waves or curls by using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair or by using a curling wand to enhance your natural texture.

3. Regular trims: To maintain the shape and style of your short pixie cut, it's important to schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will prevent the hair from becoming too long and losing its volume and texture.

4. Experiment with styling: Short pixie hairstyles are incredibly versatile and can be styled in various ways. Experiment with different styling techniques such as slicking the hair back with gel for a sleek look or using a texturizing spray for a tousled and textured style.
