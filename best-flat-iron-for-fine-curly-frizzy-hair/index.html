Best Flat Iron for Fine Curly Frizzy Hair: Tips for Success

When using a flat iron on fine curly frizzy hair, it's important to follow a few tips for success. First, always start with clean, dry hair. Applying heat to damp or wet hair can cause damage and breakage. Additionally, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize heat damage.

Next, section your hair and work in small sections to ensure even heat distribution. This will help to achieve a smoother, more polished look. Finally, be mindful of the temperature setting on your flat iron. Fine hair typically requires lower heat settings to avoid damage, so start with a lower temperature and gradually increase if necessary.
