How to Style Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60

Styling short hairstyles for round faces over 60 can be quick and easy. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If desired, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add lift and texture.
  2. Using a round brush and a blow dryer, lift the hair at the crown of your head and blow dry in an upward motion to create volume.
  3. Use a curling iron or a straightener to add texture and shape to your hair. Curl or straighten small sections of hair, starting from the roots and working your way down.
  4. For added volume and movement, use your fingers to tousle and separate the hair.
  5. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place.
