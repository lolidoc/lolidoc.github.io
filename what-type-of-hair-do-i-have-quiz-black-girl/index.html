Exploring Hair Type Quiz for Black Girls in More Detail

When taking a hair type quiz for black girls, it's important to understand the different factors that contribute to your hair's unique characteristics.

Texture: The texture of your hair refers to its overall feel and appearance. It can range from fine to coarse.

Porosity: Porosity refers to how well your hair absorbs and retains moisture. It can be low, normal, or high.

Thickness: The thickness of your hair refers to the diameter of each individual hair strand. It can range from thin to thick.

Curl Pattern: The curl pattern of your hair refers to the shape and pattern of your hair strands. It can range from straight to coily, with various curl patterns in between.
