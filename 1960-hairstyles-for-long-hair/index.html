How to Create 1960 Hairstyles for Long Hair:

Creating 1960 hairstyles for long hair can be a bit challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the iconic looks of the era. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.

  4. Smooth out the top layer of your hair and shape it into the desired style.
