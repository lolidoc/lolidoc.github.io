Short Haircuts for Girls/Kids 2019: Our Recommendations

When it comes to short haircuts for girls/kids 2019, there are endless options to choose from. However, we have narrowed down our top recommendations:

1. Pixie Cut:

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that never goes out of style. It is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. This haircut is perfect for girls with fine hair, as it adds volume and texture. It is also easy to style and maintain, making it a great option for busy parents.

2. Bob Cut:

The bob cut is a versatile and timeless haircut that can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type. It can be worn straight, curled, or even accessorized with cute hair accessories. The bob cut is perfect for girls who want a stylish and low-maintenance haircut.

3. Shag Cut:

The shag cut is a trendy and edgy haircut that is perfect for girls who want to make a statement. It features long layers and lots of texture, creating a playful and voluminous look. The shag cut is perfect for girls with thick hair, as it helps to reduce bulk and add movement.

4. Undercut:

The undercut is a bold and daring haircut that is perfect for girls who want to stand out. It involves shaving or cutting the hair short on one side or the back, while leaving the rest of the hair longer. The undercut can be customized with various designs or patterns, allowing for endless creativity.
