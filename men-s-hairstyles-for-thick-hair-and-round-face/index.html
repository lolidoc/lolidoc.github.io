Are you a man with thick hair and a round face? Do you struggle to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore men's hairstyles specifically designed for those with thick hair and a round face. Whether you want to add volume, create angles, or highlight your best features, we have got you covered.

Having thick hair and a round face can present some unique challenges when it comes to choosing a hairstyle. Thick hair can be difficult to manage and style, while a round face shape can make certain hairstyles appear unflattering. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your features and achieve a stylish look that suits your face shape and hair type.

The key to finding the perfect men's hairstyle for thick hair and a round face is to choose a haircut that adds height and angles to your face. By creating the illusion of length and adding volume to the top of your head, you can balance out the roundness of your face and create a more sculpted appearance.

In summary, when it comes to men's hairstyles for thick hair and a round face, the key is to choose a haircut that adds height and angles to your face. By creating the illusion of length and adding volume to the top of your head, you can balance out the roundness of your face and create a more sculpted appearance. Now, let's dive into the different men's hairstyles that are perfect for those with thick hair and a round face.

1. Pompadour

The pompadour is a classic hairstyle that works well for men with thick hair and a round face. This style involves cutting the sides short and leaving the hair on top longer. The longer hair on top is then swept back and styled to create height and volume. The pompadour adds angles to the face and creates a more elongated appearance.

2. Undercut

The undercut is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual preferences. This style involves shaving the sides and back of the head very short or even completely bald, while leaving the hair on top longer. The contrast between the short sides and the longer hair on top creates a sharp and defined look. By adding volume to the top, you can create a more balanced and angular appearance.

3. Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that can work well for men with thick hair and a round face. This style involves shaving the sides very short and leaving a strip of longer hair in the middle. The longer hair is then styled upwards to create a spiky and textured look. The faux hawk adds height and angles to the face, creating a more elongated appearance.

4. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a low-maintenance and stylish haircut that can work well for men with thick hair and a round face. This style involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top slightly longer. The longer hair on top is then styled with texture and movement to create a natural and effortless look. The textured crop adds dimension to the hair and can help to create a more angular appearance.

Conclusion of men's hairstyles for thick hair and round face

Choosing the right hairstyle is essential for enhancing your features and creating a confident and stylish look. If you have thick hair and a round face, opt for haircuts that add height and angles to your face. The pompadour, undercut, faux hawk, and textured crop are all great options to consider. Experiment with different styles and consult with a professional hairstylist to find the perfect look for you. Embrace your unique features and rock your thick hair and round face with confidence!

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I still have short hair if I have thick hair and a round face?

A: Absolutely! While longer hairstyles tend to be more flattering for men with thick hair and a round face, you can still rock a short haircut. Opt for a style that adds volume and height to the top of your head, such as a textured crop or a spiky hairstyle.

Q: How do I maintain my thick hair?

A: To maintain your thick hair, it's important to use the right products and follow a proper hair care routine. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for thick hair to keep your locks healthy and manageable. Additionally, regular trims can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

Q: Can I use hair products to add volume to my thick hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair products such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume to your thick hair. Apply the product to damp hair and use your fingers or a comb to distribute it evenly. Then, blow dry your hair using a round brush to create lift and volume.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have thick hair and a round face?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to hairstyles, there are certain styles that may not be as flattering for men with thick hair and a round face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short and flat on top, as these can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for styles that add height and angles to your face.
