Tips for Using the Best Flea Comb for Short Haired Dogs

When using the best flea comb for short-haired dogs, it's important to follow a few tips to ensure effectiveness and safety:

  • Start combing from the head and work your way down the body, paying special attention to areas where fleas are most likely to hide, such as behind the ears and in the armpits.
  • Comb in the direction of hair growth to avoid causing discomfort to your dog.
  • After each stroke, dip the comb in a bowl of soapy water to remove any captured fleas or eggs.
  • Regularly clean the comb to prevent the spread of fleas.
