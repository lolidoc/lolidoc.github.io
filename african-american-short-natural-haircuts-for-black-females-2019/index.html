Introduction

In 2019, African American women embraced their natural hair more than ever before. Short natural haircuts became a popular choice for black females, allowing them to showcase their unique beauty and express their individuality. These hairstyles not only highlight the natural texture of the hair but also offer versatility and low maintenance. If you're looking for inspiration for your next haircut, here are some trendy options to consider.

The Tapered Cut

The tapered cut is a classic choice for black women with short natural hair. This haircut features shorter hair on the sides and back, gradually blending into longer lengths on top. It creates a flattering shape that accentuates the facial features and adds dimension to the overall look. With the tapered cut, you can experiment with different styling options, such as wearing it sleek and straight or embracing your natural curls.

The Afro Puff

If you're looking for a simple yet stylish hairstyle, the afro puff is a go-to option. This hairstyle involves gathering all your hair at the crown of your head and securing it with a hair tie or accessories. The afro puff is not only easy to achieve, but it also adds volume and flair to your look. You can accessorize it with headbands, flowers, or even colorful scarves to add a personal touch.

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a bold and daring choice for black females with short natural hair. This haircut involves cutting the hair very short, giving it a close-cropped look. The buzz cut is not only low maintenance but also showcases the natural beauty of your face. It's a great option if you're looking for a hairstyle that requires minimal styling and upkeep.

The Finger Coils

Finger coils are a popular choice for black women who want to enhance their natural curl pattern. This hairstyle involves twisting small sections of your hair around your finger, creating defined coils. Finger coils can be worn as is, or you can unravel them for a fuller and more voluminous look. This style is versatile and can be achieved on various lengths of short natural hair.

The Mohawk

The Mohawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that never goes out of style. This haircut involves shaving or cutting the sides of your hair very short while leaving a strip of longer hair in the center. The Mohawk allows you to experiment with different lengths and textures, giving you the freedom to express your unique personality. You can wear it sleek and straight or add some curls for a softer look.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and chic hairstyle that works well for black females with short natural hair. This haircut is characterized by its short length and close-cropped sides, with slightly longer layers on top. The pixie cut is versatile and can be styled in various ways, such as adding volume, creating texture, or even adding colorful highlights. It's a low maintenance option that exudes confidence and sophistication.

The Twist Out

The twist out is a popular styling option for black women with short natural hair. This technique involves twisting small sections of your hair while wet and allowing them to dry. Once dry, you can unravel the twists, creating defined and bouncy curls. The twist out adds volume and texture to your hair, giving it a playful and youthful look. It's a versatile style that can last for several days with proper maintenance.

The Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that mimics the look of a traditional Mohawk without the commitment of shaving the sides. This hairstyle involves creating a line of closely cropped hair down the center of your head, while leaving the sides longer. The faux hawk can be styled in various ways, such as adding volume, creating twists, or even braiding the sides. It's a versatile option that allows you to switch up your look whenever you want.

The Short Afro

The short afro is a classic and timeless hairstyle that celebrates the natural beauty of black women. This haircut involves letting your hair grow out into its natural shape and texture, without manipulating or altering it. The short afro exudes confidence and embraces your natural features. It's a low maintenance option that allows you to showcase your unique beauty effortlessly.

The Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a traditional African hairstyle that has gained popularity among black women with short natural hair. This style involves sectioning your hair into small knots and securing them close to the scalp. Bantu knots can be worn as is, or you can unravel them for a curly and voluminous look. This hairstyle is not only stylish but also protects your hair from damage and breakage.

In conclusion, short natural haircuts for black females in 2019 offer a wide range of options to embrace and celebrate the unique beauty of African American women. Whether you prefer a bold and edgy look or a chic and timeless style, there is a haircut that suits your taste and personality. These hairstyles not only showcase your natural texture but also offer versatility and low maintenance. So, go ahead and rock your short natural hair with confidence and pride!
