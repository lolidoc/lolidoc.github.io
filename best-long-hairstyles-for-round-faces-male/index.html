The Classic Undercut

The classic undercut is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that works well for men with round faces. This hairstyle features short sides and back, with longer hair on top. The longer hair on top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, pushed to the side, or worn in a messy textured style. The short sides and back create a contrast that helps to elongate the face and make it appear less round.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is another great option for men with round faces. This hairstyle is characterized by short, textured hair on top with slightly longer sides. The textured hair on top can be styled in a messy, tousled look, which adds height and volume to the hair. The shorter sides create a contrast that helps to lengthen the face and create a more balanced look.

The Long Layered Cut

If you prefer to keep your hair longer, the long layered cut is a fantastic choice for men with round faces. This hairstyle features long hair with layers throughout, which adds movement and dimension to the hair. The layers help to create the illusion of a more elongated face shape and can be styled in various ways, such as wearing the hair down, pulling it back into a ponytail, or styling it in a half-up, half-down look.

The Side Part

The side part is a classic and timeless hairstyle that works well for men with round faces. This hairstyle involves parting the hair to one side and combing it neatly. The side part creates a diagonal line that helps to lengthen the face and make it appear less round. You can pair the side part with a fade or undercut for a modern twist.

The Messy Waves

If you have naturally wavy or curly hair, embracing your texture can be a great way to enhance your round face shape. The messy waves hairstyle involves allowing your hair to air dry or using a diffuser to enhance your natural waves or curls.
