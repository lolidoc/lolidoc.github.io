Are you a fan of the iconic 60s style? Do you want to achieve that voluminous, glamorous hair that was so popular during that era? Look no further! In this blog post, we will guide you on how to backcomb your hair 60s style, giving you the perfect retro look.

The 60s were a time of bold fashion statements and hairstyles that made a statement. Backcombing, also known as teasing or ratting, was a popular technique used to create volume and height in the hair. However, achieving the perfect backcombed look can be challenging, especially if you're new to the technique.

To backcomb your hair 60s style, start by sectioning your hair into small sections. Take a comb and hold it vertically against the hair section, close to the roots. Gently comb the hair downwards towards the scalp, creating friction and volume. Repeat this process for each hair section, working your way from the back to the front of your head.

In summary, to achieve the perfect 60s backcombed hair, follow these steps: section your hair, hold the comb vertically against the hair section, comb downwards towards the scalp, and repeat for each section, working from the back to the front.

How to Backcomb Hair 60s Style: A Step-by-Step Guide

Backcombing your hair 60s style requires some practice and technique. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve that iconic retro look:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. This will make the backcombing process easier and more manageable. Use hair clips or hair ties to keep the sections separate.

Step 2: Hold the Comb Vertically

Take a comb and hold it vertically against the hair section, close to the roots. Make sure the comb teeth are facing towards the scalp.

Step 3: Comb Downwards

Gently comb the hair downwards towards the scalp, creating friction and volume. Be careful not to pull too hard or tug at the hair, as this can cause damage or breakage.

Step 4: Repeat for Each Section

Repeat the backcombing process for each hair section, working your way from the back to the front of your head. Take your time and be patient, as it may take a few tries to achieve the desired volume and height.

Tips for Backcombing Hair 60s Style

Here are some tips to help you master the art of backcombing your hair 60s style:

1. Use the Right Tools

Invest in a good quality comb with fine teeth for better control and precision. Avoid using brushes or combs with wide-spaced teeth, as they will not create the desired volume.

2. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Backcombing works best on clean, dry hair. Avoid applying any heavy styling products or oils, as they can weigh down the hair and make backcombing more difficult.

3. Use Hairspray for Hold

Once you have backcombed your hair to your desired volume, use a hairspray to set the style in place. This will help maintain the volume and prevent the hair from falling flat throughout the day.

4. Be Gentle and Patient

Backcombing requires a gentle touch and patience. Avoid pulling or tugging at the hair too hard, as this can cause damage and breakage. Take your time and work in small sections to achieve the best results.

Conclusion of How to Backcomb Hair 60s Style

Backcombing your hair 60s style can be a fun and exciting way to recreate the iconic retro look. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect volume and height that was so popular during that era. Remember to be gentle, patient, and use hairspray to set the style in place. So go ahead, channel your inner 60s diva and rock that backcombed hair!
