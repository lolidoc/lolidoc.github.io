What Are the Best Hairstyles for Round Faces in 2021?

The best hairstyles for round faces in 2021 are those that complement and enhance your natural features. Here are some popular options to consider:

1. Long Layers:

Long layers can help to create movement and add dimension to round faces. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair, and can be customized to suit your individual preferences.

2. Side-Swept Bangs:

Side-swept bangs can help to create angles and draw attention away from the width of the cheeks. They can be paired with various hair lengths, from short to long, and can be styled to frame the face in a flattering way.

3. Asymmetrical Bob:

An asymmetrical bob is a stylish and modern option for round faces. This haircut features a shorter length on one side and a longer length on the other, creating an edgy and angular look that helps to elongate the face.

4. High Ponytail:

A high ponytail can create the illusion of a longer face shape by pulling the hair up and away from the face. This simple and versatile hairstyle is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

5. Loose Waves:

Loose waves can add texture and movement to round faces, creating a soft and romantic look. This hairstyle works well with medium to long hair lengths, and can be achieved with a curling iron or by braiding the hair overnight.

Tips for Best Hairstyles for Round Faces in 2021

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyles for round faces in 2021:

1. Avoid Excessive Volume:

While adding volume can help to create the illusion of length, too much volume can make your face appear rounder. Opt for styles that have controlled volume and avoid excessive teasing or backcombing.

2. Embrace Face-Framing Layers:

Face-framing layers can help to add structure and angles to round faces. Consider adding layers around the face to create a more flattering look.

3. Play with Parting:

Changing your hair parting can instantly change the look and shape of your face. Experiment with different parting styles, such as a deep side part or a middle part, to see what suits you best.

4. Don't Be Afraid to Experiment:

Ultimately, finding the best hairstyle for your round face is about trial and error. Don't be afraid to try out different styles, cuts, and techniques until you find the perfect look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
