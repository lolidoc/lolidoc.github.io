What Hair Color Will Fit Me

Written by Alexa May 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Choosing the perfect hair color can be a daunting task, especially with the wide array of options available today. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a complete transformation, finding the right hair color that suits your skin tone, eye color, and personality is crucial. In this article, we will guide you through the process of determining which hair color will fit you best in 2023.

Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a vital role in determining the most flattering hair color for you. There are three main categories: warm, cool, and neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If they appear blue, you have a cool undertone. If you can't quite distinguish between the two, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Warm Undertones

If you have warm undertones, hair colors with golden, honey, or caramel tones will complement your complexion. Think shades like golden blonde, copper, or chestnut brown. These warm hues will bring out the radiance in your skin and enhance your natural glow.

Cool Undertones

For those with cool undertones, hair colors with ashy or cool undertones will be the most flattering. Consider shades like platinum blonde, icy blue, or ash brown. These colors will beautifully contrast with your cool complexion and create a striking look.

Neutral Undertones

If you have neutral undertones, lucky you! You can pull off a wide range of hair colors. Experiment with warm and cool tones to find what you love best. From auburn to chocolate brown or even a vibrant red, the choices are endless.

Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also influence which hair color will suit you best. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop and create a captivating look.

Blue Eyes

If you have blue eyes, consider hair colors that will enhance their intensity. Shades like platinum blonde, caramel, or even a rich chocolate brown can beautifully complement your blue eyes and make them stand out.

Green Eyes

For green-eyed individuals, hair colors with warm undertones can bring out the natural beauty of your eyes. Consider shades like copper, auburn, or even a deep red. These warm hues will create a stunning contrast and make your green eyes shine.

Brown Eyes

Brown-eyed individuals have the most versatility when it comes to choosing hair colors. Whether you want to go blonde, brunette, or even experiment with vibrant colors, brown eyes can handle it all. Consider shades like honey blonde, chestnut brown, or even a bold burgundy for a dramatic look.

Personality and Style

Your personality and personal style should also be taken into consideration when choosing a hair color. Are you someone who loves to stand out in a crowd? Consider vibrant hues like pink, purple, or blue. If you prefer a more natural and subtle look, opt for shades that are closer to your natural hair color.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can be a transformative experience. By considering your skin tone, eye color, and personality, you can find the perfect hair color that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and radiant. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure the best results and maintain the health of your hair throughout the coloring process. Embrace the opportunity to express yourself through your hair and enjoy the journey of finding the hair color that fits you best in 2023!
