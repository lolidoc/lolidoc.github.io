Easy Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Thick Wavy Hair

Having shoulder length thick wavy hair can be a challenge when it comes to finding easy hairstyles. However, there are plenty of options that are both stylish and low-maintenance. One hairstyle that works well for this hair type is the messy bun. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or even a special event. To achieve the messy bun, simply gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure with bobby pins and leave a few strands loose for a relaxed look.

Another easy hairstyle for shoulder length thick wavy hair is the half-up, half-down style. This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still want to show off your waves. To achieve this look, simply take a small section of hair from each side of your head and twist them towards the back. Secure with bobby pins and leave the rest of your hair down.

If you're looking for a more polished look, try the sleek ponytail. This hairstyle is perfect for a day at the office or a night out on the town. To achieve the sleek ponytail, simply gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with a hair tie. Use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways for a sleek finish.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still wear my hair down if I have shoulder length thick wavy hair?

A: Absolutely! Shoulder length thick wavy hair looks beautiful when worn down. To enhance your natural waves, try using a curl-enhancing product and scrunching your hair with your hands while it air dries. This will help to define your waves and create a relaxed and effortless look.

Q: Are there any easy updo hairstyles for shoulder length thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, there are plenty of easy updo hairstyles for shoulder length thick wavy hair. One option is the messy bun, which involves gathering your hair into a high ponytail and twisting it into a bun. Another option is the braided updo, which involves creating a loose braid and pinning it up at the back of your head. Both of these hairstyles are quick and easy to achieve.

Q: Can I use heat tools on my shoulder length thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can use heat tools on your shoulder length thick wavy hair. However, it's important to use a heat protectant spray before styling and to avoid using excessive heat. Using a lower heat setting and limiting the amount of time that the heat tool is in contact with your hair can help to prevent damage and keep your waves looking their best.

Q: How often should I wash my shoulder length thick wavy hair?

A: The frequency of washing your shoulder length thick wavy hair will depend on your individual needs and preferences.
