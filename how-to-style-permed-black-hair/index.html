How To Style Permed Black Hair In 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

Permed black hair is a popular hairstyle that adds volume and texture to naturally curly hair. In 2023, there are various styling techniques and products available to enhance and maintain permed black hair. Whether you have recently permed your hair or are looking for new styling ideas, this article will provide you with tips and inspiration to style your permed black hair.

1. Moisturize and Hydrate

One of the key steps in styling permed black hair is to keep it moisturized and hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to replenish moisture and prevent dryness. Additionally, use a leave-in conditioner daily to keep your hair hydrated and soft.

2. Define Your Curls

To enhance the definition of your curls, apply a curl-enhancing cream or gel to damp hair. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair from the ends upwards, encouraging the formation of defined curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance the natural curls.

3. Protective Hairstyles

Protective hairstyles are a great way to style permed black hair while minimizing damage and promoting hair growth. Try braids, twists, buns, or updos to protect your hair from daily manipulation and styling. These hairstyles also help to retain moisture and prevent breakage.

4. Accessorize

Add some flair to your permed black hair by accessorizing with hair clips, headbands, or scarves. These accessories not only add style but also help to keep your hair in place. Experiment with different accessories to create unique and eye-catching looks.

5. Heatless Styling

Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage to your permed black hair. Instead, opt for heatless styling techniques such as bantu knots, flexi rods, or twist-outs. These methods create beautiful curls without the need for heat, keeping your hair healthy and vibrant.

6. Trim Regularly

Regular trims are essential for maintaining the health and shape of permed black hair. Schedule regular appointments with your hairstylist to trim away split ends and maintain the desired length. Trimming also helps to prevent tangling and breakage.

7. Nighttime Care

Protect your permed black hair while you sleep by using a satin or silk pillowcase. These materials reduce friction and prevent hair breakage. You can also wrap your hair in a satin scarf or bonnet to keep it protected and maintain your hairstyle overnight.

8. Experiment with Colors

If you're feeling adventurous, consider experimenting with hair colors to further enhance your permed black hair. From subtle highlights to bold vibrant hues, there are endless possibilities to add dimension and personality to your hairstyle. Consult with a professional colorist to find the best options for your hair.

9. Embrace Versatility

Permed black hair offers versatility in styling options. Embrace this flexibility by trying different hairstyles such as ponytails, half-up half-down styles, or sleek updos. Explore online tutorials and inspiration to discover new ways to style your permed black hair.

10. Maintain a Healthy Lifestyle

Lastly, maintaining a healthy lifestyle is crucial for the overall health of your permed black hair. Eat a balanced diet, exercise regularly, and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth. Additionally, avoid excessive use of heat styling tools and harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

Conclusion

Styling permed black hair in 2023 involves proper moisturization, defining curls, protective hairstyles, accessorizing, heatless styling, regular trims, nighttime care, experimenting with colors, embracing versatility, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve stunning and healthy styles for your permed black hair.
