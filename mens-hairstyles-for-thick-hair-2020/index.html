Mens Hairstyles for Thick Hair 2020: Explained

Mens hairstyles for thick hair in 2020 are designed to enhance the natural texture and volume of thick hair. These hairstyles range from short and cropped cuts to longer and more textured styles. The goal is to create a look that is stylish, modern, and easy to maintain.

One of the most popular mens hairstyles for thick hair in 2020 is the textured crop. This hairstyle is characterized by short, textured hair on top and faded or tapered sides. It is a versatile look that can be styled in various ways, depending on your personal preference and the occasion.

Another trendy option for men with thick hair is the undercut. This hairstyle features shorter hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. The contrast between the short sides and the longer top creates a bold and edgy look.

If you prefer a longer hairstyle, the medium-length textured cut is a great option. This hairstyle allows you to showcase your thick hair while adding texture and movement. It can be styled with a side part, slicked back, or left natural for a more relaxed look.

History and Myth of Mens Hairstyles for Thick Hair

The history of mens hairstyles for thick hair dates back to ancient times. In ancient Egypt, men would wear their hair long and braided as a symbol of wealth and status. In ancient Greece, men would often wear their hair short and curly, using oils and pomades to add shine and definition.

Throughout history, thick hair has been associated with masculinity and strength. It has been prized for its ability to add volume and texture to hairstyles. However, there have also been myths and misconceptions surrounding thick hair, such as the belief that it is more difficult to manage and style.

Recommendations for Mens Hairstyles for Thick Hair

The Importance of Styling Techniques for Mens Hairstyles for Thick Hair

Styling techniques are crucial when it comes to mens hairstyles for thick hair. By using the right techniques, you can enhance the natural texture and volume of your hair and achieve the desired look.

One important technique is blow-drying. By blow-drying your hair in the right direction and using a round brush, you can add volume and texture to your hairstyle. Additionally, using a texturizing spray or paste can help enhance the natural texture of your hair.

Another important technique is using the right styling products. Using a volumizing spray or mousse before blow-drying can add lift and volume to your hair. Additionally, using a texture paste or pomade can help define and shape your hairstyle.

How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles for Thick Hair

What If You Have Thin Hair?

If you have thin hair, don't worry! There are still plenty of options for you. While mens hairstyles for thick hair may not work as well for thin hair, there are other styles that can enhance the appearance of volume and texture.

Consider opting for shorter hairstyles, such as the buzz cut or the crew cut. These styles can create the illusion of thicker hair and are easy to maintain. Additionally, using volumizing products, such as mousses or sprays, can help add lift and texture to your hair.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I choose the right mens hairstyle for my face shape?

A: It's important to choose a mens hairstyle that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, opt for a hairstyle with volume on top and shorter sides to create the illusion of length. If you have a square face, a textured crop or an undercut can accentuate your strong jawline.

Q: What products should I use to style my thick hair?

A: When styling thick hair, it's important to use products that add texture and hold. Volumizing sprays, texture pastes, and pomades are great options. Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

Q: How often should I get a haircut for my thick hair?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on the style you choose and how fast your hair grows. As a general rule, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

Q: Can I style my thick hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can achieve stylish hairstyles for thick hair without using heat. Opt for air-drying your hair and using products that enhance your natural texture. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the ones that work best for you.
