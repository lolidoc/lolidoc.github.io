How to Make Bushy Hair Curly - 2023 Guide

Introduction

Having bushy hair can be a challenge when trying to achieve curly hairstyles. However, with the right techniques and products, you can transform your bushy hair into beautiful curls. In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to make bushy hair curly in the year 2023. Whether you have naturally bushy hair or you're looking to enhance your current curls, this article will help you achieve the desired look.

Step 1: Preparing Your Hair

Before starting the curling process, it's essential to prepare your hair properly. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help hydrate your hair and make it more manageable. Once you've washed your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess moisture.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

To enhance your natural curls or create curls in your bushy hair, apply a curl enhancing product. Look for a product that suits your hair type and desired curl pattern. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Avoid applying too much product at the roots, as it can weigh down your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

To ensure that you curl every strand of hair, divide your hair into sections. Start by creating a part in the middle of your head, then divide each side into two sections. Use hair clips or hair ties to secure each section.

Step 4: Use a Curling Wand or Curling Iron

Now it's time to curl your hair. If you have a curling wand, wrap a small section of hair around the wand, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. If you're using a curling iron, wrap the hair around the barrel, being careful not to burn yourself. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way from the bottom to the top.

Step 5: Let Your Curls Cool

After curling each section, allow your curls to cool before touching them. This will help set the curls and make them last longer. You can either let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to speed up the process. Once your curls have cooled, you can gently separate them with your fingers to create a more voluminous look.

Step 6: Apply a Holding Product

To ensure that your curls stay in place throughout the day, apply a holding product. This can be a hairspray, mousse, or gel that provides hold without making your hair stiff. Apply the product lightly, focusing on the ends of your curls. Avoid applying too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it appear greasy.

Step 7: Maintain Your Curls

To maintain your curly hairstyle, avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. Excessive touching can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern. If you notice any frizz, you can use a small amount of anti-frizz serum or hair oil to tame it. Additionally, sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and preserve your curls overnight.

Step 8: Refresh Your Curls

If your curls start to lose their shape throughout the day, you can refresh them by lightly misting your hair with water or a curl refresher spray. Scrunch your hair gently to reactivate the curls and let them air dry. Avoid using heat styling tools to touch up your curls, as it can cause damage and lead to frizz.

Step 9: Experiment with Hairstyles

Once you've mastered the art of making your bushy hair curly, don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. You can try half-up half-down styles, updos, or even braid your curly hair for a boho look. The possibilities are endless, so have fun and embrace your beautiful curls!

Step 10: Take Care of Your Curly Hair

Lastly, it's essential to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, deep condition your hair regularly, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Embrace your natural curls and follow a proper hair care routine to maintain their beauty.
