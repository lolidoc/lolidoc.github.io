How To Do The 60S Hair Flip A Step By Step Guide

Written by Michael Mar 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Introduction

The 60s hair flip is a classic hairstyle that exudes elegance and retro charm. It was popularized by iconic figures like Brigitte Bardot and Audrey Hepburn, and it continues to be a favorite among fashion enthusiasts today. If you're looking to channel your inner vintage goddess, mastering the art of the 60s hair flip is a must. In this tutorial, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of achieving this glamorous hairstyle.

Preparation

Before you begin styling your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that add volume and texture. Towel-dry your hair gently, avoiding any rough movements that could cause frizz. Apply a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools.

Step 1: Blow-Drying

To create the perfect canvas for the 60s hair flip, you'll need to blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Divide your hair into sections and start blow-drying from the roots to the ends. Use the round brush to lift the roots and create volume. As you dry each section, roll it up towards your scalp and secure it with a clip to cool down and set the shape.

Step 2: Teasing

Teasing your hair is essential for achieving the signature height and volume of the 60s hair flip. Take a small section of hair near the crown and hold it straight up. Using a fine-toothed comb, gently backcomb the hair from mid-length to the roots. Repeat this process with small sections of hair at the crown until you achieve the desired volume.

Step 3: Sectioning

Once you've teased your hair, it's time to section it for the hair flip. Divide your hair into two sections horizontally, securing the top section with clips. This will allow you to focus on styling the bottom section first.

Step 4: Curling

Using a curling iron or hot rollers, curl the bottom section of your hair away from your face. Hold the curling iron vertically and wrap small sections of hair around it, starting from the mid-lengths. Leave the ends out for a more relaxed look. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it. Repeat this process for all the hair in the bottom section.

Step 5: Flipping

Now it's time to create the iconic hair flip. Remove the clips from the top section of your hair and comb through it gently to smooth out any tangles. Take a wide-toothed comb and gently comb the hair towards the back of your head, creating a slight lift at the roots. Use your fingers to arrange the flipped hair in a natural and relaxed manner.

Step 6: Finishing Touches

To complete the 60s hair flip, apply a light-hold hairspray to set the style and add shine. You can also use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add extra volume and texture. Finish off the look by accessorizing with a headband, scarf, or a stylish hairpin to enhance the retro vibe.

Conclusion

The 60s hair flip is a timeless hairstyle that adds a touch of vintage glamour to any look. By following these simple steps, you can master this iconic hairstyle and rock it with confidence. Whether you're attending a special event or simply want to add a touch of retro charm to your everyday style, the 60s hair flip is sure to turn heads and make you feel like a true style icon.
