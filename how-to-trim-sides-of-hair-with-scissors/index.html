Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Then, using the comb, section off the hair that you want to trim. It's best to start with a small section of hair and gradually work your way around your head, rather than attempting to trim all of the hair at once.

Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and position the scissors vertically against the hair. Slowly and carefully trim the hair, moving the scissors in an upward motion. It's important to trim small amounts of hair at a time to avoid cutting off too much.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use regular scissors to trim the sides of my hair?
A: It's best to use haircutting scissors, as they are specifically designed to cut hair and will give you better control and precision.
