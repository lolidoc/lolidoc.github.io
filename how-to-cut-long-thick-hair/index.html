How to Cut Long Thick Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

When it comes to cutting long thick hair, it's important to follow the right steps to ensure a successful haircut. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut long thick hair:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into manageable sections. Use hair clips or hair ties to secure each section, so they don't get in the way while you're cutting.

Step 2: Comb Out Any Tangles

Before you start cutting, make sure to comb out any tangles or knots in your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will ensure an even result.

Step 3: Start with the Bottom

Begin cutting at the bottom of your hair, trimming off any split ends or damaged hair. Work your way up gradually, taking small sections at a time.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair While Dry

It's important to cut your hair while it's dry to ensure an even cut. Wet hair can appear longer, so cutting it while wet may result in a shorter style than you intended.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've cut your hair, style it using the right products for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and manageable.
