Most Popular Hairstyles for Over 60: Layered Bob with Volume

Another popular hairstyle for women over 60 is the layered bob with volume. This style is perfect for those who want to add volume and movement to their hair while still maintaining a polished and sophisticated look. The layered bob is characterized by shorter layers at the back of the head and longer layers at the front, creating a flattering and face-framing effect.

I have always had thinning hair, so I was instantly drawn to the idea of a layered bob with volume. The added layers create the illusion of thicker hair, while the shorter length keeps the style looking fresh and modern. I love how the layers frame my face and give me a youthful and vibrant appearance.

When opting for a layered bob with volume, it's important to use the right styling techniques and products to maintain the look. Adding a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair can help to create lift and body, while using a round brush when blow-drying can add even more volume. Finish off with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place without weighing it down.
