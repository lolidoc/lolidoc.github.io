Short Hair Styles for Older Women with Thin Hair: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can older women with thin hair pull off a pixie cut?

Q: Will a bob make my thin hair look even thinner?

A: No, a bob can actually make thin hair appear thicker. By adding layers and shaping to the haircut, a bob can create the illusion of volume and body. It is important to choose a bob length and style that works well with your thin hair.
