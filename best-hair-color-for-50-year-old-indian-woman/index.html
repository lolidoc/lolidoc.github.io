Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: The frequency of root touch-ups will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how noticeable the regrowth is. As a general guideline, it is recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain a seamless color transition and avoid any harsh lines.

Q: Will coloring my hair damage it?

A: Coloring your hair can cause some damage, particularly if it involves bleaching or lightening. However, with proper care and maintenance, you can minimize the damage and keep your hair healthy. It is important to use products specifically designed for colored hair and avoid excessive heat styling and chemical treatments.
