Short Styles For Natural Curly Hair In 2023

Written by Jhonny Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Having natural curly hair can be both a blessing and a challenge While it is beautiful and unique it can also be difficult to manage and style However with the right knowledge and techniques you can embrace your natural curls and rock short hairstyles that are both trendy and low maintenance In this article we will explore some of the best short styles for natural curly hair in 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having natural curly hair can be both a blessing and a challenge. While it is beautiful and unique, it can also be difficult to manage and style. However, with the right knowledge and techniques, you can embrace your natural curls and rock short hairstyles that are both trendy and low-maintenance. In this article, we will explore some of the best short styles for natural curly hair in 2023.

The Pixie Cut

One of the most popular short styles for natural curly hair is the pixie cut. This chic and edgy haircut is perfect for those who want to embrace their curls while keeping their hair short and manageable. The pixie cut works well with all curl patterns and can be customized to suit your individual style.

Styling Tips:

To style your curly pixie cut, start by applying a curl-enhancing mousse or gel to damp hair. Use your fingers to scrunch and shape your curls while blow-drying with a diffuser. Finish off with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Bob

If you prefer a slightly longer length, the bob is a great option for natural curly hair. The bob haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and curl pattern. It can be worn with or without bangs, and you can also add layers for more volume and movement.

Styling Tips:

To style your curly bob, apply a curl-defining cream to damp hair and comb through to distribute the product evenly. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently detangle your curls while blow-drying with a diffuser. Finish off with a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

The Afro

If you want to fully embrace your natural curls and make a bold statement, the afro is the way to go. This iconic hairstyle is not only stylish but also celebrates your natural hair texture. The afro can be worn at various lengths, from a short and tight style to a larger and more voluminous look.

Styling Tips:

To style your afro, start by applying a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to keep your curls moisturized. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently detangle your curls, then allow your hair to air dry. For extra volume, you can use a pick or a wide-toothed comb to lift your roots.

The Shag

The shag haircut is making a comeback in 2023 and is a great option for natural curly hair. This layered haircut adds texture and movement to your curls, giving them a tousled and effortless look. The shag can be styled in various ways, from a messy and bedhead style to a more polished and defined look.

Styling Tips:

To style your curly shag, apply a texturizing spray or mousse to damp hair and scrunch your curls with your fingers while blow-drying with a diffuser. Use a curling iron or wand to add more definition to certain sections if desired. Finish off with a flexible hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Conclusion

Short hairstyles for natural curly hair in 2023 offer a wide range of options for those who want to embrace their curls and make a statement. From the pixie cut to the afro, there is a style to suit every curl pattern and personal style. With the right knowledge and styling techniques, you can rock short curly hair with confidence and style.
