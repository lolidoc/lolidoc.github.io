What is Bangs and How Does it Look on a Round Face?

Bangs refer to a hairstyle where the hair is cut and styled to fall over the forehead. They can be cut straight across, angled, or side-swept. When it comes to round faces, certain types of bangs can help create the illusion of a more elongated face shape and add dimension to the overall look. Side-swept bangs, in particular, can help to soften the roundness of the face and create a flattering frame.

Tips for Bangs on a Round Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when considering bangs for a round face:

  1. Opt for side-swept bangs that are longer and angled to create a more elongated face shape.
  2. Avoid blunt bangs that are cut straight across, as they can make the face appear rounder.
  3. Consider adding layers to the bangs to add texture and movement.
  4. Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.

How to Style Bangs on a Round Face

Styling bangs on a round face can be done in various ways, depending on the desired look and the specific type of bangs. Some styling options include:

  • Using a round brush to blow-dry the bangs for a smooth and sleek look.
  • Adding texture and volume to the bangs by using a texturizing spray or a volumizing mousse.
  • Using a flat iron to create a sleek and straight look for blunt bangs.
  • Twisting or pinning the bangs to the side for a different style.

What If Bangs Don't Look Good on a Round Face?
