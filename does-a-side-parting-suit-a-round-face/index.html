Does a Side Parting Suit a Round Face: Explained

Now let's dive deeper into the topic of whether a side parting suits a round face. To do this, let's first understand what a side parting is and how it can be beneficial for those with round faces.

What is a Side Parting?

A side parting is a hairstyle where the hair is divided into two sections, with the majority of the hair swept to one side. It creates a diagonal line across the forehead, adding asymmetry to the hairstyle. This diagonal line helps to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

For those with round faces, a side parting can be a game-changer. It adds angles and breaks up the roundness, making the face appear longer and slimmer. By creating this asymmetry, a side parting helps to balance out the width of the face and create a more defined look.

The History and Myth of Side Partings

Side partings have been popular throughout history and have been seen on various iconic figures. In the 1920s, the side parting became popular among flappers, who embraced the more androgynous fashion of the time. In the 1940s and 1950s, side partings were often seen on glamorous Hollywood actresses, adding elegance and sophistication to their hairstyles.

There is a myth that a side parting is only suitable for those with certain face shapes, such as oval or heart-shaped faces. However, this myth is not true. With the right styling techniques and products, a side parting can be customized to suit any face shape, including round faces.

The Hidden Secret of Side Partings

One of the hidden secrets of side partings is that they can be used to highlight certain facial features. For those with round faces, a side parting can draw attention to the eyes or cheekbones, creating a focal point and adding dimension to the face.

Additionally, a side parting can also be used to create different hairstyles and looks. You can experiment with different textures, such as sleek and straight or tousled waves, to add variety to your side parting hairstyle. Accessories, such as bobby pins or hair clips, can also be added to enhance the overall look.

Recommendations for Side Partings

If you have a round face and want to try a side parting, here are some recommendations to keep in mind:

  • Choose a deep side parting to create maximum asymmetry and elongate the face.
  • Consider adding layers or volume to the hair to add more dimension and create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Experiment with different textures and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use styling products, such as pomade or hairspray, to keep your side parting in place and add shine.

Exploring Side Partings for Round Faces

Now let's explore side partings for round faces in more detail. When choosing a side parting hairstyle, it's important to consider factors such as hair length, texture, and personal style. Here are some ideas to inspire your next side parting look:

1. Short Pixie Cut with Side Parting: A short pixie cut with a side parting can add edginess and sophistication to a round face. Keep the sides and back of the hair short, while leaving slightly longer layers on top to create the side parting.

2. Long Waves with Side Parting: For those with longer hair, try adding loose waves and a side parting. This creates a romantic and feminine look, while also adding length and angles to a round face.

3. Sleek Bob with Side Parting: A sleek bob with a side parting can be a chic and modern choice for round faces. Keep the hair straight and smooth, and create a deep side parting to add asymmetry.

4. Half-Up Half-Down with Side Parting: To add versatility to your side parting hairstyle, try a half-up half-down look. Gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip, leaving the rest of your hair down with a side parting.

Fun Facts About Side Partings

Did you know that side partings have been popular for centuries? In ancient Egypt, both men and women would wear side partings as a way to signify their social status. Those in higher positions of power would often have more elaborate side partings, adorned with jewels or intricate designs.

Another fun fact is that side partings can create the illusion of a more symmetrical face. By adding asymmetry to the hairstyle, a side parting can draw attention away from any facial asymmetry and create a more balanced overall look.

How to Achieve the Perfect Side Parting

Now that you're convinced a side parting suits a round face, let's talk about how to achieve the perfect side parting. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb or your fingers to create a deep side parting, starting from the center of your forehead and extending towards the crown of your head.
  3. Use a styling product, such as pomade or gel, to keep your side parting in place and add shine.
  4. Style the rest of your hair as desired, whether it's straightening, curling, or leaving it natural.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to ensure your side parting stays in place throughout the day.

What If a Side Parting Doesn't Suit You?

If you've tried a side parting and feel that it doesn't suit your round face, don't worry. There are plenty of other hairstyles that can flatter your facial shape. Consider trying a center parting, which can create symmetry and balance on a round face. Bangs or layers can also add angles and definition to your hairstyle.
