Q: How do I style my hair to add volume?

A: To add volume to your hair, start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Blow-dry your hair upside down using a round brush to create lift at the roots. You can also use volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, to enhance volume.
