How To Fix Thick Hair For Guys Tips And Tricks For 2023

Written by Exel Jun 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having thick hair as a guy can be both a blessing and a curse. While it gives you a voluminous and full look, it can also be challenging to manage. In this article, we will provide you with some valuable tips and tricks to help you fix your thick hair in 2023 and achieve a stylish and manageable hairstyle.

1. Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in fixing thick hair is getting a suitable haircut. Opt for styles that reduce the bulkiness of your hair, such as layered cuts or fades. Shorter hairstyles tend to work better for thick hair, as they make it easier to manage and style on a daily basis.

2. Use the Right Hair Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for thick hair. Look for products that provide moisture and control without weighing your hair down. Use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated without adding extra volume.

3. Avoid Overwashing

Thick hair can become dry and frizzy if washed too frequently. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every two days to maintain its natural oils and prevent it from becoming excessively voluminous. Use dry shampoo or a gentle conditioner on non-wash days to freshen up your hair.

4. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help prevent breakage and minimize frizz. Start combing from the ends and gradually work your way up to avoid pulling and damaging your hair.

5. Embrace Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like hairdryers and straighteners can be your best friends when it comes to taming thick hair. Use a hairdryer on a low heat setting and a round brush to style your hair while blow-drying. For a smooth and polished look, use a straightener to straighten or curl specific sections of your hair.

6. Apply Styling Products

Styling products like pomades, waxes, and gels can be great allies in controlling your thick hair. Use a small amount of product and distribute it evenly throughout your hair to add texture and hold. Be cautious not to use too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

7. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are crucial for maintaining healthy and manageable hair. Schedule regular appointments with your hairstylist to trim the ends and remove any split ends or damaged hair. This will prevent your hair from looking unruly and help it grow stronger and more manageable.

8. Experiment with Hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits your thick hair the best. Try out different lengths, textures, and styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Consider consulting with a professional hairstylist for personalized advice.

9. Use Conditioner as a Styling Product

If you want a more natural and relaxed look, try using conditioner as a styling product. Apply a small amount of conditioner to damp hair and comb it through. This will help define your natural waves or curls and give your hair a softer and more effortless appearance.

10. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Lastly, embrace your natural texture and work with what you have. Instead of constantly fighting against your thick hair, learn to embrace its volume and uniqueness. Opt for hairstyles that enhance your natural texture and make you feel confident in your own skin.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix your thick hair and achieve a stylish and manageable look in 2023. Experiment, be patient, and don't be afraid to seek professional advice if needed. Embrace your thick hair and let it be a defining feature of your personal style!
