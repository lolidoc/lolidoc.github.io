The 1960s: A Decade of Iconic Hairstyles

The 1960s was a decade that brought about a revolution in fashion and style, and the hairstyles of that era were no exception. From the sleek and sophisticated look of Jackie Kennedy to the bold and edgy style of the Beatles, the 1960s hairstyles were diverse and iconic. If you're looking to recreate the glamour of the 1960s, here's a step-by-step guide on how to do a 1960s hairstyle.

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair. Towel-dry your hair gently and apply a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from the heat of styling tools.

Step 2: Create Volume

To achieve the signature voluminous look of the 1960s, you'll need to create some height at the crown of your head. Section your hair and gently tease the roots with a fine-tooth comb. Spray some hairspray at the roots to hold the volume in place.

Step 3: Style Your Bangs

If you have bangs, style them in a swept-back or side-swept manner to create a soft and feminine look. You can use a round brush and a blow dryer to achieve the desired shape and volume.

Step 4: Create Waves or Curls

The 1960s hairstyles often featured soft waves or curls. Depending on your hair length and preference, you can use hot rollers, a curling iron, or even braids to create the desired waves or curls. Remember to curl away from your face for an authentic 1960s look.

Step 5: Pin It Up

Once you have achieved the desired waves or curls, gently brush through your hair to create soft and loose waves. Take small sections of hair and pin them up at the back of your head using bobby pins. This will give your hairstyle a polished and elegant look.

Step 6: Add Accessories

The 1960s were known for their love of accessories. To complete your 1960s hairstyle, consider adding some accessories like headbands, hair scarves, or decorative clips. These accessories will add a touch of glamour and authenticity to your look.

Step 7: Set Your Style

To ensure that your 1960s hairstyle lasts all day, use a strong-hold hairspray to set your style. Mist the hairspray all over your hair, focusing on the pinned sections and the crown to keep the volume intact.

Step 8: Finishing Touches

Once your hairstyle is set, take a moment to check for any loose strands or flyaways. Use a small amount of hair serum or pomade to tame any frizz or flyaways and give your hairstyle a polished and sleek finish.

Step 9: Own the Look

Now that you have mastered the art of a 1960s hairstyle, it's time to rock it with confidence. Pair your hairstyle with vintage-inspired clothing and makeup to complete the retro look. Embrace the spirit of the 1960s and channel your inner fashion icon.

Step 10: Experiment and Have Fun

The beauty of the 1960s hairstyles is their versatility. Don't be afraid to experiment with different variations and adaptations of the classic 1960s looks. Whether you go for a sleek updo or tousled waves, let your creativity shine and have fun with your hairstyle.

With this step-by-step guide, you can now confidently recreate the iconic hairstyles of the 1960s. Embrace the retro charm, and get ready to turn heads with your glamorous and timeless look!
