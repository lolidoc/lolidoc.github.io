Are you struggling to find a way to make space buns with your short hair without using bobby pins? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss different methods and techniques to achieve perfect space buns with short hair, without the need for bobby pins.

Having short hair can sometimes make it challenging to create certain hairstyles, especially when it comes to updos like space buns. Bobby pins are often used to secure the hair in place, but they can be uncomfortable and may not hold well in short hair. This can be frustrating for those who want to rock the trendy space bun look.

But fear not, there are alternative ways to create space buns with short hair without relying on bobby pins. One method involves using small hair elastics to secure the hair, while another technique utilizes braiding to keep the buns in place. These methods are not only easy to do but also provide a secure hold for your space buns.

In summary, if you have short hair and want to achieve space buns without using bobby pins, there are alternative methods you can try. Using small hair elastics or braiding your hair can provide a secure hold and create the perfect space bun look. Now, let's dive deeper into each method and explore how to make space buns with short hair without bobby pins.

Method 1: Small Hair Elastics

Using small hair elastics is one of the easiest ways to create space buns with short hair. Here's how you can do it:

1. Start by parting your hair down the middle or to the side, depending on your preference.

2. Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it tightly towards the back of your head.

3. Once you've twisted the hair, coil it into a bun shape and secure it with a small hair elastic. Make sure to wrap the elastic around the bun multiple times to ensure a secure hold.

4. Repeat the process on the other side to create a second space bun.

5. To add extra hold and prevent any loose strands, you can use hairspray or texturizing spray on the buns.

Using small hair elastics allows you to achieve space buns with short hair without the need for bobby pins. The elastics provide a secure hold and keep the buns in place throughout the day.

Method 2: Braiding

Braiding your hair is another effective method to create space buns without bobby pins. Here's how you can do it:

1. Start by parting your hair down the middle or to the side.

2. Take a small section of hair from one side and begin braiding it towards the back of your head. Secure the end of the braid with a small hair elastic.

3. Repeat the process on the other side to create a second braid.

4. Once both braids are complete, take one braid and coil it into a bun shape. Secure it with a small hair elastic.

5. Repeat the process with the other braid to create the second space bun.

By braiding your hair, you not only create a stylish look but also provide a secure hold for your space buns. The braids act as a foundation, keeping the buns in place without the need for bobby pins.

Conclusion of How to Make Space Buns with Short Hair without Bobby Pins

Creating space buns with short hair can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. By using small hair elastics or braiding your hair, you can achieve the perfect space bun look without the need for bobby pins. Experiment with different techniques and find what works best for your hair type and length. With a little practice, you'll be rocking the trendy space buns with your short hair in no time!
