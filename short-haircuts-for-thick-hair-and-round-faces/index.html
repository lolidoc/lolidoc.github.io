Short Haircuts for Thick Hair and Round Faces

When it comes to short haircuts for thick hair and round faces, there are several options to choose from. One popular style is the pixie cut. This short hairstyle features cropped sides and longer layers on top, creating a playful and edgy look. The pixie cut adds height to the crown of the head, elongating the face and creating a more oval appearance.

Another option is the bob haircut. This classic style can be customized to suit round faces by adding layers and texture. A layered bob can help to reduce the bulkiness of thick hair, while still maintaining a chic and stylish look. The bob can be worn straight or with waves, depending on personal preference.

A short shag haircut is also a great choice for those with thick hair and round faces. This style features choppy layers and texture, creating a trendy and modern look. The short shag adds movement and dimension to the hair, while still maintaining a manageable length.

Lastly, a pixie bob haircut is a hybrid style that combines the best of both worlds. This short haircut features shorter layers on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. The pixie bob adds height to the crown of the head and creates a more elongated appearance.
