Mens Hairstyles for Over 50 with Round Face: The Classic Comb-Over

One of the most popular mens hairstyles for over 50 with round face is the classic comb-over. This style features longer hair on top that is swept to one side, while the sides and back are kept short and neatly trimmed. The comb-over is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions, making it a great choice for men of all ages.

The comb-over works well for men with round faces as it adds height to the top of the head, creating the illusion of a longer face shape. This style also helps to balance out the roundness of the face and draw attention upwards, towards the hair on top. To achieve the perfect comb-over, use a good quality styling product to add volume and hold to the hair, and use a comb or brush to sweep the hair to one side.
