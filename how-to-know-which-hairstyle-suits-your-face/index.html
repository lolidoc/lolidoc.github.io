Understanding Face Shapes

Face shapes can be categorized into five main types: round, oval, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. Each shape has its own unique characteristics and features. By identifying your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that enhances your best features and balances out any asymmetry.
