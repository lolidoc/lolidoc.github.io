Best Short Natural Hairstyles for Round Faces: The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that looks great on round faces. This short hairstyle features cropped hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. The pixie cut adds height and length to the face, creating a more elongated look. It also helps to accentuate the cheekbones and draw attention to the eyes.

For those with round faces, it's best to opt for a pixie cut with added volume on top. This can be achieved through techniques such as layering and texturizing. Adding volume on top helps to create the illusion of a longer face shape and adds balance to the overall look. You can also experiment with different styling options, such as side-swept bangs or a deep side part, to further enhance the face shape.

Best Short Natural Hairstyles for Round Faces: The Bob

The bob is another classic hairstyle that flatters round faces. This short hairstyle features hair that is cut to chin-length or just above the shoulders. The bob helps to create the illusion of length and adds structure to the face. It can also help to slim down the face and draw attention to the jawline.

For those with round faces, it's best to opt for a bob with layers and texture. This helps to add movement and volume to the hair, creating a more flattering look. You can also experiment with different styling options, such as adding waves or curls, to further enhance the face shape. Asymmetrical bobs can also be a great choice for round faces, as they add angles and create a more elongated look.

Best Short Natural Hairstyles for Round Faces: The Afro

If you have natural hair, embracing your afro is a great way to showcase your round face. The afro hairstyle adds volume and height, creating a bold and confident look. It also helps to add balance to the face and draw attention to your best features.

For those with round faces, it's best to opt for an afro that is slightly elongated and not too wide. This can be achieved through techniques such as stretching the hair or using styling tools to create more length. Adding height on top can also help to create a more elongated look. You can experiment with different styling options, such as picking the hair at the roots or adding accessories, to further enhance the face shape.

Best Short Natural Hairstyles for Round Faces: The Mohawk

The Mohawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can be customized to suit your round face. This short hairstyle features shaved or very short hair on the sides, with longer hair in the middle. The Mohawk adds height and length to the face, creating a more elongated look. It also helps to accentuate the cheekbones and draw attention to the eyes.

For those with round faces, it's best to opt for a Mohawk with more volume on top. This can be achieved through techniques such as styling the hair upwards or adding texture. Adding height on top helps to create the illusion of a longer face shape and adds balance to the overall look. You can also experiment with different styling options, such as adding braids or twists, to further enhance the face shape.

Best Short Natural Hairstyles for Round Faces: Conclusion

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short natural hairstyle for a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a short natural hairstyle for a round face. Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that help to create angles and add length to the face.

Q: How can I add volume to my short natural hairstyle for a round face?

A: You can add volume to your short natural hairstyle for a round face by using volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray. You can also use styling techniques, such as backcombing or teasing, to create more volume.

Q: Can I wear an updo with a short natural hairstyle for a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear an updo with a short natural hairstyle for a round face. Opt for an updo that adds height on top and creates the illusion of a longer face shape. You can also add accessories, such as headbands or hairpins, to further enhance the look.

Q: How often should I trim my short natural hairstyle for a round face?

A: It is recommended to trim your short natural hairstyle for a round face every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style. Regular trims help to keep the hair healthy and prevent split ends.
