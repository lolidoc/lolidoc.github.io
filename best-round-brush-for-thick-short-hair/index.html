Best Round Brush for Thick Short Hair: Tips for Effective Use

To get the most out of your round brush for thick short hair, follow these tips:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  3. Section your hair and work in small sections for optimal control.
  4. Use the round brush to lift the roots and create volume.
  5. Blow dry the hair in the direction you want it to fall.
  6. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.
  7. Use a hairspray or styling product to hold the style in place.
