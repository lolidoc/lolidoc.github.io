Tips for Using the Best Round Hair Brush for Short Fine Hair

Here are some tips to get the most out of your best round hair brush for short fine hair:

1. Start with dry hair

Using a round brush on wet hair can cause breakage. Make sure your hair is completely dry before styling.

2. Use heat protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

3. Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to style. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

4. Use the right technique

Hold the brush horizontally and wrap small sections of hair around the barrel, away from your face. Use the brush to pull the hair away from the scalp and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

5. Finish with a blast of cold air

After styling, use the cool setting on your hairdryer to set the shape and add shine.
