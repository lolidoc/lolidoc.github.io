Best Hairstyles for Round Face Male

One of the best hairstyles for round face male is the pompadour. This classic hairstyle involves keeping the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer and styled upwards. The height and volume created by the pompadour help to elongate the face and create a more angular look.

Another great option for men with round faces is the quiff. This hairstyle is similar to the pompadour but involves less height and more volume at the front. The quiff can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back or messy, depending on your personal preference.

If you prefer a more edgy and modern look, the faux hawk is a great choice. This hairstyle involves keeping the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the middle section longer and styled upwards. The faux hawk creates a vertical line that helps to elongate the face and create a more defined look.

For those who prefer a more relaxed and casual look, a messy textured hairstyle can work well with a round face. This hairstyle involves keeping the hair longer with layers and adding texture with a styling product. The messy texture helps to create a more angular and less rounded appearance.
