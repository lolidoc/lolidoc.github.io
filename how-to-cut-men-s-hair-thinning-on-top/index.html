Question and Answer

Q: Can I cut my own hair if it's thinning on top? A: While it's possible to cut your own hair, it's recommended to seek professional help, especially if you're dealing with thinning hair. A hairstylist can provide personalized advice and solutions to achieve the best results.

Q: How often should I trim my thinning hair? A: It's recommended to trim your thinning hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain a neat and tidy appearance. Regular trims can also help to remove split ends and promote healthier hair growth.
