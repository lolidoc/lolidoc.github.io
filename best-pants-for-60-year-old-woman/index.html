Q&A about the Best Pants for 60-Year-Old Women

Q: What are the best pants for 60-year-old women with a pear-shaped body?

A: Yes, 60-year-old women can definitely wear skinny jeans. Look for pairs with a higher rise and a stretchy fabric for comfort.
