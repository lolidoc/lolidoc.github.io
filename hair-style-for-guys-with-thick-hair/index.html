Are you a guy with thick hair who struggles to find the right hairstyle? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore various hair styles for guys with thick hair and provide you with tips and recommendations to help you achieve the perfect look.

Having thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it gives you plenty of volume and texture to work with. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. Many guys with thick hair struggle with frizz, unruliness, and an overall lack of control over their hair.

If you're tired of dealing with these pain points, we have the solution for you. By choosing the right hairstyle and using the appropriate styling techniques and products, you can transform your thick hair into a stylish and manageable mane.

In this article, we will discuss the best hair styles for guys with thick hair, provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve each look, and share some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best. Whether you prefer a short and sleek style or a long and tousled look, we have options for every preference and hair length.

The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless and sophisticated hairstyle that works well for guys with thick hair. This style features short sides and back with longer hair on top that is swept back and styled with volume. To achieve the pompadour, you will need a good pomade or styling cream to add texture and hold to your hair.

Start by blow drying your hair to add volume and lift at the roots. Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of pomade or styling cream to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair back and create the desired shape and volume. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Messy Textured Crop

If you prefer a more casual and effortless look, the messy textured crop is a great option for guys with thick hair. This style is characterized by a short and textured cut with a slightly messy and tousled appearance. To achieve this look, you will need a good texturizing spray or paste to add definition and separation to your hair.

Start by applying a small amount of texturizing spray or paste to your hair and distribute it evenly. Use your fingers to create texture and separation by tousling your hair and scrunching it up. You can also use a comb or brush to create a more defined and structured look. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Undercut with a Side Part

The undercut with a side part is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that works well for guys with thick hair. This style features a short and tapered cut on the sides and back with longer hair on top that is styled to the side with a deep side part. To achieve this look, you will need a good styling gel or pomade to add hold and shine to your hair.

Start by blow drying your hair to add volume and lift at the roots. Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of styling gel or pomade to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots. Use a comb or your fingers to create a deep side part and style your hair to the side. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Conclusion

In conclusion, guys with thick hair have a wide range of hairstyle options to choose from. Whether you prefer a classic and polished look or a more casual and tousled style, there is a hair style for guys with thick hair that will suit your preferences and hair type. By choosing the right products and styling techniques, you can achieve a stylish and manageable hairstyle that will make you look and feel your best.

So why wait? Try out one of these hair styles for guys with thick hair today and see the difference it can make!
