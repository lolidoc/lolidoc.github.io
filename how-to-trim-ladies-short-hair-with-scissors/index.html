The first step in trimming ladies short hair with scissors is to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It is important to use proper hair cutting scissors as regular scissors can damage the hair.

Now that you have your tools ready, it's time to start trimming. Begin by parting your hair into sections and combing through each section to remove any tangles. Take a small section of hair between your fingers and twist it slightly. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more precise trim.

Next, carefully trim the ends of the twisted section using the hair cutting scissors. Start by cutting a small amount and gradually trim more if needed. Remember to cut in a straight line and avoid cutting too much at once to prevent any mistakes.
