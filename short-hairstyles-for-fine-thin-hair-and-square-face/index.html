How to Short Hairstyles for Fine Thin Hair and Square Face

When it comes to styling short hairstyles for fine thin hair and a square face, there are a few key techniques to keep in mind:

1. Use volumizing products: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair before blow-drying. This will help add volume and create the illusion of thicker hair.

2. Blow-dry with a round brush: Use a round brush while blow-drying to add volume and create soft, bouncy curls. Focus on lifting the roots to create lift and volume.

3. Add texture: Use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture and create a more lived-in look. This will help add dimension and movement to your short hairstyle.

4. Consider styling techniques: Experiment with different styling techniques, such as tousling the hair with your fingers or using a curling iron to create loose curls or waves.
