History and Myth of Red Hair

The history of red hair dates back thousands of years and is often associated with unique characteristics and myths. In ancient civilizations, red hair was seen as a sign of special powers or connections to the supernatural. In some cultures, red hair was even believed to bring good luck and fortune.

Throughout history, red hair has been both admired and stigmatized. In certain periods, red-haired individuals were considered exotic and desirable, while in others, they were associated with negative stereotypes and discrimination.
