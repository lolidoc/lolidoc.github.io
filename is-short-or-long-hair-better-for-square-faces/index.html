On the other hand, long hair can also look stunning on square faces. Long hairstyles can help to soften your features and add femininity to your look. Question and Answer

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle with a square face?

A: Absolutely! Short hairstyles can look stunning on square faces, as they can help to emphasize your strong jawline and create a more angular look. Talk to your hairstylist about which short hairstyle would best suit your face shape.

Q: Will long hair make my square face look even more angular?

A: Not necessarily. Long hair can actually soften your features and add femininity to your look. Loose waves or curls can create a more romantic and ethereal look, while straight and sleek styles can add sophistication and elegance.

Q: How can I add volume to my hair with a square face?

A: To add volume to your hair with a square face, consider hairstyles that create height at the crown of your head. This can help to balance out your features and create a more flattering look. Talk to your hairstylist about different volumizing techniques and products.

Q: Should I consider bangs with a square face?

A: Bangs can be a great option for square faces, as they can help to soften your features and add dimension to your look. Consider side-swept bangs or curtain bangs, as they can create a more flattering and face-framing effect.
