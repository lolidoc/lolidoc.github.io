As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose its natural volume. This can be a challenge for women with thick wavy hair, as it can be difficult to manage and style. Additionally, finding a hairstyle that flatters our facial features and complements our age can be a daunting task. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and achieve a youthful and trendy look.

So, what are some of the best short hairstyles for thick wavy hair over 50? Let's take a look:

1. The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can be tailored to suit any age and hair type. For women over 50 with thick wavy hair, a pixie cut can add volume and texture to their locks. This short and edgy hairstyle is low-maintenance and perfect for women who want to embrace their natural waves.

2. The Bob

The bob is a classic and elegant hairstyle that never goes out of style. For women over 50 with thick wavy hair, a bob can be a flattering and youthful option. Whether you opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob, this hairstyle can add movement and dimension to your hair. It can also be styled in various ways to suit different occasions and personal preferences.

3. The Shag

The shag is a trendy and effortless hairstyle that is perfect for women over 50 with thick wavy hair. This layered haircut can add texture and volume to your locks, creating a chic and modern look. The shag is also low-maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways, making it a versatile choice for women who want to switch up their look.

4. The Layered Crop

The layered crop is a stylish and sophisticated hairstyle that suits women of all ages. For women over 50 with thick wavy hair, a layered crop can add movement and dimension to their locks. This short and textured hairstyle can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style, creating a flattering and youthful look.

5. The Textured Pixie

The textured pixie is a modern and edgy hairstyle that is perfect for women over 50 with thick wavy hair. This short and tousled haircut can add volume and texture to your locks, creating a chic and trendy look. The textured pixie is also low-maintenance and can be styled in various ways to suit different occasions and personal preferences.

Conclusion

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have thick wavy hair over 50?

A: Absolutely! While short hairstyles are a popular choice for women over 50 with thick wavy hair, you can still rock long locks if that's what you prefer. Just make sure to get regular trims to avoid split ends and keep your hair healthy and manageable.

Q: What styling products should I use for my thick wavy hair?

A: For thick wavy hair, it's best to use products that enhance your natural texture without weighing it down. Opt for lightweight mousses, texturizing sprays, and curl-enhancing creams to define your waves and add volume. Avoid heavy products that can make your hair look greasy or flat.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut if I have thick wavy hair?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on your personal preference and hair growth rate. However, it's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends and promote hair growth.

Q: Can I color my thick wavy hair over 50?

A: Of course! Coloring your hair can be a fun way to change up your look and enhance your natural beauty. Whether you opt for highlights, lowlights, or a full color change, make sure to consult with a professional stylist to choose the right shade and technique that suits your skin tone and personal style.
