Short Ethnic Hairstyles for Round Faces

Introduction

Having a round face shape can be both a blessing and a challenge when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. While a round face is often associated with youthfulness and a soft appearance, it can also be tricky to find hairstyles that flatter your facial features. If you have short hair and an ethnic background, there are plenty of trendy and stylish options that can enhance your natural beauty and highlight your round face shape.

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can work well for round faces. The short length helps to elongate the face and draw attention to your facial features. You can opt for a sleek and polished pixie cut or add some texture and volume for a more edgy look. Consider experimenting with different side parts or adding a side-swept fringe to further enhance your features.

2. Bob with Bangs

A bob hairstyle is another great option for round faces. Opt for a short bob that ends just below the chin and pair it with bangs. The bangs will help to create angles and add definition to your face shape. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs can also be flattering options. Play around with different lengths and layers to find the perfect bob hairstyle that suits your personal style.

3. Faux Hawk

If you're looking for a bold and edgy short hairstyle, consider trying a faux hawk. This hairstyle involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides very short while leaving a longer strip of hair in the center. The faux hawk can help to create the illusion of length and height, which can be flattering for round faces. Style the longer strip of hair upwards to create the signature faux hawk look.

4. Textured Crop

A textured crop is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle that can work well for round faces. This hairstyle involves cutting the hair short all over and adding texture and layers for a more voluminous look. The textured crop is perfect for those who prefer a tousled and effortless hairstyle. Use a texturizing spray or wax to add definition to your hair and enhance the overall texture.

5. Afro Puff

If you have natural hair and a round face, consider embracing your curls with an afro puff hairstyle. This hairstyle involves gathering your natural hair into a high ponytail or bun at the top of your head. The afro puff can help to elongate your face and create a flattering silhouette. You can also add accessories like headbands or hair clips to enhance the overall look.

6. Mohawk

A mohawk hairstyle can be a daring and stylish choice for round faces. This hairstyle involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides very short while leaving a longer strip of hair in the center. The mohawk can help to create the illusion of length and height, which can balance out the roundness of your face. Style the longer strip of hair upwards or create different patterns to personalize your mohawk.

7. Tapered Cut

A tapered cut is a versatile and chic hairstyle that can work well for round faces. This hairstyle involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back while leaving more length on the top. The tapered cut can help to create a more angular and defined look, which can flatter round faces. You can keep the top section longer and style it in various ways, such as slicked back or textured.

8. Braided Bob

If you want to add some flair to your short bob hairstyle, consider incorporating braids. A braided bob can add a unique and stylish touch to your look while also helping to frame your face. You can experiment with different braid styles, such as cornrows or box braids, to create a personalized and eye-catching hairstyle.

9. Curly Mohawk

If you have curly hair and a round face, a curly mohawk can be a fantastic option. This hairstyle involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides very short while leaving the curly hair on top. The curly mohawk can help to create a bold and voluminous look that highlights your natural curls and adds dimension to your face shape.

10. Side-Swept Pixie

A side-swept pixie is a chic and elegant hairstyle that can flatter round faces. This hairstyle involves keeping the hair on one side longer and sweeping it to the side. The asymmetrical look can create the illusion of length and add sophistication to your overall appearance. Style the longer side with some texturizing products or add a deep side part for a more dramatic effect.
