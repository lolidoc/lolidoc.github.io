How To Create A Sixties Hairstyle

The Iconic Sixties Hairstyle

The 1960s was a decade known for its bold and iconic fashion and beauty trends. One of the most memorable aspects of this era was the unique hairstyles that became popular during this time. From the voluminous beehives to the sleek and straight looks, the sixties hairstyles continue to inspire and captivate us even today. In this article, we will guide you on how to recreate the classic sixties hairstyle and add a touch of retro glamour to your look.

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair thoroughly. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your locks. Towel dry your hair gently and apply a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools.

Step 2: Add Volume

To achieve the iconic sixties volume, you can use a few different techniques. One option is to use hot rollers or a large-barrel curling iron to create loose curls throughout your hair. Another option is to tease your hair at the crown using a fine-tooth comb. This will create the desired height and volume for your sixties hairstyle.

Step 3: Create a Deep Side Part

To achieve the signature sixties look, create a deep side part in your hair. Use a tail comb to create a precise parting, starting from the front of your hairline and extending towards the back. Comb the hair on either side of the parting to ensure a smooth and polished finish.

Step 4: Smooth and Pin

Next, take a brush and gently smooth the top layer of your hair to give it a sleek and polished appearance. Use bobby pins to secure the sides of your hair behind your ears, creating a clean and elegant look. This will also help to keep the volume intact throughout the day.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

To complete the sixties hairstyle, apply a generous amount of hairspray to hold everything in place. This will ensure that your hair remains voluminous and styled throughout the day. You can also add a headband or a decorative hair accessory to enhance the retro vibe of your look.

Rocking the Sixties Hairstyle

The sixties hairstyle is versatile and can be adapted to suit various occasions. Whether you're attending a retro-themed party or simply want to add a touch of vintage charm to your everyday look, this hairstyle is perfect for you. Pair it with a winged eyeliner and a bold red lip to complete the sixties-inspired makeup look.

Conclusion

Creating a sixties hairstyle is a fun and glamorous way to pay homage to the iconic fashion era. With a few simple steps, you can achieve the classic sixties look and turn heads wherever you go. Embrace the retro vibes and unleash your inner vintage diva with this timeless hairstyle.
