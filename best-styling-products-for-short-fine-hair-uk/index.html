2. Volumizing Mousse

A volumizing mousse is another great product for adding volume and body to short fine hair. Apply a small amount of mousse to your roots and distribute it evenly through your hair. Then, blow dry your hair using a round brush to create lift and volume.

A lightweight hairspray is essential for keeping your style in place without weighing your hair down. Look for a hairspray that provides a flexible hold and adds shine to your hair. Spray it onto your finished style to lock it in place.
