How to Style Thick Wavy Hair Medium Length: Tips and Techniques

Now that you have a better understanding of how to style thick wavy hair medium length, let's dive into some tips and techniques:

1. Start with freshly washed hair: Clean hair provides a good base for styling. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your waves hydrated.

2. Apply a styling product: After washing your hair, apply a curl-enhancing product or mousse to enhance your natural waves. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a diffuser: Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing the natural waves. Use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair gently with the diffuser.

4. Try the scrunching method: To enhance your waves, scrunch your hair gently with your hands while it's still damp. This will encourage your waves to form and reduce frizz.
