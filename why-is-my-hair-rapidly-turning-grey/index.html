The Hidden Secret of why is my hair rapidly turning grey

The hidden secret behind why hair turns grey rapidly lies in the biology of hair growth. Each hair strand on our scalp contains pigment-producing cells called melanocytes. These cells produce melanin, the pigment responsible for giving hair its color. As we age, the melanocytes gradually decrease in number and become less efficient at producing melanin. This leads to a loss of color and the appearance of grey hair.
