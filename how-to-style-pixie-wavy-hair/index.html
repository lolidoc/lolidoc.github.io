How To Style Pixie Wavy Hair

Introduction

If you have pixie wavy hair, you're in luck! This hairstyle is not only trendy but also versatile. With the right styling techniques, you can create different looks for various occasions. Whether you want to enhance your natural waves or add more texture, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of styling pixie wavy hair, providing you with tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look.

1. Start with the Right Cut

The first step to styling pixie wavy hair is to have the right cut. Consult with your hairstylist to ensure that the length and layers complement your natural waves. A well-executed cut will make it easier to style and maintain your pixie wavy hair.

2. Enhance Your Natural Waves

If you want to enhance your natural waves, start by applying a curl-enhancing mousse or gel to damp hair. Use your fingers to scrunch the product into your hair, encouraging the waves to form. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to gently dry your waves. Avoid touching or combing your hair while it dries to prevent frizz.

3. Add Texture with Sea Salt Spray

If you prefer a more textured look, try using a sea salt spray. Spritz it onto damp hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers. The salt spray will give your pixie wavy hair a beachy, tousled appearance. You can let it air dry or use a diffuser for added volume.

4. Create Loose Waves with a Curling Iron

If you want to switch up your look, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your dry hair. Then, wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron barrel, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for all the sections, making sure to curl in different directions for a more natural look.

5. Define Your Curls with a Curl Cream

To define your curls and reduce frizz, apply a curl cream or mousse to your damp hair. Scrunch your hair with your fingers and let it air dry or use a diffuser. This will give your pixie wavy hair more structure and definition.

6. Use a Texturizing Spray for Effortless Volume

If you're looking for extra volume, try using a texturizing spray. Apply it to dry hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Gently tousle your hair with your fingers to distribute the product and create volume. This will give your pixie wavy hair a more effortless and lived-in look.

7. Opt for a Sleek and Straight Look

For a sleek and straight look, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your dry hair. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, working in small sections. Comb through each section with a fine-toothed comb before straightening to ensure a smooth finish. Finish with a shine serum or hair oil for added gloss.

8. Play with Accessories

Add some flair to your pixie wavy hair by incorporating accessories. Use bobby pins, headbands, or hair clips to create different styles. You can pin back one side of your hair or add a headband for a more playful look. Experiment with different accessories to find what suits your style.

9. Maintain Your Pixie Wavy Hair

To keep your pixie wavy hair looking its best, it's important to maintain it regularly. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your pixie cut. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

10. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Finally, don't be afraid to embrace your natural texture. Pixie wavy hair is unique and beautiful, so let it shine. Embrace the waves, curls, and texture that your hair naturally has. With the right styling techniques and products, you can enhance your natural beauty and rock your pixie wavy hair with confidence.
