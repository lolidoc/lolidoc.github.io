Does Short Hair Make Guys Look Older: Explained

When discussing whether short hair makes guys look older, it's essential to understand the concept of face shape and how it relates to aging. Different face shapes age differently, and certain hairstyles can accentuate or minimize the signs of aging.

On the other hand, those with square or angular faces may prefer shorter hairstyles to emphasize their features and maintain a more youthful appearance.

Additionally, hair texture can also play a role in how short hair affects someone's age. Thicker, coarser hair tends to have a more youthful appearance, while thinning or fine hair may benefit from shorter styles that create the illusion of thickness.
