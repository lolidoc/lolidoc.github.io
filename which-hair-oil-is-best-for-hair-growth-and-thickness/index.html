What is Argan Oil?

Argan oil is a natural oil that is extracted from the nuts of the argan tree. It has been used for centuries by the Berber people of Morocco for its numerous health and beauty benefits. Argan oil is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that nourish the hair and scalp, promoting healthy hair growth and thickness.

Question and Answer

Q: Can argan oil be used on all hair types?

A: Yes, argan oil is suitable for all hair types, including curly, straight, and chemically-treated hair.
