To trim short relaxed hair yourself, you will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then dry it completely. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Next, section off your hair into small sections. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and position the scissors horizontally at the desired length you want to cut. Slowly close the scissors, cutting a small amount of hair at a time. Repeat this process for each section of hair until you have achieved your desired length.
