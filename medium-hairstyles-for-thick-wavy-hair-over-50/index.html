Medium Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair Over 50: Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Styling and maintaining medium hairstyles for thick wavy hair over 50 can be simple with the right techniques. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet to prevent breakage and minimize frizz.
  • Apply a styling product, such as mousse or curl-enhancing cream, to damp hair to enhance your natural waves and add definition.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting to minimize frizz and enhance volume.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to add more defined curls or waves to your hair. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds before releasing.
  • Finish off your style with a lightweight hairspray to hold your waves or curls in place without weighing them down.
  • For maintenance, trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and keep it looking healthy and fresh. Consider scheduling regular deep conditioning treatments to nourish and hydrate your hair.
