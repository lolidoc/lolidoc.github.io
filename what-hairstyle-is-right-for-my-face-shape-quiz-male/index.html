Listicle: Top 5 Hairstyles for Different Face Shapes

1. Oval Face Shape: The oval face shape is considered the most versatile, as it suits a wide range of hairstyles. Opt for a classic short cut or experiment with longer hairstyles that add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Square Face Shape: To soften the angles of a square face shape, consider a hairstyle with layers or textured cuts. A side-swept fringe or a tapered undercut can also help balance the proportions.

3. Round Face Shape: For a round face shape, choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face. Textured crops, pompadours, or angular fringes can create the illusion of a longer face shape.

4. Heart Face Shape: To complement a heart-shaped face, opt for hairstyles that add width to the lower part of your face. Side-swept bangs, layered cuts, or medium-length hairstyles are great options.

5. Diamond Face Shape: With a diamond face shape, consider hairstyles that minimize the width of the cheekbones and add volume to the forehead and chin area. Textured quiffs, messy fringes, or medium-length hairstyles work well for this face shape.
