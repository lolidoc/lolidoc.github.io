Tips for Using the Best Curling Iron for Short Hair 2020 UK

Here are some tips for using the best curling iron for short hair 2020 UK:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair: Ensure that your hair is clean and dry before using the curling iron. This will help the curls to hold better.
  2. Use heat protectant: Before using any heat styling tool, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  3. Section your hair: Divide your hair into small sections before curling. This will ensure that each strand is evenly curled.
  4. Wrap the hair around the barrel: Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the barrel, starting from the root and working towards the ends. Hold for a few seconds and then release.
  5. Hold the curl: After releasing the curl, hold it in your hand for a few seconds to allow it to cool and set.
  6. Finish with hairspray: To ensure that the curls last all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray.
