How To Style Short Natural Hair Step By Step

Written by Exel Dec 22, 2022 · 4 min read

Short natural hair can be versatile and beautiful allowing you to showcase your unique style Whether you re a beginner or an experienced naturalista this step by step guide will help you achieve stunning hairstyles that highlight your natural texture In this article we will explore various techniques and tips to make the most of your short natural hair

Table of Contents

Introduction

Short natural hair can be versatile and beautiful, allowing you to showcase your unique style. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced naturalista, this step-by-step guide will help you achieve stunning hairstyles that highlight your natural texture. In this article, we will explore various techniques and tips to make the most of your short natural hair.

1. Start with Clean and Moisturized Hair

Before diving into styling, it's crucial to have a clean canvas. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and a nourishing conditioner. This will remove any product buildup and provide the necessary moisture for styling.

2. Choose the Right Products

Selecting the right products for your hair type is essential. Look for moisturizing leave-in conditioners, styling creams, and natural oils to keep your curls hydrated and defined. Avoid products with harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Short natural hair has its own unique beauty, so embrace your natural texture. Experiment with different curl patterns and find the ones that suit you best. From coils to kinks, each texture is beautiful in its own way.

4. Protective Styling

If you want to give your hair a break from daily manipulation, opt for protective styles. These include braids, twists, and bantu knots that keep your hair tucked away and protected from environmental damage. Remember to moisturize your hair before installing any protective style.

5. Twist Outs

One popular style for short natural hair is the twist out. Start by sectioning your hair and applying a styling cream or gel. Twist each section tightly and allow them to dry. Once your hair is completely dry, unravel the twists to reveal defined and bouncy curls.

6. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are not only a stylish choice but also a great way to achieve heatless curls. Divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly. Wrap the twisted section around itself to form a knot and secure it with bobby pins. Leave the knots overnight and unravel them in the morning for beautiful curls.

7. Finger Coils

Finger coils are a great option for defining your natural curls. Apply a styling cream or gel to your damp hair and use your fingers to twirl small sections of hair around themselves. This technique will create defined coils that can last for several days.

8. Accessorize

Add flair to your short natural hair by accessorizing with headbands, scarves, or hair clips. These accessories not only enhance your style but also keep your hair away from your face. Experiment with different colors and patterns to find your signature look.

9. Regular Trims

Keeping your hair healthy and free from split ends is crucial for maintaining your short natural hair. Schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks to remove any damaged ends and promote healthy growth.

10. Embrace the Journey

Styling short natural hair is a journey of self-discovery and embracing your unique beauty. Celebrate every step of the process and enjoy experimenting with different styles. Remember, confidence is the key to rocking any hairstyle with grace.

Conclusion

Styling short natural hair can be fun and creative. By following these steps, you'll be able to achieve a variety of stunning hairstyles that showcase your natural texture. Remember to keep your hair moisturized, protect it with gentle styling products, and embrace your unique beauty. With time and practice, you'll become a pro at styling your short natural hair.
