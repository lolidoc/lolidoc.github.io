What is Pomade?

Pomade is a hair product that is used to style and hold hair in place. It typically has a thick, wax-like consistency that allows for easy application and manipulation. Pomade is known for its strong hold and ability to add texture and definition to hair, making it a popular choice for those with thick wavy hair.

The History and Myth of Pomade

Pomade has a long history dating back to ancient times. It was originally made from animal fats and used by ancient Egyptians to style their hair. Over the years, the ingredients and formulations of pomade have evolved, but its purpose remains the same: to provide hold and style to hair.

Best Pomade for Thick Wavy Hair: How to Use It

To get the most out of your pomade, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingertips.
  3. Warm the pomade between your palms to soften it.
  4. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  5. Style your hair as desired, using your fingers or a comb.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to set your style.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use pomade on wet hair?

A: It is best to apply pomade to towel-dried hair to achieve the best results. Wet hair may dilute the pomade and make it less effective.

Q: How do I wash out pomade?

A: Pomade can be easily washed out with shampoo and warm water. It is important to thoroughly rinse your hair to remove all traces of the pomade.

Q: Can I restyle my hair after applying pomade?

A: Yes, you can easily restyle your hair after applying pomade. Simply dampen your hair with water and reshape it as desired.

Q: Can I use pomade every day?

A: Yes, you can use pomade every day. However, it is important to wash your hair thoroughly to remove any build-up of product.
