Short Hairstyles for Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

When it comes to short hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair, there are several options to choose from. One popular choice is the textured crop. This hairstyle involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving some length on top. The hair is then styled in a messy, textured manner to enhance the natural waves. Another option is the undercut, which features shaved sides and back with longer hair on top. This style works well with thick wavy hair as the contrast between the short sides and the volume on top creates a stylish and modern look.

An alternative to the textured crop and undercut is the classic short back and sides. This hairstyle involves keeping the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving enough length on top to create texture and movement. The hair is then styled with a matte pomade or wax to enhance the waves and give a natural, lived-in look. For those who prefer a more polished appearance, the side swept undercut is a great option. This hairstyle features a deep side part with the hair swept to one side, creating a sleek and sophisticated look.
