The History and Myth of Best Short Bob Haircuts 2021

The bob haircut has a long and storied history, dating back to the early 20th century. It first gained popularity in the 1920s as a symbol of rebellion and female empowerment. Women who cut their hair into a bob were seen as progressive and forward-thinking.

Over the years, the bob haircut has gone through various transformations and adaptations. In the 1960s, the bob became shorter and more geometric, reflecting the mod fashion trend of the time. In the 1980s, the bob saw a resurgence with the popularity of the "power bob," a sleek and polished version of the hairstyle.
