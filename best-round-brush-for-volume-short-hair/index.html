Tips for Using the Best Round Brush for Volume Short Hair

Using the best round brush for volume short hair requires some technique and practice. Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray for added lift.
  2. Divide your hair into sections for easier styling. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the brush, starting at the roots.
  4. Gently pull the brush through the hair, following the brush with a blow dryer to add heat.
  5. Repeat this process for each section of hair, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  6. Once your hair is dry and styled, use a light-hold hairspray to set the look and add extra volume.
