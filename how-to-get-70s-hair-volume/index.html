How To Get 70S Hair Volume In 2023

Written by Vannesa Jun 25, 2023 · 3 min read

The 70s hair volume trend has made a major comeback in 2023 with many people seeking to recreate the iconic hairstyles of that era Achieving voluminous hair like the stars of the 70s can seem challenging but with the right techniques and products you can easily rock this retro look In this article we will provide you with step by step instructions on how to get that gorgeous 70s hair volume

Table of Contents

Introduction

The 70s hair volume trend has made a major comeback in 2023, with many people seeking to recreate the iconic hairstyles of that era. Achieving voluminous hair like the stars of the 70s can seem challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can easily rock this retro look. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to get that gorgeous 70s hair volume.

Preparation

Before diving into the styling process, it's important to prepare your hair properly. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help create a solid foundation for achieving maximum volume.

After washing, gently towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or foam. This will add extra texture and lift to your hair, making it easier to achieve that 70s volume.

Blow Drying Techniques

To create the desired 70s hair volume, you need to master the art of blow drying. Start by flipping your hair upside down and using a round brush to lift the roots as you blow dry. This technique will help create volume at the roots, which is essential for achieving that retro look.

Once your hair is about 80% dry, flip it back and continue blow drying section by section. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the crown and direct the airflow towards the roots. This will further enhance the volume and create a bouncy effect.

Teasing for Extra Volume

Teasing, also known as backcombing, is a crucial step in achieving 70s hair volume. Take small sections of hair near the crown, hold them straight up, and gently comb downwards towards the roots using a fine-toothed comb. Repeat this process until you've teased the desired areas to create volume and lift.

Styling Products

Choosing the right styling products is essential to maintain the 70s hair volume throughout the day. Opt for a volumizing powder or a dry shampoo to give your roots an extra boost and add texture. These products will help create a long-lasting lift and prevent your hair from falling flat.

Finish off your styling with a light-hold hairspray to set the volume in place without weighing your hair down. Avoid heavy products that can make your hair appear greasy or stiff.

Final Touches

To complete your 70s hair volume look, consider incorporating some accessories. Headbands, scarves, and hair clips were popular in the 70s and can enhance your retro hairstyle. Experiment with different accessories to find the ones that best suit your personal style.

Remember to be gentle when brushing or combing your hair throughout the day to avoid flattening the volume. Use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle any knots or tangles.

Conclusion

With the right techniques, products, and a little bit of practice, you can easily achieve that stunning 70s hair volume. Follow the step-by-step instructions provided in this article, and you'll be rocking a retro hairstyle that turns heads in no time. Embrace the nostalgia and enjoy the voluminous and glamorous look of the 70s!
