Thick Short Hairstyles For Round Faces In 2023

Introduction

When it comes to hairstyles, it's important to choose one that flatters your face shape. For those with round faces, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. However, with the right style and length, you can accentuate your features and embrace your natural beauty. In this article, we will explore some trendy and chic short hairstyles specifically designed for individuals with round faces and thick hair in the year 2023.

The Classic Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless hairstyle that works well for round faces. This short and chic haircut helps to create the illusion of length and adds definition to your facial features. For individuals with thick hair, the pixie cut is ideal as it allows for easy maintenance and styling. Whether you opt for a sleek and polished look or a messy and textured style, the pixie cut is sure to turn heads.

Bob with Blunt Bangs

A bob haircut is another excellent choice for individuals with round faces and thick hair. By adding blunt bangs to the mix, you can create a striking and edgy look. The bob with blunt bangs frames the face beautifully, drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. This hairstyle is perfect for those looking to make a bold statement and add a touch of sophistication to their overall appearance.

Layered Lob

If you prefer a longer hairstyle, consider opting for a layered lob. This shoulder-length cut adds volume and movement to your hair, while the layers help to elongate your face. By incorporating soft, face-framing layers, you can create a more balanced and flattering look. The layered lob is versatile and can be styled both straight and wavy, allowing you to switch up your look effortlessly.

Textured Crop

The textured crop is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle that works wonders for round faces with thick hair. This short and choppy cut adds texture and dimension to your hair, giving it a modern and youthful vibe. The textured crop allows you to embrace your natural hair texture and requires minimal styling. Simply add some texturizing spray or wax to enhance the texture and create a tousled look.

Side-Swept Bangs with Shaggy Layers

If you're looking for a hairstyle that adds softness and femininity to your round face, consider side-swept bangs with shaggy layers. This haircut helps to create a slimming effect and adds movement to your hair. The side-swept bangs draw attention away from the width of your face, while the shaggy layers add texture and volume. This combination is perfect for those who want a playful and effortless look.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is a bold and fashionable choice for individuals with round faces and thick hair. This edgy haircut adds a modern twist to the classic bob. By incorporating uneven lengths, you can create a unique and eye-catching hairstyle. The asymmetrical bob helps to elongate the face and adds a touch of drama. Whether you opt for a sleek or tousled look, this hairstyle is sure to make a statement.

Pixie Bob

For those who want the best of both worlds - a pixie cut and a bob - the pixie bob is the perfect choice. This hybrid hairstyle combines the short length of a pixie cut with the versatility and elegance of a bob. The pixie bob helps to add structure to your face while maintaining a playful and youthful vibe. This hairstyle is ideal for individuals with thick hair as it allows for easy styling and maintenance.

Short Shag

The short shag is a trendy and effortless hairstyle for round faces with thick hair. This cut features layers throughout the hair, creating movement and texture. The short shag is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and stylish. By adding some texturizing spray or mousse, you can enhance the layers and create a tousled look. This hairstyle is versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Curly Bob

If you have naturally curly hair and a round face, a curly bob is a great option. This hairstyle embraces your natural texture and adds volume to your hair. The curly bob helps to elongate the face and creates a flattering and feminine look. By opting for a shorter length, you can prevent your hair from overwhelming your face. Embrace your curls and rock a curly bob with confidence.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle is essential for individuals with round faces and thick hair. By opting for a short haircut, you can enhance your features and create a flattering look. Whether you choose a classic pixie cut, a bob with blunt bangs, or a textured crop, there are plenty of options to suit your style and personality. Embrace your natural beauty and rock a trendy and chic hairstyle in 2023.
