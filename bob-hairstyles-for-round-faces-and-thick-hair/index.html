Bob Hairstyles For Round Faces And Thick Hair

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you tired of your round face and thick hair making it difficult to find the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the world of bob hairstyles that are specifically tailored for round faces and thick hair. With these stylish options, you can enhance your features and rock a trendy look that suits your unique style.

1. The Classic Bob

The classic bob is a timeless hairstyle that suits almost everyone. Its length typically falls just below the chin, framing the face beautifully. For those with round faces and thick hair, this cut can help create the illusion of a slimmer face by adding angles and definition. It is a versatile option that can be worn straight or with soft waves for added texture.

2. The Angled Bob

If you're looking for a more edgy and modern look, the angled bob is a great choice. This style features shorter layers in the back and longer layers in the front, creating an angled shape. The asymmetry of this cut helps to elongate the face and draw attention away from its roundness. It is perfect for those with thick hair as it adds movement and reduces bulk.

3. The Long Bob

The long bob, also known as the "lob," is a trendy and flattering option for round faces and thick hair. This style falls just above the shoulders, allowing for versatility in styling. Whether you choose to keep it straight, add curls, or opt for beachy waves, the long bob can help create a balanced and elongated look for your round face.

4. The Layered Bob

For those with thick hair, the layered bob is an excellent choice. This cut allows for weight to be removed from the hair, making it more manageable and reducing the appearance of thickness. The layers also add movement and dimension to the style, complementing the features of a round face. Whether you prefer a choppy or textured look, the layered bob can be customized to suit your preferences.

5. The Pixie Bob

If you're feeling adventurous and want to go for a shorter style, the pixie bob is worth considering. This cut combines the elements of a pixie cut and a bob, resulting in a chic and bold look. The shorter length helps to elongate the face, while the layers add texture and volume. It is a low-maintenance option that can be styled effortlessly.

6. The Shaggy Bob

The shaggy bob is a trendy and effortless style that works well for round faces and thick hair. This cut features layers throughout the hair, creating a tousled and lived-in look. The textured ends help to add movement and reduce the heaviness of thick hair. It is a versatile option that can be styled straight, wavy, or with loose curls.

7. The Blunt Bob

If you prefer a sleek and polished look, the blunt bob is a perfect choice. This cut features a straight and even length, creating a sharp and defined appearance. The blunt ends help to add structure to the round face, while the thickness of the hair adds volume and body. It is a statement-making style that exudes confidence.

8. The Inverted Bob

The inverted bob, also known as the "graduated bob," is a stylish option that adds drama and flair to your look. This cut features shorter layers at the back and longer layers at the front, creating an inverted shape. The shorter layers help to add height and lift to the crown, while the longer layers frame the face beautifully. It is a bold choice that suits those with thick hair.

9. The Curly Bob

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, the curly bob can be a game-changer for your round face. This style embraces your natural texture and adds bounce and movement to your hair. The length of the bob can be customized to your preference, allowing you to showcase your curls in the best possible way. It is a fun and playful option that adds a touch of personality to your look.

10. The Side-Swept Bob

The side-swept bob is a flattering option that helps to create asymmetry and balance for round faces. This style features a deep side part and longer layers swept to one side. The diagonal line created by the side-swept hair helps to elongate the face and draw attention away from its roundness. It is a glamorous choice that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions.

Conclusion

When it comes to bob hairstyles for round faces and thick hair, the options are endless. Whether you prefer a classic, edgy, or trendy look, there is a bob hairstyle that will suit your unique features and style. Experiment with different lengths, layers, and textures to find the perfect bob that enhances your natural beauty. Book an appointment with your hairstylist today and get ready to rock a fabulous new look!
