Question and Answer

Q: Will cutting my hair short make it grow faster?

A: No, cutting your hair has no effect on its growth rate. Hair growth happens at the root, not at the ends.

Q: Can I still wear extensions with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear extensions with short hair. There are clip-in extensions specifically designed for short hair that can add length and volume.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: The frequency of washing your short hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go a few days without washing.
