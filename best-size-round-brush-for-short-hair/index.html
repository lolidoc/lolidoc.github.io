The Hidden Secret of Best Size Round Brush for Short Hair

The hidden secret of using the best size round brush for short hair lies in the technique. It's not just about the size of the brush, but how you use it. To get the best results, start by sectioning your hair into smaller sections. Take a small section, wrap it around the brush, and gently pull the brush through the hair, directing the heat from your blow dryer towards the brush. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

This technique helps to create volume and body while also smoothing out any frizz or flyaways. By using the right size round brush and the correct technique, you can achieve salon-worthy results at home.

Tips for Using the Best Size Round Brush for Short Hair

Here are some tips for using the best size round brush for short hair:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair to ensure smooth and manageable strands.

2. Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

3. Section your hair into smaller sections for easier styling.

4. Use the round brush to lift the roots and create volume.

5. Gently pull the brush through the hair, directing the heat from your blow dryer towards the brush.
