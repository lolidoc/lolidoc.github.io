Haircuts for Wavy Hair 2021

When it comes to haircuts for wavy hair in 2021, there are several options to choose from. One popular choice is the long bob, also known as the "lob." This haircut is perfect for wavy hair as it gives a modern and chic look while allowing the waves to shine.

Another trendy haircut for wavy hair in 2021 is the shag haircut. This layered haircut adds texture and movement to the hair, making it perfect for those with wavy locks. It works well with both short and long hair lengths, allowing you to customize the look to your preference.
