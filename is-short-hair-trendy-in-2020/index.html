Short hair is definitely trendy in 2020, and there are many reasons why it's a great option. First and foremost, short hair is incredibly versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you want a sleek and polished look or a messy and tousled style, short hair can be easily manipulated to achieve the desired effect.

Personal Experience with Short Hair

As someone who has recently made the switch to a shorter hairstyle, I can confidently say that it has been a game-changer. Not only do I love the way my new cut frames my face and enhances my features, but I also love how much easier it is to style and maintain.

One of the things I love most about short hair is the versatility it offers. I can easily switch up my look by adding some texture with a curling iron or creating a sleek and polished style with a flat iron. Short hair also allows me to experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and clips, to add some extra flair to my look.

This has given me more time to focus on other aspects of my morning routine and has made getting ready for the day much more efficient.

The trend of short hair is not a new phenomenon. In fact, short hair has been popular throughout history and has been worn by many iconic figures. From the flapper hairstyles of the 1920s to the pixie cuts of the 1960s, short hair has always been associated with independence, confidence, and a sense of style.

There is a myth that only certain face shapes can pull off short hair, but this is simply not true. Short hair can be flattering on anyone, regardless of their face shape or hair texture. It's all about finding the right cut and style that suits you and enhances your natural features.
