How To Make Curly Hair Puffy

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having curly hair is a blessing, but sometimes you might want to add some extra volume and make it look puffy. Whether you have naturally curly hair or have recently curled it, there are several techniques you can try to achieve that puffy look. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks to help you make your curly hair look voluminous and puffy.

1. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

One of the best ways to make your curly hair appear puffy is by using a wide-toothed comb. This type of comb will help you detangle your hair without causing frizz. Start combing your hair from the ends and gradually work your way up to the roots. This will help distribute the natural oils from your scalp and create more volume.

2. Apply Mousse or Foam

Another effective way to make your curly hair puffy is by applying mousse or foam. These products are designed to add volume and texture to your hair. Take a small amount of mousse or foam and apply it to your damp hair. Scrunch your hair gently using your fingers to create the desired puffy effect.

3. Blow Dry with a Diffuser

If you want to make your curly hair look puffy, try blow drying it with a diffuser attachment. A diffuser helps distribute the heat evenly and reduces frizz. Flip your hair upside down and blow dry it on a low heat setting. Scrunch your hair with your fingers while blow drying to add more volume.

4. Use a Curling Wand

If your hair is naturally straight or has loose curls, you can use a curling wand to create more defined curls and make your hair look puffy. Wrap small sections of your hair around the wand and hold it for a few seconds. Release the curl and let it cool down before scrunching it with your fingers.

5. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is another great way to add volume and make it look puffy. Take small sections of your hair near the roots and backcomb them using a fine-toothed comb. Gently brush the teased hair to smooth it out and create a voluminous look.

6. Use Hair Accessories

Incorporating hair accessories can instantly make your curly hair look puffy. Try using hair clips, headbands, or scrunchies to create height and volume. Place the accessories strategically to lift your hair from the roots and give it a puffy appearance.

7. Sleep with a Pineapple Updo

If you want to wake up with puffy hair, try sleeping with a pineapple updo. Gather all your hair on top of your head and secure it with a soft scrunchie. This will prevent your curls from getting flattened while you sleep and create a puffy look in the morning.

8. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products like oils or serums on your curly hair as they can weigh it down and make it look flat. Opt for lightweight products that add volume and texture without making your hair greasy or heavy.

9. Embrace the Frizz

Curly hair often comes with frizz, and that's okay! Embrace the frizz and use it to your advantage to create a puffy look. Gently scrunch your hair with your fingers to enhance the frizz and create a voluminous effect.

10. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to maintain healthy and bouncy curls. Split ends can make your hair look flat and lifeless. Visit your hairdresser regularly to get rid of the damaged ends and keep your curls looking puffy and fresh.

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks, you can easily make your curly hair look puffy and voluminous. Experiment with different techniques and find what works best for your hair type. Remember to embrace your natural curls and have fun styling them in various ways to achieve that desired puffy look.
