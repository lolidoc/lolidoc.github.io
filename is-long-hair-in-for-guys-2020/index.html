So, is long hair in for guys in 2020? The answer is yes! Long hair has been trending for men in recent years, and it continues to be a popular choice in 2020. Listicle: Long Hair Styles for Guys in 2020

2. Long Layers - Add movement and texture to your long hair with layers that frame the face.
