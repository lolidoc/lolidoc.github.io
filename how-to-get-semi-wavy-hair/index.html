How To Get Semi Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having semi wavy hair can give you a fresh and effortless look that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you have naturally straight hair or curly locks, achieving semi wavy hair is easier than you might think. In this guide, we will walk you through the step-by-step process of getting semi wavy hair in 2023.

Preparation

Before you start styling your hair, it is important to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Follow this with a nourishing conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Once you have rinsed out the conditioner, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 1: Apply Heat Protectant

Heat protectant is essential when using hot tools on your hair. Apply a generous amount of heat protectant spray or cream to shield your hair from damage caused by heat styling. Make sure to distribute the product evenly from roots to ends.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections to make styling easier. Start by parting your hair horizontally, separating the top section from the bottom. Clip the top section away and work with the bottom section first. Secure the top section with a hair tie or clip.

Step 3: Use a Curling Wand

Take a small section of hair from the bottom section and wrap it around a curling wand. Hold the wand vertically and wrap the hair away from your face. Leave the ends of your hair out for a more natural look. Hold the hair around the wand for about 10-15 seconds and then release. Repeat this process for all the sections in the bottom section of your hair.

Step 4: Repeat on the Top Section

Once you have finished curling the bottom section, release the top section and repeat the curling process. Make sure to curl in the same direction as before for a consistent look. Take smaller sections if you want tighter waves or larger sections for looser waves.

Step 5: Finger Comb Your Hair

After curling your entire head, let the curls cool down for a few minutes. Then, gently run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create semi wavy texture. Avoid using a brush or comb as it can make the waves look too defined and unnatural.

Step 6: Apply Texturizing Spray

To enhance the semi wavy texture, apply a texturizing spray to your hair. This will add volume and hold to your waves, giving them a more lived-in look. Spray the product evenly throughout your hair and scrunch the waves gently to distribute the product.

Step 7: Set Your Waves

To make your semi wavy hair last longer, set it with a flexible hold hairspray. Hold the hairspray about 12 inches away from your head and mist it lightly over your hair. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your waves.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to get semi wavy hair, you can rock this trendy hairstyle whenever you want. Remember to use heat protectant, section your hair, and curl it with a wand for the best results. Finish off with finger combing, texturizing spray, and a light hold hairspray to perfect your semi wavy look. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve beautiful semi wavy hair effortlessly.
