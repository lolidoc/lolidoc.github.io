What are the Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair and Round Face?

The best haircuts for thick wavy hair and a round face are those that add volume, create angles, and enhance your natural waves. Here are some popular options to consider:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those who prefer to keep their hair long. This haircut helps to reduce bulkiness and adds movement to the hair. The layers also create angles and lengthen the face, making it appear more oval-shaped.

2. Bob with Side Swept Bangs

A bob with side swept bangs is a classic haircut that suits many face shapes, including round faces. The side swept bangs help to create angles and draw attention away from the width of the face. The bob length can be tailored to your preference, whether you prefer a chin-length bob or a longer bob that grazes the shoulders.

3. Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is a bold and stylish haircut that can beautifully complement a round face. This haircut helps to create angles and elongate the face, making it appear slimmer. The short length also enhances the natural waves and reduces frizz.

4. Shaggy Layers

A shaggy layered haircut is a trendy and effortless style that works well with thick wavy hair. This haircut adds texture and movement to the hair, while the layered ends create angles and lengthen the face. The shaggy layers also help to reduce bulkiness and enhance the natural waves.

Recommendation of Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair and Round Face

When it comes to choosing the best haircut for your thick wavy hair and round face, it is always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They have the knowledge and expertise to assess your hair type and face shape and recommend a haircut that will flatter your features.

Additionally, consider your personal style, lifestyle, and maintenance preferences when choosing a haircut. If you prefer low-maintenance styles, opt for shorter cuts that require minimal styling. If you enjoy experimenting with different looks, longer haircuts may be more suitable for you.

Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair and Round Face: Tips and Tricks

1. Embrace your natural texture: Instead of fighting against your waves, embrace them and enhance them through the right haircut and styling products.

2. Add layers: Layers are your best friend when it comes to managing thick wavy hair. They help to reduce bulkiness and add movement to the hair.

3. Consider face-framing layers: Face-framing layers can help to draw attention to your facial features and create angles.

4. Avoid heavy bangs: Heavy bangs can make a round face appear even rounder. Opt for side swept bangs or wispy bangs instead.

Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair and Round Face: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I have long hair if I have a round face and thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can have long hair if you have a round face and thick wavy hair. However, consider adding layers to reduce bulkiness and create angles.

Q: Will a short pixie cut suit my round face?

A: Yes, a short pixie cut can beautifully complement a round face. The short length helps to create angles and elongate the face.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my thick wavy hair?

A: To reduce frizz in your thick wavy hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, apply a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum, and avoid excessive heat styling.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have a round face and thick wavy hair?

A: It is best to avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt cuts that end at the jawline or heavy bangs that cut across the forehead.
