Question and Answer: Haircuts for Wavy Hair 2020

Q: What are the best haircuts for wavy hair?

A: The best haircuts for wavy hair are those that enhance your natural waves and create a stylish and manageable look. This can include long layered haircuts, short bobs, or medium-length shags.

Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair?

A: While it is possible to straighten wavy hair, it is not recommended as it can damage the hair and alter its natural texture. It is best to embrace your natural waves and choose a haircut that enhances them.

Q: How often should I get a haircut for wavy hair?

A: The frequency of haircuts for wavy hair will depend on your hair type and desired look. On average, it is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
