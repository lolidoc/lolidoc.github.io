I typically start by sectioning off the top half of my hair and securing it with a clip or hair tie. I then let the remaining hair fall freely or add some loose waves for added texture. This style is effortless yet chic, and it's perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

When it comes to accessories, I love using hair clips and headbands to elevate my updo.
