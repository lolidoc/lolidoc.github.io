Best 2020 Hairstyles for Over 60: Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for achieving the best 2020 hairstyles for women over 60:

2. Embrace your natural texture: Instead of fighting against your hair's natural texture, embrace it. If you have curly hair, consider a short pixie cut that enhances your natural curls. If you have straight hair, try a sleek layered bob.

3. Add volume: Thinning hair is a common concern for women over 60. To add volume to your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots, and blow dry with a round brush for added lift.
