What is the best haircut for thick curly hair?

When it comes to finding the best haircut for thick curly hair, it's important to consider both the shape of your face and the texture of your hair. One popular option for those with thick curly hair is the layered haircut. Layers help to remove excess bulk and weight, allowing your curls to bounce and move freely. Another great option is the long bob, also known as the "lob." This haircut is versatile and can be styled in various ways to suit your individual preferences. Additionally, the pixie cut is a bold and trendy choice for those who want to embrace their curls and show off their natural texture.
