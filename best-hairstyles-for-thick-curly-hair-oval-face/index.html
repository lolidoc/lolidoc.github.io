Best Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair Oval Face

Written by Exel Jan 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having thick curly hair and an oval face shape can be a blessing. Your hair is naturally voluminous and your face shape is versatile, allowing you to experiment with various hairstyles. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles that complement thick curly hair and an oval face shape, helping you enhance your natural beauty.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those with thick curly hair and an oval face shape. This hairstyle adds movement and dimension to your hair, while framing your face elegantly. The layers also help to reduce bulk and enhance the natural texture of your curls.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob haircut with side-swept bangs is another fantastic choice for oval faces with thick curly hair. The bob adds structure to your hair, while the side-swept bangs draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. This combination creates a flattering and modern look.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

If you want a more casual and effortless hairstyle, consider the half-up half-down look. This style allows you to showcase your curls while keeping your hair away from your face. Simply gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a stylish clip or hair tie.

4. Pixie Cut

For those who are daring and want a low-maintenance option, a pixie cut can be a great choice. This short and chic hairstyle highlights your facial features and adds a touch of edginess to your overall look. It's important to find a skilled stylist who can tailor the cut to your specific curly hair texture.

5. Updo with Loose Curls

For special occasions or when you want a more polished appearance, an updo with loose curls is an excellent option. This hairstyle allows you to showcase your curls while keeping your hair off your face. Consider adding a few face-framing tendrils for a softer look.

6. Side Part with Waves

A side part with waves is a classic and timeless hairstyle that suits oval faces with thick curly hair. This style adds sophistication and elegance to your overall look. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves, and then gently sweep your hair to one side.

7. High Ponytail

For a sleek and polished look, a high ponytail is a simple yet stylish choice. This hairstyle not only keeps your hair off your face but also elongates your neck. Use a good quality hair tie to secure your ponytail and smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

8. Braided Crown

Add a touch of romance and femininity to your look with a braided crown hairstyle. This style works particularly well for special occasions or weddings. Braid two sections of your hair, one on each side, and secure them at the back of your head to create a crown-like effect.

9. Messy Bun

For a laid-back and effortless look, opt for a messy bun. This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still look stylish. Secure your hair into a loose bun at the nape of your neck, allowing a few tendrils to escape for a relaxed vibe.

10. Curly Fringe

If you're looking to change up your look, consider adding a curly fringe to your hairstyle. This can be a bold and eye-catching choice that adds a unique touch to your overall appearance. Work with your stylist to find the perfect length and shape that complements your face shape.

Conclusion

With thick curly hair and an oval face shape, you have endless hairstyle options to choose from. Experiment with different cuts, lengths, and styles to find the ones that make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to consult with a professional stylist who can guide you in finding the best hairstyles for your specific hair type and face shape.
