Recommendations for Short Natural Hairstyles for Black Females 2021

If you're considering a short natural hairstyle for 2021, here are some recommendations to inspire your next haircut:

1. Tapered Cut

The tapered cut is a popular choice for black women with natural hair. This haircut features shorter sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. It creates a sleek and modern look that is both stylish and low-maintenance.

2. Afro Puff

The afro puff is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. Simply gather your natural hair into a high ponytail or bun, and you have an effortlessly chic look. This style works well for both casual and formal occasions.

3. Braided Bob

If you want to add some flair to your short natural hairstyle, consider a braided bob. This style combines the elegance of a bob haircut with the intricate beauty of braids. It's a versatile option that can be customized to fit your personal style.

4. Mohawk

The mohawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that is sure to make a statement. This style features shaved or closely cropped sides, with longer hair in the center. It can be worn straight, curly, or braided, depending on your preference.

Tips for Short Natural Hairstyles for Black Females 2021

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling and maintaining your short natural hairstyle:

1. Moisturize regularly

Short natural hair tends to be drier than longer hair, so it's important to moisturize regularly. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.

2. Protect your hair at night

Use a satin or silk bonnet or pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep. This will help to prevent frizz and maintain the integrity of your hairstyle.

3. Experiment with different products

Don't be afraid to try different products to find what works best for your hair. Whether it's a curl cream, gel, or mousse, finding the right product can make a big difference in the overall look and feel of your hair.

4. Embrace accessories

Add some flair to your short natural hairstyle with accessories such as headbands, scarves, or hair clips. These can add a personal touch and elevate your look.
