It can also help create the illusion of volume for thinning hair. Another popular choice is a bob haircut, which can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A layered bob can add movement and body to your hair, while a blunt bob can create a sleek and polished look.

Tips for Best Haircuts for 65-Year-Old Women

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for a 65-year-old woman:
