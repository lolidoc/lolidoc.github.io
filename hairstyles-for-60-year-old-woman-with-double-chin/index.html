What is a Double Chin?

A double chin, also known as submental fat, is an extra layer of fatty tissue that develops beneath the chin. It can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, aging, and weight gain. As we age, our skin loses elasticity and the muscles and tissues in the chin area may become weaker, leading to the appearance of a double chin. While a double chin is a common concern for many people, there are ways to minimize its appearance, including through the use of flattering hairstyles.
