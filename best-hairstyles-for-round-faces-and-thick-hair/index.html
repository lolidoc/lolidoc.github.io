Introduction

Understanding Round Faces and Thick Hair

Having a round face and thick hair can sometimes be a challenging combination when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. However, with the right knowledge and guidance, you can enhance your natural features and achieve a stunning look that suits your face shape and hair type. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for round faces and thick hair in 2023, providing you with inspiration and helpful tips to achieve a stylish and flattering look.

1. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob haircut is a timeless choice that works exceptionally well for round faces and thick hair. By adding side-swept bangs, you can create an illusion of length and slim down the face shape. This hairstyle also adds movement and texture to your thick hair, making it easier to manage and style.

2. Layered Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a short and edgy hairstyle, a layered pixie cut is an excellent option. This haircut adds volume and dimension to your thick hair while accentuating your facial features. The layers also help to create a more elongated appearance for your round face, giving you a chic and modern look.

3. Long Layered Waves

For those with long and thick hair, opt for long layered waves to complement your round face shape. This hairstyle adds softness and movement to your hair, creating a flattering and romantic look. To enhance the effect, use a curling iron to create loose waves and finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

4. Angled Bob

An angled bob is a versatile and stylish choice for round faces and thick hair. This haircut features longer front strands that gradually become shorter towards the back, creating an angled effect. The asymmetry of the style helps to elongate the face shape, making it appear more oval. This haircut also adds texture and dimension to your hair, giving it a trendy and sophisticated look.

5. Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyle

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great choice for those with thick hair and a round face. This hairstyle allows you to show off the natural volume of your hair while keeping it away from your face. To create this look, simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or decorative clip. You can leave some strands loose to frame your face for a soft and elegant finish.

6. Side-Swept Long Layers

If you prefer to keep your hair long, consider adding side-swept long layers to add movement and definition. This hairstyle helps to break up the roundness of your face while adding texture and volume to your thick hair. To style, simply blow-dry your hair with a round brush and finish with a lightweight hairspray for hold and shine.

7. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and effortless hairstyle that works well for round faces and thick hair. This hairstyle adds height to the crown of your head, creating the illusion of a longer face shape. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it around the base, and secure it with bobby pins. Leave some strands loose around your face for a relaxed and romantic vibe.

8. Braided Crown

A braided crown is a beautiful and elegant hairstyle that suits round faces and thick hair. This hairstyle adds a touch of sophistication and femininity to your overall look. To create this style, part your hair down the middle and start braiding from one side, working your way around the crown of your head. Secure the braid with bobby pins and gently pull out a few strands to create a soft and romantic finish.

9. Textured Ponytail

A textured ponytail is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that works well for thick hair and round faces. This hairstyle adds height and length to your face, creating a more elongated appearance. To achieve this look, tease the crown of your hair for added volume, gather it into a high ponytail, and secure it with a hair tie. To add texture, use a curling iron to create loose waves in the ponytail.

10. Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are a trendy and versatile choice for round faces and thick hair. These bangs are parted down the middle, framing your face and adding a touch of softness. Curtain bangs also help to create the illusion of a longer face shape, making them a perfect choice for those with round faces. This hairstyle can be paired with various hair lengths and styles, allowing you to experiment and find the perfect look for you.

Conclusion

Finding the best hairstyle for a round face and thick hair can be an exciting journey. By considering your face shape and hair type, you can choose a hairstyle that enhances your natural features and flatters your overall appearance. Whether you opt for a short bob, long waves, or a braided crown, remember to embrace your unique beauty and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles. With the right hairstyle, you can confidently rock any look in 2023.
