Tips for Using the Best Brush for Thick Hair

1. Start by gently detangling your hair with your fingers or a wide-toothed comb before using a brush.

2. Use a brush with wide bristles to prevent breakage and damage.

3. Brush your hair in sections to ensure that you are evenly distributing natural oils and removing tangles.

4. Avoid brushing wet hair, as it is more prone to breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or detangling brush.

5. Clean your brush regularly to remove any build-up of hair products or oils.

Q: Can I use a regular brush on my thick hair?

Q: Is it better to brush thick hair when it's wet or dry?
