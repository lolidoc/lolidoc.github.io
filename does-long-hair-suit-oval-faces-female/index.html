Question and Answer

Q: Can short hair suit oval faces?

A: While long hair is generally considered more flattering for oval faces, short hair can also suit this face shape. The key is to find a short hairstyle that complements your individual features and enhances your natural beauty.

Q: What other face shapes can suit long hair?

A: Long hair can suit various face shapes, but it may require some adjustments. Round faces can benefit from long hair with layers to add angles and create a more elongated look. Heart-shaped faces can also pull off long hair, but it's essential to avoid excessive volume around the crown area.

Q: Are there any hairstyles to avoid for oval faces?

A: While oval faces can carry many hairstyles, there are a few to avoid. Avoid hairstyles that add excessive volume on the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider. It's also best to avoid hairstyles that cover the face completely, as this can hide the natural symmetry.

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have an oval face with a larger forehead?

A: Absolutely! Having a larger forehead doesn't mean you can't rock long hair. In fact, long hair can help balance out a larger forehead and create a more proportionate look. Consider adding bangs or face-framing layers to draw attention away from the forehead.
