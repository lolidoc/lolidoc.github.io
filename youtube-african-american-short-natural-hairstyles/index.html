Introduction

African American short natural hairstyles have gained immense popularity in recent years, and YouTube has become a go-to platform for finding inspiration and tutorials. With the rise of the natural hair movement, many women are embracing their natural hair texture and exploring various styles that highlight their unique beauty. In this article, we will explore some of the best YouTube channels to follow for African American short natural hairstyles in 2023. Whether you're looking for tips, tutorials, or simply some hairstyle ideas, these channels have got you covered.

1. Natural Hair Queen

With over 500k subscribers, Natural Hair Queen is one of the top YouTube channels dedicated to African American natural hairstyles. The channel features a wide range of tutorials and tips specifically tailored for short natural hair. From wash-and-go styles to protective hairstyles, you'll find plenty of inspiration and guidance on this channel.

2. Naptural85

Naptural85 is a popular YouTube channel run by Whitney White, who is known for her beautiful natural hair journey. Although she has longer hair now, her channel has a vast collection of videos dedicated to short natural hairstyles. Whitney's tutorials are easy to follow, and she often uses affordable products that are easily accessible.

3. Tastepink

Tastepink is a rising YouTube star in the natural hair community. Her channel primarily focuses on short and medium-length natural hairstyles for African American women. From bold afros to trendy pixie cuts, her channel offers a variety of creative and stylish options for those with shorter hair.

4. GlamTwinz334

GlamTwinz334 is a dynamic duo consisting of identical twin sisters, Kelsey and Kendra. Their channel features a mix of beauty, fashion, and hair content, with a particular emphasis on natural hairstyles. With their bubbly personalities and informative tutorials, the GlamTwinz334 channel is a must-follow for anyone looking for short natural hairstyle inspiration.

5. Mini Marley

Mini Marley is another influential YouTube channel that showcases a wide range of natural hairstyles. Although her hair is currently longer, she has a playlist dedicated to shorter hair where you can find tutorials for various styles. Mini Marley's channel is known for its high-quality production and diverse hairstyle ideas.

Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair

Styling short natural hair can be a fun and exciting journey. Here are some tips to help you embrace and enhance your natural curls:

1. Moisturize Regularly

Short natural hair tends to be more prone to dryness. Make sure to moisturize your hair regularly using a leave-in conditioner or moisturizing hair cream to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

2. Experiment with Accessories

Add some flair to your short natural hairstyles by incorporating accessories like headbands, scarves, or hair jewelry. These can instantly elevate your look and add a stylish touch.

3. Embrace Protective Styles

Protective styles like braids, twists, and bantu knots can help protect your hair from damage and promote healthy growth. Don't be afraid to explore different protective styles to find what works best for you.

4. Follow a Proper Haircare Routine

Establishing a consistent haircare routine is essential for maintaining healthy natural hair. This includes regular washing, deep conditioning, and gentle detangling. Be sure to use products that are specifically formulated for natural hair.

5. Find Inspiration

YouTube is a treasure trove of inspiration for natural hairstyles. Explore different channels and playlists to discover new styles and techniques that you can incorporate into your hair routine.

Conclusion

YouTube is a fantastic resource for African American women with short natural hair. The channels mentioned in this article offer a wealth of information, inspiration, and tutorials for styling and caring for your natural hair. By following these channels and incorporating the tips provided, you'll be able to rock your short natural hair with confidence and style in 2023.
