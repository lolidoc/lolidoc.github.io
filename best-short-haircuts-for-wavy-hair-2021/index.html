Having wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Finding the right haircut for wavy hair is essential to enhance your natural waves and create a flattering look.

So, what are the best short haircuts for wavy hair in 2021? Let's dive in and find out!

The target of the best short haircuts for wavy hair in 2021 is to enhance the natural texture of wavy hair while providing a stylish and easy-to-manage look. These haircuts are designed to work with the natural waves and add movement and volume to the hair.

Personal Experience with Best Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair 2021

What are the Best Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair 2021?

The best short haircuts for wavy hair in 2021 include the bob with layers, the pixie cut, the shag haircut, and the lob. Let's take a closer look at each of these hairstyles:

1. Bob with Layers

The bob with layers is a timeless and classic haircut that works well with wavy hair. The layers add movement and texture to the hair, while the length of the bob keeps it easy to manage. This haircut is perfect for those who want a stylish and trendy look.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and edgy haircut that looks great on wavy hair. It is a short haircut that requires minimal styling and maintenance. The pixie cut is perfect for those who want to embrace their natural waves and make a statement with their hairstyle.

3. Shag Haircut

The shag haircut is a layered haircut that works well with wavy hair. It adds volume and texture to the hair, making it look effortlessly chic. The shag haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

4. Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a versatile haircut that works well with wavy hair. It is a slightly longer version of the bob and falls just above the shoulders. The lob is perfect for those who want a feminine and elegant look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I get a short haircut if I have wavy hair?
A: Yes, you can definitely get a short haircut if you have wavy hair. In fact, many short haircuts are specifically designed to enhance the natural waves of the hair.

Q: How do I style my short wavy hair?
A: Styling short wavy hair is relatively easy. You can use a curl-enhancing product to define your waves and add texture to your hair. You can also use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance the natural waves.

Q: Will a short haircut make my wavy hair frizzy?
A: A good haircut should not make your wavy hair frizzy. However, it is important to use the right products and styling techniques to keep your waves defined and minimize frizz. Using a hydrating shampoo and conditioner and applying a leave-in conditioner can help keep your waves smooth and frizz-free.

Q: How often do I need to trim my short wavy hair?
A: It is recommended to trim your short wavy hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your haircut. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends and breakage.
