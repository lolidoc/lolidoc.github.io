Tips for Best Shampoo for Over 50 Colored Hair

When using the best shampoo for over 50 colored hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind for optimal results:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo:

Sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause color fade. Opt for a sulfate-free shampoo that is gentle on the hair and helps to preserve the color.

2. Follow up with a conditioner:

After shampooing, use a conditioner specifically formulated for colored hair to further hydrate and nourish the hair. This will help to seal in moisture and protect the color.

3. Limit heat styling:

Excessive heat can damage colored hair and cause the color to fade more quickly. Minimize the use of heat styling tools and always use a heat protectant spray before styling.

4. Protect from the sun:

UV rays can also cause color fade. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.
