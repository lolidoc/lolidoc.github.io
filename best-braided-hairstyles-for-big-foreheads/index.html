Recommendation of Braided Hairstyles for Big Foreheads

If you have a big forehead and are looking for a braided hairstyle that will flatter your face shape, here are a few recommendations:

  1. Side Braid: This style involves braiding your hair to one side of your head, creating a diagonal line that draws attention away from your forehead.
  2. Crown Braid: This style involves braiding your hair in a circular motion around the crown of your head, creating a beautiful halo effect.

  4. Dutch Braid: This style involves weaving three sections of hair together in a reverse braid, creating a unique and eye-catching look.

Braided Hairstyles for Big Foreheads: Tips and Tricks

When it comes to styling braided hairstyles for big foreheads, there are a few tips and tricks that can help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse before braiding your hair to add texture and volume.
  • Consider adding accessories, such as flowers or jeweled pins, to enhance the look of your braided hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different braiding techniques, such as French braids or fishtail braids, to create a unique and eye-catching look.
  • If you have shorter hair, consider using hair extensions or clip-in braids to create the illusion of a longer and fuller hairstyle.

These tips and tricks can help you achieve the best results when styling braided hairstyles for big foreheads. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
