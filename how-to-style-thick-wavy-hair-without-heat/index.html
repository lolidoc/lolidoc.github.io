How to Style Thick Wavy Hair Without Heat: Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, moisturized hair. This will help your waves hold better and prevent frizz.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to your damp hair, focusing on the mid-lengths to ends.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to evenly distribute the product and detangle your hair.

4. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section away from your face. Secure each twist with a bobby pin or hair clip.

5. Leave the twists in your hair for a few hours or overnight to allow your waves to set.

6. Once your hair is completely dry, remove the twists and gently separate the waves with your fingers.

7. Set your style with a lightweight hairspray or a sea salt spray for added hold.
