How To Style Half Wavy Half Curly Hair

Written by Alexa Mar 24, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having hair that is half wavy and half curly can be both a blessing and a challenge. This unique hair texture allows for versatile styling options. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and techniques to help you style your half wavy half curly hair and achieve a stunning look.

1. Know Your Hair Type

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to understand your hair type. Is it more wavy or curly? Knowing this will help you choose the right products and techniques for your hair.

2. Start with a Good Haircut

A good haircut is essential for styling half wavy half curly hair. Consult with a professional stylist who specializes in curly hair to find the best cut that complements your unique texture.

3. Moisturize and Hydrate

Curly and wavy hair tends to be drier than straight hair. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent frizz. Additionally, incorporating a weekly deep conditioning treatment can help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

4. Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

To enhance your natural curls and waves, apply a curl enhancing product such as a mousse or a curl cream. This will define your curls and add more texture to your hair.

5. Diffuse or Air Dry

Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair as it can cause frizz. Instead, gently squeeze out the excess water using a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. If you are in a hurry, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to speed up the drying process while preserving your curls.

6. Embrace Heatless Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair over time. Embrace heatless styling methods to achieve different looks. For loose waves, braid your hair before going to bed and unravel them in the morning. For tighter curls, twist small sections of your hair and secure them with bobby pins overnight.

7. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb or Your Fingers

Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can disrupt your natural curl pattern and cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet and coated with conditioner.

8. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your half wavy half curly hair. Try a half-up half-down style, a messy bun, or a high ponytail to switch up your look.

9. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep. You can also protect your curls by wrapping your hair in a silk scarf or using a satin bonnet.

10. Regular Trims

Keep your half wavy half curly hair healthy and prevent split ends by getting regular trims. Aim for a trim every 8-12 weeks to maintain the shape and integrity of your hairstyle.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently style your half wavy half curly hair and embrace its natural beauty. Remember, experimentation is key, so don't be afraid to try new products and hairstyles to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
