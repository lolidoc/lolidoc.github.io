Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle this year? Short hair is making a major comeback in 2021, with various trendy styles that are perfect for any hair type or face shape. Many people struggle with finding the right hairstyle that suits their needs and preferences. Some may find it difficult to manage long hair or feel like they are stuck in a hairstyle rut. Short hair can be a great solution to these pain points, offering a low-maintenance and stylish option that can transform your look.

Trending short hair for 2021 is all about embracing your natural texture and adding some fun and edgy elements to your hairstyle. From pixie cuts to shaggy bobs, there are so many options to choose from that can suit your personal style and enhance your features.

Trending Short Hair for 2021: Embrace Your Natural Texture

One of the biggest trends in short hair for 2021 is embracing your natural texture. Instead of fighting against your natural hair type, let it shine and work with it to create a unique and stylish look. Whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair, there are short hairstyles that can enhance your natural texture and make a statement.

For those with curly hair, a short pixie cut with layers can help define your curls and add volume. If you have straight hair, a blunt bob or a textured lob can create a sleek and chic look. Wavy hair can be styled into a tousled and beachy bob for a laid-back and effortless vibe.

Trending Short Hair for 2021: The History and Myth

Short hair has a long history and has been a symbol of strength and rebellion throughout the years. From the flapper girls of the 1920s to the punk rockers of the 1970s, short hair has always been associated with breaking the norms and making a statement.

There is a common myth that short hair is not feminine or attractive, but this couldn't be further from the truth. Short hair can be incredibly feminine and can enhance your features in a way that long hair may not. It's all about finding the right style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Trending Short Hair for 2021: The Hidden Secret

The hidden secret to rocking short hair in 2021 is confidence. Short hair can be a bold and daring choice, but it can also be incredibly empowering. When you feel confident in your hairstyle, it can radiate through your entire presence and make you feel like a million bucks.

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel the most confident and beautiful. Short hair can be a blank canvas for self-expression and creativity, so embrace the opportunity to try something new and exciting.

Trending Short Hair for 2021: Recommendations

If you're considering trying out a short hairstyle, here are some recommendations to inspire you:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and classic short hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's perfect for those who want to make a bold statement and showcase their facial features.

2. Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is a versatile and chic option that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. Whether you prefer a blunt bob or a layered bob, adding bangs can instantly update your look.

3. Shaggy Bob

The shaggy bob is a trendy and textured hairstyle that adds movement and volume to your hair. It's a great option for those with thin or fine hair, as it can create the illusion of thickness and fullness.

4. Undercut

The undercut is a daring and edgy hairstyle that involves shaving or cutting the hair shorter on one side or at the nape of the neck. It's a great option for those who want to make a statement and show off their personality.

Trending Short Hair for 2021: Tips

If you're ready to take the plunge and try out a short hairstyle, here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consult with a Professional

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can assess your hair type, face shape, and personal style to recommend the best short hairstyle for you.

2. Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a short hairstyle, consider your lifestyle and how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair. Some short hairstyles may require more maintenance and styling than others, so choose one that fits into your daily routine.

3. Experiment with Styling Products

Styling products can be your best friend when it comes to maintaining and styling your short hair. Invest in a good quality texturizing spray, pomade, or hairspray to add volume, texture, and hold to your hairstyle.

4. Embrace the Change

Switching to a short hairstyle can be a big change, but it can also be incredibly liberating and empowering. Embrace the change and have fun experimenting with different styles and looks.

Trending Short Hair for 2021: Fun Facts

Did you know that short hair is not only stylish but also has some fun facts associated with it? Here are a few interesting tidbits about short hair:

- The shortest recorded haircut belonged to a woman named Maureen O'Hara, who had her hair cut to only 1/8 inch for a role in the movie "The Redhead from Wyoming."

- Short hair can make you appear taller. Without the weight and length of long hair dragging you down, your posture can improve and give the illusion of height.

- Short hair can save you time and money on hair care products and styling tools. With less hair to manage, you'll spend less time washing, drying, and styling your hair.

Trending Short Hair for 2021: How to Style

Styling short hair can be quick and easy once you find the right techniques and products. Here are some tips on how to style your short hair:

- Use a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to your hair.

- Use a flat iron or curling wand to create waves or curls for added texture and dimension.

- Experiment with different partings and hair accessories to change up your look and add a personal touch.

Trending Short Hair for 2021: What If
