A Listicle of Hairstyles Like an Old Lady

To wrap up our guide on how to style your hair like an old lady, here is a listicle of hairstyles that you can try. These hairstyles are perfect for achieving that timeless old lady look and can be customized to suit your personal style and preferences.

1. Classic Chignon

The classic chignon is a timeless hairstyle that is perfect for special occasions or everyday wear. To achieve this look, start by gathering your hair into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray for extra hold.

2. Soft Waves

If you prefer a more relaxed and casual look, soft waves are the way to go. To achieve this hairstyle, start by curling small sections of your hair with a curling iron. Once all of your hair is curled, gently brush through the curls to create soft waves. Finish off with a bit of texturizing spray for added texture and hold.

3. Vintage-Inspired Updo

If you're looking to truly embrace the old lady aesthetic, a vintage-inspired updo is the way to go. This hairstyle is characterized by its intricate twists and curls, which create a stunning and elegant look. To achieve this hairstyle, you may need to enlist the help of a hairstylist or watch online tutorials for step-by-step instructions.

4. Half-Up, Half-Down

If you're looking for a hairstyle that is both elegant and effortless, a half-up, half-down style is the perfect choice. To achieve this look, start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and securing it with a clip or hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down and add soft curls or waves for added texture. Finish off with a bit of hairspray to keep everything in place.
