Short Hairstyles for Girls/Kids 2021: Trendy and Stylish Options

When it comes to short hairstyles for girls/kids in 2021, there are plenty of options to choose from. From classic bobs to edgy pixie cuts, there is a hairstyle that will suit every girl's unique style. Here are some trendy short hairstyles for girls/kids in 2021:

1. Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that works well for girls and kids of all ages. This short hairstyle can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. Whether your little one prefers a sleek and straight bob or a curly and textured bob, this hairstyle is sure to make a statement.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring short hairstyle that is perfect for girls and kids who want to stand out. This hairstyle is characterized by its short length and cropped layers. It can be styled in various ways, including sleek and smooth or tousled and textured. The pixie cut is a low-maintenance option that requires minimal styling and upkeep.

3. Braided Hairstyles

Braided hairstyles are a popular choice for girls and kids of all ages. They are not only stylish but also practical, as they keep the hair off the face and prevent tangles. There are many different braided hairstyles to choose from, including French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. These hairstyles can be done on short hair and are perfect for special occasions or everyday wear.

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when choosing and maintaining short hairstyles for girls/kids in 2021:

- Use the right products and tools to style and maintain short hair, such as lightweight styling creams, texturizing sprays, and mini hairbrushes.

Short Hairstyles for Girls/Kids 2021: FAQs

Q: Can girls with curly hair have short hairstyles?

A: Absolutely! Short hairstyles can work well for girls with curly hair. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair to find the right cut and styling techniques for your little one.

Q: How often should I trim my child's short hair?

A: The frequency of trims will depend on your child's hair growth and the style of their short haircut. On average, a trim every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain the shape and health of the hair.

Q: Are short hairstyles suitable for formal events?

A: Yes, short hairstyles can be styled in a way that is suitable for formal events. Consider adding accessories, such as hair clips or headbands, to elevate the look.

Q: Can my child wear short hairstyles to school?

A: Many schools have dress code policies that dictate the length of a child's hair. Consult with your child's school to ensure that their chosen short hairstyle is within the guidelines.
