How to Maintain Long Hair Over 60

Maintaining long hair in your senior years requires some extra care and attention. Here are some tips to help you keep your long hair looking its best:

1. Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

3. Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
