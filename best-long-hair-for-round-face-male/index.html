Are you a man with a round face and long hair? Do you struggle to find the best hairstyle that suits your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best long hair styles for round-faced men and provide you with some helpful tips and recommendations.

Having a round face can sometimes make it challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. The wrong hairstyle can make your face appear even rounder, while the right one can enhance your facial structure and make you look more attractive.

So, what is the best long hair style for a round face? The answer is simple - a hairstyle that adds length and angles to your face. By creating the illusion of a more elongated face shape, you can balance out the roundness and achieve a more flattering look.

In summary, the best long hair styles for round-faced men are those that add length and angles to the face, creating a more elongated and balanced appearance. Now, let's dive deeper into some specific hairstyles that you can consider.

1. The Side Swept Bangs

The side-swept bangs are a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can work well for men with round faces. This style involves growing out your hair and sweeping it to one side, creating an asymmetrical look. The diagonal line of the bangs helps to create angles and adds length to your face.

If you have thick hair, you can try adding layers to reduce the bulkiness and create movement. This will help to further elongate your face and create a more flattering appearance.

2. The Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that can be customized to suit various face shapes, including round faces. This style involves shaving the sides and back of your head short while leaving the top long. By creating a contrast between the long hair on top and the shorter sides, you can create the illusion of a longer face.

For round-faced men, it is recommended to keep the top hair longer and styled upwards or to the side. This will create more height and angles, balancing out the roundness of your face.

3. The Long Layers

If you prefer to keep your hair all one length, you can opt for long layers. This style involves adding layers throughout your long hair, starting from the chin or below. The layers help to create movement and texture, making your face appear more elongated.

To enhance the effect, you can try styling your hair by tucking it behind your ears or adding a slight wave or curl. These techniques will add more angles and soften the roundness of your face.

4. The Textured Pompadour

The pompadour is a classic hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different face shapes. For round-faced men, the key is to add texture and volume to the top of your hair while keeping the sides short. This will create a more vertical and elongated appearance.

To achieve the textured pompadour, you can use a styling product such as pomade or wax to add volume and definition. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair upwards and slightly back, creating height and angles.

Conclusion

When it comes to finding the best long hair style for a round face, the key is to add length and angles to create a more elongated appearance. Hairstyles such as the side swept bangs, undercut, long layers, and textured pompadour can help achieve this effect. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best. Remember, confidence is key, so rock your long hair with pride!
