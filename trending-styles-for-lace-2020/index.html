Trending Styles for Lace 2020: The Bohemian Flair

One of the most popular styles for lace in 2020 is the bohemian flair. This trend features delicate lace dresses with flowing silhouettes and intricate lace patterns. The bohemian flair is perfect for those who love a casual and carefree look. These dresses can be styled with sandals and a floppy hat for a day at the beach or dressed up with heels and statement jewelry for a special occasion.

When it comes to the bohemian flair, the key is to choose lace dresses with loose and flowy silhouettes. Look for styles that are cinched at the waist or have a defined waistline to create a flattering shape. Pair your lace dress with natural and earthy accessories, such as a woven bag or leather sandals, to complete the bohemian look.

Not only are bohemian lace dresses stylish, but they are also comfortable to wear. The loose and flowy silhouette allows for easy movement and breathability, making it perfect for summer days or tropical vacations. Additionally, the delicate lace patterns add a touch of femininity and elegance to any outfit, making it a versatile piece that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions.

Trending Styles for Lace 2020: The Statement Sleeves

Another popular trend for lace in 2020 is the statement sleeves. This style features lace tops with oversized and dramatic sleeves that add a touch of femininity and elegance to any outfit. The statement sleeves can be paired with jeans for a casual and trendy look or with a skirt or trousers for a more formal occasion.

When choosing lace tops with statement sleeves, opt for styles that have a simple and clean design. The focus should be on the sleeves, so avoid tops with too many embellishments or details. Look for lace tops with bell sleeves, ruffled sleeves, or bishop sleeves for a dramatic and eye-catching look.

The statement sleeves trend is perfect for those who want to add a touch of glamour and sophistication to their outfit. The oversized and dramatic sleeves create a bold and stylish look that is sure to turn heads. Pair your lace top with simple and understated bottoms to let the sleeves take center stage.

Trending Styles for Lace 2020: The History and Myth

The use of lace in fashion dates back centuries, with its origins in 16th century Europe. Lace was initially handcrafted using intricate techniques, making it a symbol of luxury and wealth. Over time, lace became more accessible and affordable, and it was incorporated into various styles and designs.

Throughout history, lace has been associated with femininity, elegance, and romance. It has been used in wedding dresses, evening gowns, and lingerie, adding a touch of sophistication and allure to these garments. Lace has also been used in traditional costumes and cultural attire, representing the rich heritage and traditions of different regions.

In mythology, lace is often associated with fairies and ethereal beings. It is believed that lace was created by spiders or dewdrops, adding to its mystical and enchanting qualities. Lace has been used in folklore and fairy tales to symbolize purity, delicacy, and beauty.

Trending Styles for Lace 2020: Recommendations

If you're looking to update your wardrobe with lace pieces in 2020, here are some recommendations to get you started:

1. Lace Dress with Bohemian Flair

Choose a lace dress with a flowing silhouette and intricate lace patterns. Pair it with sandals and a floppy hat for a casual and bohemian look, or dress it up with heels and statement jewelry for a special occasion.

2. Lace Top with Statement Sleeves

3. Lace Skirt with Classic Elegance

4. Lace Accessories for a Touch of Femininity

Add a touch of lace to your outfit with lace accessories, such as a lace scarf, lace gloves, or lace socks. These small details can add a feminine and elegant touch to any outfit.

Trending Styles for Lace 2020: Tips

When it comes to styling lace in 2020, here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Balance Lace with Simple Pieces

When wearing lace, keep the rest of the outfit simple to let the lace take center stage. Pair lace tops or dresses with simple bottoms and accessories to create a balanced and stylish look.

2. Play with Contrasting Textures

Add interest to your outfit by pairing lace with contrasting textures, such as leather or denim. This combination creates a unique and edgy look that is both feminine and modern.

3. Experiment with Different Colors

While white and ivory are classic choices for lace, don't be afraid to experiment with different colors. Try lace in bold and vibrant hues, such as red or emerald green, for a statement-making look.

4. Choose the Right Fit

When shopping for lace pieces, make sure to choose the right fit for your body type. Look for styles that flatter your figure and highlight your best features. This will ensure that you feel confident and comfortable in your lace outfit.
