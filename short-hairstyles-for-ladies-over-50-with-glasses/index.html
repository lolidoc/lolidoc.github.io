Short Hairstyles for Ladies Over 50 with Glasses: How to

Here are some steps to follow when styling a short hairstyle for ladies over 50 with glasses:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling to ensure a clean and fresh base.

2. Apply styling products

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to add volume and hold.

3. Use a comb or brush

5. Set with hairspray
