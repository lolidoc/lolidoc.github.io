What Are Best Short Haircuts for Long Faces?

Best short haircuts for long faces are hairstyles that add width and volume to your face. These haircuts can include pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts. They are designed to balance out the length of your face and create the illusion of a more proportionate appearance.

Pixie cuts are short and cropped haircuts that can make your face appear wider. They are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance and edgy hairstyle. Bob cuts, on the other hand, are versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape. They can be chin-length or shoulder-length, depending on your preference.

Layered cuts involve adding different lengths of hair to create dimension and movement. They can be styled in various ways and are suitable for those who want more versatility in their haircut.

Question and Answer about Best Short Haircuts for Long Faces

Q: Can I have bangs with a short haircut for a long face?

A: Absolutely! Bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut for a long face. Side-swept bangs, in particular, can draw attention away from the length of your face and add width.

Q: Will a bob cut make my face appear even longer?

A: Not necessarily. A bob cut can actually balance out the length of your face and create a more proportionate appearance. It's important to consult with a professional hairstylist who can recommend the best length and style for you.

Q: Are there any short haircuts that should be avoided for long faces?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, some short haircuts may not be as flattering for long faces. These can include extremely short cuts that accentuate the length of the face. It's best to consult with a hairstylist who can guide you in choosing the best haircut for your face shape.

Q: How can I style my short haircut for a long face?

A: Styling your short haircut for a long face can be simple and effortless. You can use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair. You can also experiment with different partings and styles to find what suits you best.
