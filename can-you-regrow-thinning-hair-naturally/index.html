Understanding Hair Growth and the Natural Regrowth Process

Hair growth is a complex process that involves the hair follicles and the scalp. Each hair follicle goes through a growth cycle that consists of three phases: anagen (growth), catagen (transition), and telogen (resting). During the telogen phase, the hair falls out, making room for new hair to grow. The natural regrowth process can be influenced by various factors, including genetics, hormones, and overall health.
