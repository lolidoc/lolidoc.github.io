Question and Answer

Q: How do I determine my face shape?

A: To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or washable marker. Step back and look at the shape you've drawn to determine whether it is round, square, oval, heart, or diamond.

Q: Can I change my face shape with a haircut?

A: While a haircut can't change your actual face shape, it can create the illusion of a different shape by enhancing or balancing your features. For example, a haircut with layers and volume can make a round face shape appear more elongated.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on your individual hair type and desired style. On average, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Q: How do I find a skilled hairstylist?

A: To find a skilled hairstylist, ask for recommendations from friends or family members who have hairstyles you admire. You can also look for hairstylists online and read reviews to get an idea of their expertise and client satisfaction.
