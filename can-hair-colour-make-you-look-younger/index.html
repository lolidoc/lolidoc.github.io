What is Hair Color?

Hair color is the result of pigments called melanin that are produced in the hair follicles. As we age, the production of melanin decreases, resulting in gray or white hair. By adding color to the hair, whether through dye or highlights, we can enhance our natural beauty and create a more youthful appearance.
