Are you looking for the best curly haircuts for round faces in 2021? If so, you're in the right place! Curly hair can be difficult to manage, especially if you have a round face shape. Having a round face shape can come with its own set of challenges when it comes to finding the right haircut. Round faces tend to have softer features and a fuller appearance, so it's important to choose a haircut that will complement your face shape and enhance your natural beauty.

The best curly haircuts for round faces in 2021 are those that add height and length to the face, creating the illusion of a more elongated shape. These haircuts typically include layers, which help to add volume and movement to the hair. They also often feature side-swept bangs or a deep side part, which can help to create angles and balance out the roundness of the face.

In summary, the best curly haircuts for round faces in 2021 are those that add height and length to the face, include layers for volume and movement, and feature side-swept bangs or a deep side part for added angles. Now, let's dive deeper into each of these aspects and explore some specific haircut recommendations for round faces with curly hair.

Best Curly Haircuts for Round Faces 2021: Adding Height and Length

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a haircut for a round face is adding height and length to the face. This can help to create the illusion of a more elongated shape and balance out the roundness. There are several ways to achieve this with curly hair.

First, consider a haircut with layers. Layers can add volume and movement to the hair, making it appear fuller and longer. This can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape. Opt for long layers that start around the chin or shoulder-length layers for the best results.

Another option is to add height at the crown of the head. This can be achieved with a haircut that has more volume on top, such as a layered pixie cut or a curly bob with layers on top. This added height can help to elongate the face and balance out the roundness.

Best Curly Haircuts for Round Faces 2021: Side-Swept Bangs and Deep Side Part

Side-swept bangs and a deep side part can also be great options for round faces with curly hair. These styling techniques can help to create angles and add definition to the face.

Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a more elongated face shape by drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones. They can also help to soften the roundness of the face. Opt for longer, wispy bangs that blend seamlessly into the rest of your curls for a natural look.

A deep side part can also help to create angles and balance out the roundness of the face. This can be done with any haircut, whether it's a long, medium, or short curly style. Simply part your hair deeply to one side and let your curls fall naturally. This will create a diagonal line that adds visual interest and definition to your face shape.

Best Curly Haircuts for Round Faces 2021: Recommendations

Now that we've discussed some general guidelines for choosing the best curly haircuts for round faces in 2021, let's explore some specific haircut recommendations:

  1. A layered curly bob with side-swept bangs
  2. A long curly pixie cut with height at the crown
  3. A shoulder-length haircut with long layers and a deep side part
  4. A curly shag haircut with layers and a deep side part

These are just a few examples of the many curly haircuts that can work well for round faces. The key is to choose a style that adds height and length to the face, includes layers for volume and movement, and features side-swept bangs or a deep side part for added angles.

Best Curly Haircuts for Round Faces 2021: Final Thoughts

Choosing the best curly haircut for your round face in 2021 doesn't have to be a daunting task. By considering factors such as adding height and length, incorporating layers, and styling with side-swept bangs or a deep side part, you can find a haircut that enhances your natural beauty and makes you feel confident.

Remember, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist who can assess your face shape and hair type to recommend the best curly haircut for you. They can also provide personalized tips and tricks for styling and maintaining your curls.

So, go ahead and embrace your curly hair! With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can rock a beautiful, confident look that flatters your round face shape in 2021.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear my curly hair long if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your curly hair long if you have a round face. However, it's important to add layers to create volume and movement. Long layers that start around the chin or shoulder-length layers can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape and balance out the roundness.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face and curly hair?

A: No, you don't have to avoid bangs if you have a round face and curly hair. In fact, side-swept bangs can be a great option for round faces as they help to create angles and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Opt for longer, wispy bangs that blend seamlessly into the rest of your curls for a natural look.

Q: Can I wear a bob with curly hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob with curly hair if you have a round face. Consider a layered curly bob with side-swept bangs for a flattering and stylish look. The layers will add volume and movement, while the side-swept bangs will create angles and soften the roundness of the face.

Q: How often should I trim my curly hair if I have a round face?

A: The frequency of trims for curly hair depends on your individual hair type and goals. However, it's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your curls. Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking fresh and vibrant.

Choosing the best curly haircut for your round face in 2021 is all about creating balance and enhancing your natural beauty. By adding height and length, incorporating layers, and styling with side-swept bangs or a deep side part, you can find a haircut that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist for personalized recommendations and tips for styling and maintaining your curls. Embrace your curly hair and rock a beautiful, confident look in 2021!
