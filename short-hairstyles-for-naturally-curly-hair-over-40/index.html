Short Hairstyles For Naturally Curly Hair Over 40

Table of Contents

Introduction

As women age, their hair tends to become thinner and lose its natural shine. However, embracing your naturally curly hair can help you achieve a youthful and vibrant look. Short hairstyles are a great option for women over 40 with curly hair, as they are low-maintenance and can enhance your facial features. In this article, we will explore some trendy and stylish short hairstyles specifically designed for women with naturally curly hair who are over 40.

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless hairstyle that works well for women of all ages. For women over 40 with naturally curly hair, a pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair. This short hairstyle is easy to style and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. Opt for layers to add movement to your curls and consider adding highlights to create dimension and depth.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another versatile hairstyle that looks great on women with naturally curly hair. For women over 40, a short bob can give a fresh and youthful appearance. Consider a layered bob to add volume and bounce to your curls. This hairstyle is low-maintenance and can be easily styled with a diffuser and some curl-enhancing products.

3. Curly Shag

The curly shag is a fun and playful hairstyle that suits women with naturally curly hair. This short hairstyle features layers and adds texture to your curls. For women over 40, a curly shag can create a soft and romantic look. This hairstyle requires minimal styling and can be easily achieved with a styling mousse or curl cream.

4. Tapered Cut

A tapered cut is a trendy and modern hairstyle that works well for women with curly hair. This short hairstyle is characterized by longer curls on top and shorter curls on the sides and back. The tapered cut can help accentuate your facial features and give a youthful appearance. Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to enhance the dimension of your curls.

5. Mohawk

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, consider a curly mohawk. This short hairstyle is perfect for women over 40 who want to make a statement. The mohawk adds height and volume to your curls, giving you a confident and stylish look. You can customize the length and shape of the mohawk to suit your personal style.

6. Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to your short curly hair can instantly transform your look. Side-swept bangs can frame your face and soften your features. This hairstyle works well for women over 40 as it can help hide forehead wrinkles and add a youthful touch to your appearance. Consider adding layers to your bangs for added texture and movement.

7. Afro

If you have naturally tight curls, embracing your afro is a great option. The afro hairstyle celebrates the natural beauty of your curls and can give you a confident and powerful look. For women over 40, an afro can help you stand out and embrace your unique style. Make sure to keep your afro well-moisturized and use a wide-toothed comb to prevent breakage.

8. Short and Sassy

A short and sassy hairstyle is perfect for women over 40 who want a chic and stylish look. This short hairstyle features shorter layers and adds volume to your curls. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and can be styled with some texturizing spray for added definition.

9. Faux Hawk

A faux hawk is a trendy and modern hairstyle that can give you an edgy and youthful look. This short hairstyle is achieved by creating a strip of longer curls down the center of your head and keeping the sides shorter. For women over 40, a faux hawk can help you make a bold statement and showcase your confidence. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and add texture.

10. Curly Bob with Bangs

A curly bob with bangs is a classic and timeless hairstyle that suits women of all ages. This short hairstyle is characterized by a chin-length bob and soft, curly bangs. For women over 40, a curly bob with bangs can add a touch of elegance and sophistication. Consider using a curling iron or rollers to enhance your curls and create a polished look.

Conclusion

Short hairstyles for naturally curly hair over 40 can enhance your natural beauty and give you a youthful and vibrant look. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, bob, or an edgier style like a mohawk or faux hawk, there are plenty of options to choose from. Embrace your curls and experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your personal style and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
