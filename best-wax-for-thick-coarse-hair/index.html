Best Wax for Thick Coarse Hair: How to Use and Apply

Using the best wax for thick coarse hair is a straightforward process. Start by warming the wax in your hands until it becomes pliable. Then, apply a small amount of wax to the desired area, working it into the hair in the direction of growth. Once the wax has dried, gently remove it in the opposite direction of hair growth, taking care not to pull the hair.
