Are you tired of dealing with your thick wavy hair and round face? Do you feel like there are limited options for short hairstyles that suit your specific hair type and face shape? If so, you're not alone. Many people with thick wavy hair and round faces struggle to find the perfect haircut that flatters their features. But fear not, because in this blog post, we will explore some stylish and trendy short hairstyles that are perfect for those with thick wavy hair and round faces.

Having thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it adds volume and texture to your hair, giving it a natural bounce. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you have a round face. Round faces tend to have a wider shape, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds length and angles to your face.

When it comes to short hairstyles for thick wavy hair and round faces, the key is to find a style that adds height and volume to the crown of your head, while also creating angles around your face. This will help to elongate your face shape and draw attention away from its roundness.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for thick wavy hair and round faces can be both stylish and flattering. By choosing a haircut that adds height and angles to your face, you can create a look that is both trendy and suits your unique features. So don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Trendy Short Pixie Cut

The short pixie cut is a popular choice for those with thick wavy hair and round faces. This haircut is characterized by its short length and cropped layers, which create a textured and edgy look. The pixie cut works well with thick wavy hair, as it adds volume and movement to your locks. It also helps to elongate your face shape, making it appear slimmer and more angular. To style a pixie cut, simply apply a texturizing product to your hair and tousle it with your fingers for a messy and effortless look.

Chic Bob with Bangs

The bob haircut is a classic choice for those with thick wavy hair and round faces. This hairstyle is characterized by its chin-length length and blunt ends, which create a sleek and polished look. To add some dimension and angles to your face, consider adding bangs to your bob. Bangs can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape and draw attention away from its roundness. To style a bob with bangs, simply blow dry your hair straight and use a flat iron to create a sleek and smooth finish.

Effortless Beach Waves

If you prefer a more relaxed and casual look, consider embracing your natural waves with an effortless beach wave hairstyle. This hairstyle is characterized by its loose and tousled waves, which create a laid-back and bohemian look. To achieve beach waves, simply apply a salt spray to your damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers. You can also use a curling iron to create more defined waves. This hairstyle works well with thick wavy hair, as it adds texture and movement to your locks. It also helps to create the illusion of a longer face shape, making it ideal for those with round faces.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are plenty of stylish and trendy short hairstyles for those with thick wavy hair and round faces. From the chic pixie cut to the effortless beach waves, there are options for every style and preference. The key is to choose a haircut that adds height and angles to your face, creating the illusion of a longer and more angular face shape. So don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
