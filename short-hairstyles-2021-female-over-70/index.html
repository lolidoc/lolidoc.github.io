Short Hairstyles 2021 Female Over 70: Question and Answer

Q: Can I still have long hair at 70?

A: Absolutely! There is no age limit for long hair. However, it's important to consider your hair's health and maintenance requirements. If your hair is thinning or becoming more fragile, a shorter style may be more manageable and flattering.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Short hair can actually have a rejuvenating effect when styled correctly. It can help to frame the face and draw attention to your best features, making you look more youthful and vibrant.

Q: How often should I get my short hair trimmed?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your short hairstyle. Regular trims will also prevent split ends and breakage, keeping your hair looking its best.

Q: Can I still experiment with color on short hair?

A: Absolutely! Short hair is a great canvas for experimenting with color. Whether you want to embrace your natural gray hair or add a pop of color, there are plenty of options to choose from.
