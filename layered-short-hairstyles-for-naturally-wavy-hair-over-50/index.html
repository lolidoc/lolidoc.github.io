How to Style Layered Short Hairstyles for Naturally Wavy Hair Over 50

Styling layered short hairstyles for naturally wavy hair over 50 is relatively easy. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves to your hair.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to enhance the waves and add hold.
  5. Gently tousle your hair with your fingers to loosen the waves and create a more relaxed look.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

By following these steps, you can achieve a stylish and effortless look with your layered short hairstyle.
