Common Questions About Haircuts for Curly Hair 2020

Q: Can I get a short haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! There are many short haircut options for curly hair, such as a pixie cut or a bob. Just make sure to find a stylist who understands curly hair and can create a cut that works well with your curls.

Q: How often should I get a haircut for my curly hair?

A: The frequency of haircuts for curly hair depends on the individual and their hair type. Some people may need a trim every 6-8 weeks, while others can go longer between haircuts. It is important to listen to your hair and schedule haircuts accordingly.

Q: Are there any specific products I should use for my curly hair?

A: Yes, there are many products on the market specifically designed for curly hair. Look for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, as well as styling products that enhance curls and reduce frizz.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair after getting a haircut?
