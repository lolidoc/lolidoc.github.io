Best Short Haircut for Long Oval Face: Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is both trendy and flattering for long oval faces. This cut features short layers at the top of the head, with longer layers at the sides and back. The pixie cut adds volume to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. It also highlights your cheekbones and jawline, drawing attention to your best features.

When getting a pixie cut, you can choose to keep it sleek and polished or add texture for a more edgy look. If you have straight hair, a sleek pixie cut can give you a sophisticated and chic appearance. If you have wavy or curly hair, adding texture to your pixie cut can add volume and movement to your hair, creating a fun and playful look.
