How to Cut a Woman's Hair Step by Step

Now that you have an understanding of the basics, let's dive into the step-by-step process of cutting a woman's hair:

1. Start by washing and drying the hair thoroughly.

2. Comb through the hair to remove any tangles or knots.

3. Section the hair into manageable sections using hair clips.

4. Begin cutting from the back, working your way towards the front.

5. Use a comb as a guide to ensure even cuts and lengths.

6. Take your time and make small cuts, checking frequently for symmetry.

7. Once you've finished cutting, style the hair as desired.
