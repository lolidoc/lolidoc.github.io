What is a Receding Hairline?

A receding hairline, also known as male pattern baldness or androgenic alopecia, is a common condition that affects many men as they age. It is characterized by the gradual thinning and receding of the hairline, typically starting at the temples and progressing upwards. This condition is primarily caused by a combination of genetic and hormonal factors.

While a receding hairline can be a source of insecurity for some individuals, it is essential to remember that it is a natural part of the aging process and does not define your attractiveness.
