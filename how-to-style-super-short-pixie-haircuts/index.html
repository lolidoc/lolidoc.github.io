How to Style Super Short Pixie Haircuts

When it comes to styling super short pixie haircuts, it's all about embracing your natural texture and working with what you have. Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve different looks with your super short pixie haircut:

Tip 1: Use a Flat Iron for Sleek and Smooth Looks

If you want a sleek and polished look with your super short pixie haircut, a flat iron can be your best friend. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage. Then, use a small flat iron to straighten your hair section by section, starting from the roots to the ends. Finish off with a lightweight hair serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Tip 2: Use a Texturizing Spray for Volume and Texture

If you prefer a more textured and tousled style, a texturizing spray can be your go-to product. Start by spraying the texturizing spray onto your dry hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create volume and texture. You can also use a teasing comb to add more volume at the crown of your head. Finish off with a flexible hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tip 3: Use a Small Curling Iron for Soft Waves or Curls

If you want to add some soft waves or curls to your super short pixie haircut, a small curling iron can be a game-changer. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, use a small curling iron to curl small sections of your hair, holding the iron vertically. Alternate the direction of the curls to create a more natural look. Finish off with a lightweight hair spray to hold your curls in place.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use hair extensions with a super short pixie haircut?

A: Hair extensions are not typically recommended for super short pixie haircuts, as there may not be enough hair to blend the extensions seamlessly. However, if you want to add length or volume to your pixie haircut, you can consider clip-in hair extensions that are specifically designed for short hair. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best options for your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my super short pixie haircut?

A: It is recommended to trim your super short pixie haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style. Regular trims will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Q: Can I color my super short pixie haircut?

A: Yes, you can definitely color your super short pixie haircut. Whether you want to go for a bold and vibrant color or a subtle highlight, consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best color options for your hair and skin tone.

Q: How do I maintain my super short pixie haircut?

A: To maintain your super short pixie haircut, it is important to keep up with regular trims, use the right products for your hair type, and follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using heat protectant products before styling, and protecting your hair from the sun and environmental damage.
