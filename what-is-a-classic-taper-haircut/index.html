This creates a tapered effect that is achieved through precise cutting techniques. The hair on top is usually left longer and can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, textured, or combed over. The sides and back are cut shorter, often with a clipper or trimmer, to create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.
