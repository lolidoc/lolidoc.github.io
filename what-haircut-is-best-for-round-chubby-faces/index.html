The Length, Layers, and Angles

The length of your hair can greatly affect the overall appearance of your face. Generally, longer haircuts tend to elongate the face and create a slimmer look. However, it's important to avoid a straight cut that falls at the chin level, as this can make your face appear wider.

Instead, opt for layered haircuts that add movement and texture to your hair. Layers can help to create the illusion of length and slim down the face. Soft, face-framing angles can also be incorporated into the haircut to further enhance your features.

It's also important to consider the placement of the layers. Avoid adding too much volume around the cheeks, as this can make your face look rounder. Instead, focus on adding volume at the crown of your head to create height and elongate your face.
