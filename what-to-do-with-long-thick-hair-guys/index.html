Are you a guy with long, thick hair and you're not sure what to do with it? Well, you're not alone. Many men struggle with finding the right hairstyles and grooming techniques for their long, thick locks. But don't worry, we're here to help! In this blog post, we'll discuss different options and tips for what to do with long thick hair guys, so you can find a style that suits you and keeps your hair looking its best.

Having long, thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a lot of hair to work with and can experiment with different styles. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you're not sure where to start. This is where our guide comes in handy.

So, what should you do with your long, thick hair? The answer depends on your personal style and preferences. Some guys prefer to keep their hair long and embrace the natural volume and texture, while others prefer a more polished and groomed look. Here are a few options to consider:

1. Embrace the Flow

If you're a fan of the "surfer" or "skater" look, then embracing the flow is the way to go. This style involves letting your hair grow out freely and allowing it to flow naturally. To maintain this look, you'll want to keep your hair healthy and well-conditioned, and consider using styling products like sea salt spray to enhance the natural texture.

2. Try a Man Bun or Ponytail

If you're looking for a more practical and low-maintenance option, a man bun or ponytail could be the answer. These styles keep your hair out of your face and off your neck, while still allowing you to showcase your long locks. To achieve a man bun or ponytail, you'll need to gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band.

3. Experiment with Braids

Braids are a versatile and trendy option for guys with long, thick hair. You can try different types of braids, such as a French braid, Dutch braid, or fishtail braid, to add some interest and texture to your hair. Braids can be styled in various ways, such as a full head of braids, a half-up half-down style, or a braided crown.

4. Get a Haircut

If you're looking for a change or want to reduce the volume of your hair, getting a haircut is always an option. You can go for a shorter style, such as a layered cut or a fade, to make your hair more manageable. A skilled hairstylist or barber can help you find a haircut that suits your face shape and personal style.

Conclusion:

When it comes to what to do with long thick hair guys, the possibilities are endless. Whether you choose to embrace the flow, try a man bun or ponytail, experiment with braids, or get a haircut, the key is to find a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Don't be afraid to try different techniques and products to achieve the look you want. With a little experimentation and patience, you'll find the perfect style for your long, thick hair.
