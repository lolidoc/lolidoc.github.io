New Short Hairstyles for Fall 2021: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a short hairstyle suit my face shape?

A: Short hairstyles can be customized to suit different face shapes. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the best style for your face shape.

Q: How can I add volume to my short hair?

A: You can add volume to your short hair by using styling products, such as volumizing mousse or texturizing sprays. Additionally, you can use styling techniques, such as backcombing or using a round brush while blow-drying.

Q: Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled for special occasions by using accessories, such as hair clips or headbands, or by adding texture and volume with styling products and techniques.

Q: How often should I get my short hair cut?

A: The frequency of haircuts will depend on the style and how fast your hair grows. On average, most people with short hair get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.
