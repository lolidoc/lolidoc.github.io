What is argan oil?

Argan oil is a natural oil that is extracted from the kernels of the argan tree, which is native to Morocco. It has been used for centuries in Moroccan culture for its numerous health and beauty benefits. Argan oil is rich in vitamin E, antioxidants, and fatty acids, which help to nourish and moisturize the hair and skin. It is also known for its ability to protect against environmental damage and promote healthy hair growth.

The history and myth of argan oil

Argan oil has a long history of use in Moroccan culture. It has been used for centuries by the Berber people, who are native to the region. In addition to its use as a beauty oil, argan oil is also used in cooking and as a traditional medicine. The production of argan oil is a labor-intensive process, as the kernels must be hand-cracked and cold-pressed to extract the oil. This has led to argan oil being referred to as "liquid gold" due to its rarity and high value.

The hidden secret of argan oil

One of the hidden secrets of argan oil is its ability to promote healthy hair growth. The fatty acids in argan oil help to nourish the hair follicles, promoting stronger, healthier hair. Additionally, argan oil can help to reduce scalp inflammation and improve blood circulation, which can also contribute to improved hair growth. By incorporating argan oil into your hair care routine, you can help to promote the growth of longer, thicker, and healthier hair.

Recommendation for using argan oil

If you're ready to start using argan oil for your thick curly hair, here are a few recommendations to get you started:

1. Start with a small amount: Argan oil is highly concentrated, so a little goes a long way. Start with a dime-sized amount and add more if needed.

2. Apply to damp hair: Argan oil is best applied to damp hair, as it helps to seal in moisture. Apply a small amount to the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

3. Use as a leave-in treatment: Argan oil can be used as a leave-in treatment to provide ongoing hydration and protection for your hair. Simply apply a small amount to your hair after washing and leave it in.

4. Protect your hair from heat: If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, be sure to apply argan oil beforehand to protect your hair from heat damage.
