Styles For Very Short Black Natural Hair In 2023

Written by Exel Apr 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

As we enter 2023, it's time to embrace the beauty and versatility of very short black natural hair. While some may think that short hair limits styling options, there are actually numerous trendy and chic styles that can be achieved. Whether you're rocking a TWA (teeny weeny afro) or a slightly longer cropped cut, this article will provide you with inspiration and tips on how to style your short natural hair.

1. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a bold and low-maintenance style that showcases your natural hair texture. This style involves shaving the hair close to the scalp, creating a clean and edgy look. It's perfect for those who want to embrace their natural hair without the hassle of styling.

2. The Finger Coils

Finger coils are a popular styling option for short natural hair. This technique involves twirling small sections of hair around your finger to create defined coils. It's a great way to enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume to your hair.

3. The Frohawk

The frohawk is a stylish and versatile option for short natural hair. This style involves creating a mini mohawk by brushing the hair on the sides towards the center and securing it with bobby pins or hair gel. You can leave the hair in its natural state or add some flair by accessorizing with hair jewelry.

4. The Wash and Go

The wash and go is a simple yet stunning option for short natural hair. This style involves washing your hair, applying a leave-in conditioner, and allowing your curls to air dry. It's a quick and easy way to showcase your natural texture and embrace your curls.

5. The Twist Out

The twist out is a classic and timeless style for short natural hair. This technique involves twisting small sections of hair and allowing them to dry overnight. In the morning, you can unravel the twists to reveal beautiful and defined curls. It's a great way to switch up your look and add some texture to your hair.

6. The Mohawk

The mohawk is a bold and fierce style that can be achieved with short natural hair. This style involves shaving the sides of your head and leaving a strip of hair in the center. You can style the center strip in various ways, such as twisting, braiding, or leaving it in its natural state.

7. The Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a versatile and stylish option for short natural hair. This technique involves sectioning your hair into small sections and twisting them into knots. You can leave the knots as is for a cute and playful look, or unravel them to create beautiful curls.

8. The Head Wrap

When you're in a rush or want to switch up your look, a head wrap is a perfect accessory for short natural hair. You can choose from a variety of colors, patterns, and textures to match your outfit or mood. It's a simple and stylish way to add some flair to your hair.

9. The Afro Puff

The afro puff is a quick and easy style for short natural hair. Simply gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie or elastic band. You can leave the puff as is for a full and voluminous look, or use a pick to add some height and volume.

10. The Side Part

A side part is a chic and sophisticated option for short natural hair. Simply create a deep part on one side of your head and use a comb or your fingers to smooth down the hair on the other side. You can leave the hair in its natural state or add some curls or waves for added texture.

Conclusion

With these stylish and trendy options for very short black natural hair, you can embrace your natural texture and showcase your personal style. Experiment with different styles and accessories to find the ones that make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember, short hair doesn't limit your styling options – it opens up a world of possibilities!
