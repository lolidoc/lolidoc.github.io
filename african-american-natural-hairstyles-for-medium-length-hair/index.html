African American Natural Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair In 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

African American natural hairstyles for medium length hair have gained popularity in recent years, and in 2023, they continue to be a trendy and stylish choice for women. These hairstyles allow individuals to embrace their natural texture and showcase their unique beauty. Whether you have curly, coily, or kinky hair, there are numerous options to explore and experiment with.

1. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a versatile and chic hairstyle that can be worn for casual or formal occasions. This hairstyle involves sectioning the hair and twisting it into small knots. It creates a stunning look that accentuates the natural texture of African American hair.

2. Twist Out

A twist out is a popular hairstyle that involves twisting small sections of hair and allowing them to unravel for defined and bouncy curls. This hairstyle is perfect for medium length hair and adds volume and dimension to your overall look.

3. Protective Styles

a) Box Braids

Box braids are a timeless and low-maintenance protective style that can be achieved with medium length hair. These braids are versatile and can be styled in various ways, allowing you to switch up your look whenever you desire.

b) Senegalese Twists

Senegalese twists are another popular protective style that involves adding synthetic hair to your natural hair. This style is perfect for medium length hair as it provides versatility and protects your natural hair from damage.

4. Afro Puff

An afro puff is a simple and stylish hairstyle that involves gathering your hair into a high or low puff. This hairstyle is perfect for medium length hair as it showcases the natural volume and texture of your hair while keeping it off your face.

5. Wash and Go

A wash and go is a quick and easy hairstyle that involves washing your hair and allowing it to air dry, embracing its natural texture. This style is perfect for medium length hair as it enhances the natural curl pattern and adds definition.

6. Cornrows

Cornrows are a classic and versatile hairstyle that can be achieved with medium length hair. This style involves braiding the hair close to the scalp, creating intricate patterns. Cornrows can be worn as a standalone style or used as a base for various other hairstyles.

7. Finger Coils

Finger coils are a popular hairstyle for medium length natural hair. This style involves twirling small sections of hair around your finger to create defined coils. Finger coils add texture and volume to your hair, giving it a stylish and playful look.

8. Updo Hairstyles

Medium length natural hair is perfect for various updo hairstyles. From elegant buns to stylish top knots, there are numerous options to choose from. Updos are a great way to showcase your natural hair while keeping it off your face and neck.

9. Flexi Rod Set

A flexi rod set is a popular heatless curling method that can be achieved with medium length natural hair. This style involves wrapping small sections of hair around flexible rods and allowing them to set overnight. The result is bouncy and defined curls without the use of heat.

10. Half Up, Half Down

A half up, half down hairstyle is a versatile and stylish option for medium length natural hair. This style involves gathering the top half of your hair and securing it while leaving the remaining hair down. It is a simple yet elegant look that can be dressed up or down.

Conclusion

African American natural hairstyles for medium length hair offer a wide range of options to embrace and showcase your natural beauty. From protective styles to wash and go looks, there is a hairstyle to suit every occasion and personal preference. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
