What to Do with Grey Hair at 30: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can stress cause grey hair at 30?

A: While stress can contribute to premature greying, it is not the sole cause. Genetics and other factors also play a role.

Q: Is it true that plucking a grey hair will cause more to grow in its place?

A: No, plucking a grey hair will not cause more grey hairs to grow. However, repeated plucking may damage the hair follicle.

Q: Can I dye my grey hair at 30?

A: Yes, many people choose to dye their grey hair to cover up the greys or achieve a different look.
