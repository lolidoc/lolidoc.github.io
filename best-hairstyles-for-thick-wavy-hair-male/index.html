Introduction

Having thick and wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse for men. While it adds volume and texture to your hairstyle, it can also be difficult to manage and style. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can embrace your thick wavy hair and rock a stylish look. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for men with thick wavy hair in 2023.

1. Short Textured Crop

If you prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle, a short textured crop is a great option for thick wavy hair. This haircut is characterized by short sides and a slightly longer length on top. To enhance the natural texture of your hair, use a texturizing product like a sea salt spray or a matte pomade. This will give your hair a messy and effortless look.

2. Medium Length Waves

If you want to showcase your thick wavy hair, go for a medium length hairstyle. This style allows your waves to flow freely and adds a touch of sophistication to your look. Ask your barber to add layers to your hair to prevent it from looking too bulky. To style, apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to damp hair and let it air dry for natural and defined waves.

3. Long Flowing Locks

If you're willing to embrace your inner rockstar, long flowing locks are a fantastic choice for men with thick wavy hair. This hairstyle exudes confidence and rebelliousness. To maintain your long hair, make sure to regularly trim the ends to prevent split ends and frizz. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your locks hydrated and shiny.

4. Undercut with Wavy Top

An undercut with a wavy top is a trendy and versatile hairstyle for men with thick wavy hair. The undercut on the sides and back creates a sharp contrast with the voluminous waves on top. To style the wavy top, use a curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair to enhance the natural texture. You can also use a little bit of hair wax or pomade to define the waves and add shine.

5. Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a stylish and effortless hairstyle that works well with thick wavy hair. This hairstyle combines volume and texture with a casual and tousled look. To achieve the messy quiff, apply a styling product like a texturizing powder or clay to your damp hair. Use a hairdryer and your fingers to create volume and texture, then finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

6. Slicked Back Waves

If you prefer a more polished and sophisticated look, try slicking back your waves. This hairstyle is perfect for formal occasions or when you want to make a statement. Apply a generous amount of styling gel or pomade to wet hair and comb it back. Use a fine-toothed comb to create sleek and defined waves. Finish off with a strong-hold hairspray to keep the style intact.

7. Side Part with Waves

The classic side part hairstyle is a timeless choice for men with thick wavy hair. This style adds a touch of elegance and can be worn for both formal and casual occasions. To achieve the side part, apply a styling product to damp hair and comb it to the side. Use your fingers to tousle the waves and create a natural look. Finish off with a medium-hold hairspray for added hold.

8. Faux Hawk

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, the faux hawk is an excellent option for thick wavy hair. This style features a strip of longer hair in the middle of the head, with the sides shaved or closely trimmed. Use a strong-hold styling product like a gel or wax to create height and texture in the middle section. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to make a statement.

9. Mohawk with Waves

If you're feeling adventurous, a mohawk with waves is a unique and eye-catching hairstyle for men with thick wavy hair. This style combines the boldness of a mohawk with the natural texture of waves. Shave or closely trim the sides, leaving a strip of longer hair in the middle. Style the middle section with a curl-enhancing product to create defined waves. Finish off with a strong-hold hairspray to keep the waves intact.

10. Braided Man Bun

For those with longer thick wavy hair, the braided man bun is a trendy and fashionable hairstyle. This style keeps your hair out of your face while adding a unique and intricate touch. Start by braiding a section of your hair at the top of your head. Gather the rest of your hair into a bun and secure it with an elastic band. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to showcase their individuality.
