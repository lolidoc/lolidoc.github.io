What is Frizzy Thick Hair?

Frizzy thick hair is characterized by its coarse texture, tendency to frizz, and abundance of volume. It can be challenging to style and manage due to its unruly nature. Frizz occurs when the hair cuticle is raised, allowing moisture to enter the hair shaft and causing it to swell. This results in a frizzy appearance and can make the hair difficult to control. Thick hair, on the other hand, refers to the density of individual hair strands. It has more hair per square inch than fine or thin hair, which can make it look fuller and more voluminous.
