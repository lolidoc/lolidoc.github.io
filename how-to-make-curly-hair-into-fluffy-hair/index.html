How To Make Curly Hair Into Fluffy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Vannesa Dec 03, 2022 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having curly hair can be both a blessing and a challenge. While curls are beautiful and unique, they can also be difficult to manage and style. If you're looking to transform your curly hair into fluffy hair, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we will provide you with simple yet effective tips and techniques to achieve the desired fluffy look for your curls.

Understanding Your Hair Type

Before we begin, it's important to understand your hair type. Curly hair can range from loose waves to tight coils, and each type requires different care and styling methods. Determine your hair type to tailor your routine accordingly.

Step 1: Proper Cleansing

The foundation of achieving fluffy hair starts with proper cleansing. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner specifically formulated for curly hair. Gently massage your scalp to remove any product buildup or impurities without disrupting the natural oils that keep your hair moisturized.

Step 2: Hydration is Key

Curly hair tends to be dry and prone to frizz, so keeping it hydrated is crucial. Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to replenish moisture and nourish your curls. Additionally, apply a leave-in conditioner daily to lock in moisture and enhance the fluffiness of your hair.

Step 3: Ditch the Towel

Traditional towels can cause friction, leading to frizz and breakage. Instead, opt for a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water from your hair. Avoid rubbing or twisting your curls, as this can disturb their natural pattern.

Step 4: Apply a Styling Product

To achieve fluffy hair, it's essential to apply a styling product that provides hold and definition without weighing down your curls. Look for lightweight creams, gels, or mousses that are specifically designed for curly hair. Apply the product evenly from roots to ends, scrunching your hair to encourage natural volume.

Step 5: Diffuse or Air Dry

Ditch the heat tools and opt for air drying or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Diffusing allows you to dry your hair while enhancing your curls' natural shape and volume. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser to avoid disturbing the curls.

Step 6: Fluff and Separate

Once your hair is dry, it's time to fluff and separate your curls to achieve the desired fluffy look. Use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to gently separate the curls, starting from the roots and working your way down. Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the natural pattern.

Step 7: Protect Overnight

To maintain the fluffiness of your hair overnight, consider using a silk or satin pillowcase. These materials help reduce friction, preventing frizz and preserving the shape of your curls. You can also pineapple your hair by loosely gathering it on top of your head and securing it with a scrunchie or silk scarf.

Step 8: Refresh as Needed

Throughout the day or between washes, you may need to refresh your hair to maintain the fluffiness. Use a water-based curl refresher spray or simply dampen your hands with water and scrunch your curls to revive them. Avoid over-applying products, as this can weigh down your hair.

Step 9: Embrace Natural Oils

Natural oils, such as argan oil or jojoba oil, can help add shine and moisture to your curls. Apply a small amount of oil to your palms and gently scrunch it into your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help seal in moisture and give your curls a healthy, fluffy appearance.

Conclusion

Transforming your curly hair into fluffy hair is achievable with the right techniques and products. By following the steps outlined in this guide, you can enhance your curls' volume and achieve the desired fluffy look. Remember to embrace your natural texture and experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair type. With patience and dedication, you'll have beautifully fluffy hair that turns heads wherever you go.
