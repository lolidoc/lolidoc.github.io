How to Style Short Hairstyles for Women Over 40

Styling short hairstyles for women over 40 is quick and easy with the right tools and techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a small amount of styling mousse or gel to your hair to add hold and texture. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or create soft waves or curls. Start at the roots and slowly glide the flat iron down the length of your hair, twisting it slightly to create waves or curls.

Step 4: Add Finishing Touches

To add volume and texture, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo at the roots of your hair. This will help to create lift and give your hair a more lived-in look. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.
