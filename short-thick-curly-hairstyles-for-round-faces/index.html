Short Thick Curly Hairstyles for Round Faces

Introduction

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse, especially for those with round faces. While it adds volume and texture, it can also make the face appear wider. However, with the right hairstyle, you can accentuate your features and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we will explore some short and thick curly hairstyles that are perfect for round faces.

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and chic hairstyle that works well for round faces. The short length helps to elongate the face, while the curls add volume and texture. You can opt for a tousled pixie cut or a more defined one, depending on your preference. Pair it with side-swept bangs to further flatter your face shape.

2. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers is another excellent choice for round faces with thick curly hair. The layers add movement and dimension to the hair, while the length helps to balance out the roundness of the face. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to enhance the texture and showcase your curls.

3. Curly Shag

A curly shag haircut is a trendy and stylish option for those with round faces. It features short layers throughout the hair, creating a textured and voluminous look. This hairstyle works well with thick curly hair as it allows the curls to bounce freely, framing the face beautifully.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

If you want to draw attention away from the roundness of your face, consider adding side-swept bangs to your short curly hairstyle. The diagonal line created by the bangs helps to create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face shape. This versatile hairstyle can be paired with various short haircuts, such as a bob or a pixie cut.

5. Curly Mohawk

For a bold and edgy look, try a curly mohawk hairstyle. This style involves shaving the sides of the head while leaving a strip of longer curly hair in the middle. The height and volume of the mohawk help to elongate the face, making it an excellent choice for round faces.

6. Curly Ponytail

If you prefer to wear your hair up, a curly ponytail can be a stylish option. Gather your thick curly hair into a high or low ponytail and let the curls cascade down. This hairstyle not only keeps your hair out of your face but also showcases your natural curls, enhancing your overall appearance.

7. Curly Bob

A curly bob is a classic and timeless hairstyle that suits round faces. Opt for a chin-length bob to create a balanced and flattering look. The curls add softness and dimension to the hairstyle, while the length helps to elongate the face. This versatile haircut can be worn sleek and polished or tousled for a more relaxed vibe.

8. Short Curly Updo

For special occasions or events, consider a short curly updo. Gather your curls into a low bun or a twisted updo, securing them with bobby pins. This elegant hairstyle not only keeps your hair off your face but also adds a touch of sophistication to your overall look.

9. Curly Lob

A curly lob, or long bob, is a flattering choice for round faces with thick curly hair. The length of the lob helps to elongate the face, while the curls add movement and texture. This versatile hairstyle can be styled straight or with loose curls, depending on your preference and the occasion.

10. Curly Side Sweep

If you want to create a dramatic and glamorous look, try a curly side sweep hairstyle. Part your hair to one side and let the curls flow over your shoulder. This asymmetrical style helps to create the illusion of a narrower face shape and highlights your natural curls.
