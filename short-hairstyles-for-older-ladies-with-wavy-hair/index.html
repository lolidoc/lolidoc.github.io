How to Achieve Short Hairstyles for Older Ladies with Wavy Hair

If you're ready to try out a short hairstyle for older ladies with wavy hair, here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed to enhance your natural waves.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to your damp hair.
  3. Using a diffuser, blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting, scrunching your hair with your hands to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls throughout your hair.
  5. Finish off your look with a light-hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.
