What is a Medium Haircut?

A medium haircut typically falls between the chin and shoulder length. It offers versatility and can be styled in various ways, making it a popular choice for those with round faces. The length of a medium haircut allows for different styling options, from sleek and straight to voluminous and wavy.

Now that we know what a medium haircut is, let's take a look at its history and myths.

History and Myth of Medium Haircuts

Medium haircuts have been popular throughout history, with various iterations seen in different eras. In the 1920s, the bob haircut gained popularity, and it has remained a classic choice for those with round faces. In the 1960s, the "Twiggy" haircut, characterized by its short length and voluminous crown, became a trend.

There is a common myth that round-faced individuals should avoid medium haircuts as they can make the face appear wider. However, this myth is debunked by the fact that the right medium haircut can actually enhance the features of a round face and create a more balanced look.

The Hidden Secret of Best Medium Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

The hidden secret of the best medium haircuts for round faces in 2020 lies in the power of layers. Recommendations for Best Medium Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

When it comes to recommendations for the best medium haircuts for round faces in 2020, it's important to consider your individual features and preferences. However, some popular choices include the shoulder-length bob with layers, the long bob (also known as the "lob"), and the shag haircut.

Tips for Best Medium Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear bangs with a medium haircut for a round face?

A: Yes, bangs can be a great addition to a medium haircut for a round face. Opt for side-swept bangs or curtain bangs, as they help to create angles and soften the face.

Q: Are there any specific styling products recommended for medium haircuts for round faces?

A: It's always important to use products that suit your hair type and desired style. However, volumizing products, texturizing sprays, and lightweight mousses can help to add volume and texture to a medium haircut for a round face.

Q: Can I wear my medium haircut straight and sleek?

A: Absolutely! A medium haircut can be styled straight and sleek, which can create a sophisticated and polished look. Just remember to add volume at the crown to balance out the roundness of your face.

Q: How often should I get my medium haircut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your medium haircut.
