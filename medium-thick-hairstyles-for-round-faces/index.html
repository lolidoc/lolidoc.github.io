Medium Thick Hairstyles For Round Faces

Medium Thick Hairstyles for Round Faces

Introduction

Having a round face shape can be a challenge when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. However, with medium thick hair, there are plenty of options to enhance your features and create a flattering look. In this article, we will explore various hairstyles that are suitable for round faces and provide tips on how to style them.

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a versatile hairstyle that works well for round faces. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, creating a slimming effect. To style, use a round brush while blow-drying to add extra lift at the roots. Finish with a texturizing spray for added definition.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to elongate the face and draw attention away from the roundness. Opt for longer bangs that graze the eyebrows and sweep them to the side. This will create a diagonal line that visually lengthens the face.

3. Shoulder-Length Waves

Shoulder-length waves are a flattering choice for round faces. The waves add softness and create the illusion of a narrower face shape. Use a curling iron to create loose waves and finish with a texturizing spray to enhance the texture.

4. Messy Updo

A messy updo is a stylish option for medium thick hair. Pull the hair back into a loose bun or twist and leave a few strands loose to frame the face. This hairstyle adds height and elongates the face, making it a great choice for round faces.

5. Long Layers

Long layers are a classic option that works well for round faces. The layers add movement and create a slimming effect. To style, use a large barrel curling iron to create loose curls. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray to maintain the shape throughout the day.

6. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a versatile choice for medium thick hair. It allows you to showcase the length while adding volume at the crown. Tease the hair at the crown for extra height and secure the top section with bobby pins or a decorative clip.

7. Textured Pixie Cut

If you prefer shorter hairstyles, a textured pixie cut can complement a round face shape. Ask your stylist to add layers and texture to create movement. Use a styling wax or pomade to enhance the texture and create a tousled look.

8. Side Braid

A side braid is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that can flatter round faces. Sweep the hair to one side and create a loose braid. Pull out a few strands to frame the face and secure the braid with a clear elastic. This hairstyle adds visual interest and draws attention away from the roundness of the face.

9. Sleek Ponytail

A sleek ponytail is a chic option for round faces. Pull the hair back into a high or low ponytail, ensuring that it is sleek and free from any bumps or flyaways. Use a shine-enhancing serum to add a polished finish.

10. Faux Hawk

A faux hawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can add dimension to a round face shape. Use a styling gel or mousse to create height at the crown and push the hair towards the center. Secure with bobby pins and finish with a strong hold hairspray for long-lasting style.

Conclusion

Medium thick hairstyles for round faces offer a wide range of options to enhance your features and create a flattering look. Whether you prefer short or long hair, there are various styles that can complement your round face shape. Experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find the one that suits you best. Remember to consider your hair texture and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. With the right hairstyle, you can embrace your round face shape and feel confident in your appearance.
