Common Questions About Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair and Round Face

1. Can I have a short haircut if I have thick wavy hair and a round face?

Yes, you can have a short haircut if you have thick wavy hair and a round face. However, it's important to choose a style that adds length and creates balance.

2. How often should I get my haircut if I have thick wavy hair and a round face?

The frequency of haircuts depends on personal preference and hair growth rate. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of the hair.
