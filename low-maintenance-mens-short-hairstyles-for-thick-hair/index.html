Having thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it adds volume and texture to your hair. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. Many men with thick hair struggle to find a hairstyle that is both low maintenance and flattering.

Fortunately, there are several options for low maintenance mens short hairstyles for thick hair. One popular choice is the buzz cut. This style involves cutting the hair very short all over the head. It requires minimal styling and maintenance, making it perfect for busy men.

Another option is the crew cut. This style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving it slightly longer on top. It is a versatile and timeless look that requires minimal styling. Simply run your fingers through your hair in the morning and you're good to go!

Low Maintenance Mens Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair: The Buzz Cut

Not only is the buzz cut low maintenance, but it also looks great on men with thick hair. It adds a rugged and masculine touch to your appearance, making you look more confident and self-assured.

Low Maintenance Mens Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair: The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a timeless and classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is suitable for all ages and face shapes, making it a popular choice among men with thick hair. Plus, it requires minimal styling and maintenance, making it perfect for busy men.
