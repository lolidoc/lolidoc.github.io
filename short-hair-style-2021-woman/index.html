How to Style Short Hair

Styling short hair style 2021 woman is easier than you might think. Here are some simple steps to achieve a stylish look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling gel or mousse to add texture and hold.
  3. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to create volume at the roots and shape your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls for a more polished look.
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place throughout the day.
