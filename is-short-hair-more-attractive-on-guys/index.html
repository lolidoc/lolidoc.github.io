What is Short Hair?

Short hair refers to hairstyles that are cut above the shoulders or cropped close to the scalp. It typically involves minimal length and requires regular maintenance to keep the style intact. Short hair can range from buzz cuts to textured crops, offering a wide variety of options for men to choose from.

History and Myth of Short Hair

Throughout history, short hair has been associated with masculinity and strength. In ancient times, warriors and soldiers would often have short hair to prevent their opponents from having something to grab onto during combat. This notion of short hair symbolizing power and masculinity has transcended through different eras and cultures.
