Are you a black girl with short hair and wondering how to achieve the trendy space buns hairstyle? Look no further! In this blog post, we will guide you step-by-step on how to do space buns with short hair. Whether you're going for a casual look or getting ready for a special occasion, space buns are a fun and stylish way to rock your short hair.

Many black girls with short hair often struggle to find hairstyles that suit their hair texture and length. The space buns hairstyle can be particularly challenging for those with short hair. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect space buns look and feel confident in your style.

How to do space buns with short hair black girl

To achieve space buns with short hair, you'll need a few essential tools and products:

1. Comb or brush

2. Hair elastics or small rubber bands

3. Bobby pins

4. Hair gel or styling product

5. Hairspray

Now let's dive into the step-by-step process of creating space buns with short hair:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by combing or brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Apply a small amount of hair gel or styling product to add some texture and hold to your hair. This will make it easier to manipulate your hair into space buns.

Step 2: Part your hair

Decide where you want to part your hair. You can choose a middle part or a side part, depending on your preference. Use your comb to create a clean and straight part.

Step 3: Create ponytails

Separate your hair into two equal sections, one on each side of your head. Gather each section into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair elastic or small rubber band. Make sure the ponytails are positioned towards the top of your head.

Step 4: Form the buns

Take one ponytail and twist it around the base to create a bun shape. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide the ends of your hair. Repeat this step with the other ponytail.

Step 5: Finishing touches

Once you have formed both buns, use hairspray to set the style and keep the buns in place. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.

And there you have it! You've successfully created space buns with your short hair. Now you can rock this trendy hairstyle and feel fabulous.

Tips for achieving the perfect space buns with short hair

1. Use hair extensions: If you have very short hair and want to create fuller and longer space buns, consider using hair extensions. They can add volume and length to your hair, making it easier to form the buns.

2. Experiment with different partings: Don't be afraid to try different partings to achieve different looks with your space buns. You can try a zigzag parting or a diagonal parting for a unique and stylish twist.

3. Play with accessories: Add some flair to your space buns by incorporating accessories such as hair clips, ribbons, or beads. This can elevate your hairstyle and make it even more eye-catching.

4. Practice makes perfect: Creating space buns with short hair may require some practice, especially if you're new to styling your own hair. Don't get discouraged if it doesn't turn out perfectly the first time. Keep practicing, and you'll get the hang of it!

Conclusion of how to do space buns with short hair black girl

Now that you know how to do space buns with short hair, you can confidently rock this trendy hairstyle. Remember to prep your hair, create ponytails, form the buns, and finish with hairspray for a long-lasting style. Don't forget to experiment with different partings and accessories to make the look your own. With a little practice and creativity, you'll be able to achieve the perfect space buns with your short hair.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do space buns with natural hair?

A: Yes, absolutely! Space buns can be done with natural hair, regardless of its length. Just make sure to moisturize and detangle your hair before styling to ensure a smooth and defined look.

Q: How long does it take to do space buns with short hair?

A: The time it takes to do space buns with short hair can vary depending on your hair texture and experience with styling. On average, it can take anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes to complete the hairstyle.

Q: Can I sleep with space buns in my hair?

A: It is not recommended to sleep with space buns in your hair, as it can cause discomfort and damage to your hair. It's best to take them out before going to bed and recreate the style in the morning if desired.

Q: How do I maintain space buns throughout the day?

A: To maintain your space buns throughout the day, you can carry a small brush or comb with you to touch up any loose strands. You can also use hairspray to keep the buns in place and bobby pins to secure any flyaways.
