Medium Hairstyles for Wavy Hair Over 50: Recommendations

If you have wavy hair and are over 50, here are some medium hairstyles that you might consider:

  1. Shoulder-length bob: This classic hairstyle is timeless and looks great on women of all ages. The shoulder-length bob is versatile and can be styled straight or with loose waves for a more relaxed look.
  2. Layered cut: Adding layers to your medium-length hair can create movement and volume, making your waves appear fuller and more defined. This hairstyle is perfect for women with thin or fine hair.
  3. Shaggy style: The shag haircut is a trendy and edgy choice for women with wavy hair. The layers and choppy ends add texture and volume, giving your hair a modern and youthful look.
  4. Long bob: Also known as the "lob," the long bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn straight or wavy. It falls just below the shoulders and is a great option for women who want a medium-length hairstyle with minimal maintenance.

These are just a few of the many medium hairstyles for wavy hair over 50. The key is to find a hairstyle that suits your face shape, enhances your natural waves, and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Medium Hairstyles for Wavy Hair Over 50: Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Here are some tips to help you style and maintain your medium wavy hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and lift to your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent it from becoming dry and frizzy. Instead, try washing it every 2-3 days.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to damp hair to control frizz and define your waves.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your natural waves and add volume.
  • Invest in a good quality heat protectant spray to minimize damage when using heat styling tools.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.
