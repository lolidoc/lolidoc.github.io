How to Achieve 1960 Hairstyles for Short Hair

If you're ready to embrace the retro glamour of 1960 hairstyles for short hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume.
  3. Section off your hair and use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls.
  4. Once your curls have cooled, gently brush them out to create soft waves.
  5. Tease the crown of your hair to add height and volume.
  6. Smooth out any flyaways with a bit of hairspray or styling gel.
  7. Finish off the look with a spritz of shine spray for a polished finish.
