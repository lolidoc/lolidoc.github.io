Is Short Hair Better for Curly Hair: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will cutting my hair short make it grow faster?

A: No, cutting your hair short does not affect its growth rate. Hair growth occurs at the root, not the ends.

Q: Will cutting my hair short make it less curly?

A: No, cutting your hair short does not change its natural curl pattern. The curl pattern is determined by the shape of the hair follicle.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair if it is short?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair if it is short. However, keep in mind that frequent heat styling can cause damage to your hair.

Q: How often do I need to trim my short curly hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
