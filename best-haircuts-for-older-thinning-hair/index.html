Thinning hair can be a common concern for many as they get older. It can affect both men and women and may be caused by factors such as hormonal changes, genetics, and overall health. Thinning hair can lead to a loss of volume and density, making it challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your features.

The key to finding the best haircut for older thinning hair is to choose a style that adds volume and texture. Layered cuts are a great option as they create the illusion of fullness and movement.
