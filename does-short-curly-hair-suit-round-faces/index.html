Understanding Short Curly Hair and Round Faces

Before we delve deeper into the topic of short curly hair and round faces, let's first understand what it means to have a round face shape. A round face is characterized by equal width and length, with soft and rounded features. The goal when styling short curly hair for a round face is to create angles and add height to balance out the roundness.

When choosing a haircut for short curly hair, consider options that add volume and texture to the crown area, such as layered cuts, pixie cuts, or bob hairstyles. These hairstyles provide the necessary height and dimension to counterbalance the roundness of your face.

Why Does Short Curly Hair Suit Round Faces?

Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair and Round Faces

Styling short curly hair for a round face requires some special considerations. Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural curls and minimize frizz.
  • Avoid heavy products that weigh down your curls and opt for lightweight, curl-enhancing products instead.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to create the illusion of length and balance.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I have bangs with short curly hair and a round face?

A: Absolutely! Bangs can be a great addition to short curly hair and a round face. Opt for side-swept bangs or curtain bangs to create angles and add dimension to your face.

Q: Will short curly hair make my face look rounder?

A: Short curly hair, when styled correctly, can actually create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. By adding volume and height to the crown area, you can balance out the roundness of your face.

Q: Are there any specific hairstyles I should avoid with short curly hair and a round face?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, it's generally recommended to avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, as they can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length to create balance.

Q: Can I straighten my short curly hair if I have a round face?

A: Of course! Straightening your short curly hair can be a fun way to switch up your look and create a sleeker appearance. Just be sure to use heat protectant products and avoid excessive heat styling to maintain the health of your hair.
