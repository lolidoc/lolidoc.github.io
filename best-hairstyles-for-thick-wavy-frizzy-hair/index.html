Best Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Frizzy Hair - 2023

Introduction

Having thick, wavy, and frizzy hair can be a challenge to manage and style. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can embrace your natural texture and create stunning hairstyles that suit your personality and lifestyle. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for thick wavy frizzy hair in 2023, providing you with inspiration and tips to enhance your locks.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for thick wavy frizzy hair as they help to reduce bulk and add movement to your tresses. Ask your hairstylist to create long layers that start around your chin or collarbone. This will help to distribute the volume evenly and prevent your hair from looking weighed down. Style your hair with a curl-enhancing mousse or cream to define your waves and control frizz.

2. Bob with Soft Waves

A bob haircut with soft waves is a trendy and low-maintenance option for thick wavy frizzy hair. Opt for a chin-length or slightly longer bob and use a curling iron to create loose waves. This hairstyle adds texture and dimension to your hair while keeping it manageable. Apply a frizz-control serum or oil to tame any unruly strands and give your hair a shiny finish.

3. Textured Pixie Cut

If you're feeling daring, a textured pixie cut can be a fantastic choice for thick wavy frizzy hair. This short hairstyle brings out the natural texture of your hair and requires minimal styling. Ask your hairstylist to add layers and texture to create movement and volume. Use a lightweight texturizing spray or pomade to enhance the natural waves and add definition.

4. Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyle

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a versatile and stylish option for those with thick wavy frizzy hair. Gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair clip or elastic, leaving the rest of your hair down. This hairstyle keeps your hair away from your face while showcasing your beautiful waves. Apply a curl-defining gel or cream to enhance the texture and prevent frizz.

5. Braided Crown

A braided crown is a romantic and elegant hairstyle that works well with thick wavy frizzy hair. Divide your hair into two sections and braid each section. Cross the braids over the top of your head and secure them with bobby pins. This hairstyle not only tames frizz but also adds a touch of sophistication to your overall look. Use a strong-hold hairspray to keep the braids in place throughout the day.

6. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and effortless hairstyle that can be achieved with thick wavy frizzy hair. Gather your hair into a high or low ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and allow some strands to fall loose for a relaxed look. This hairstyle is perfect for casual occasions or when you're on the go. Apply a smoothing serum or cream to tame frizz and add shine.

7. Side Swept Curls

Side swept curls are a glamorous and flattering option for thick wavy frizzy hair. Use a curling iron to create loose curls and sweep them to one side of your face. This hairstyle adds volume and movement to your hair while keeping it under control. Apply a flexible-hold hairspray to keep the curls in place throughout the day.

8. Top Knot

A top knot is a stylish and practical hairstyle for those with thick wavy frizzy hair. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun at the top of your head. Secure the bun with bobby pins and use a hairband or scrunchie to add an extra touch of style. This hairstyle keeps your hair off your face and neck, making it perfect for hot summer days or workouts.

9. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a timeless and effortless hairstyle that complements thick wavy frizzy hair beautifully. Apply a sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser to speed up the process. This hairstyle gives your hair a relaxed, beachy look and adds texture and definition to your waves.

10. Updo with Twists

An updo with twists is an elegant and sophisticated hairstyle for special occasions or formal events. Divide your hair into two sections and twist each section towards the back of your head. Cross the twists over each other and secure them with bobby pins. This hairstyle not only tames frizz but also adds an intricate detail to your overall look. Finish with a strong-hold hairspray to keep everything in place.
