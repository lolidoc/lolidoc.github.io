Tips for Using the Best Curler for Short Thin Hair

Question and Answer:

1. Can I use a regular curling iron for short thin hair?

2. How often should I curl my short thin hair?

It is recommended to curl your hair no more than 2-3 times a week to prevent damage. Give your hair time to rest and recover between styling sessions.
