How To Style Naturally Curly Hair For Guys

Written by Michael Mar 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to style especially for guys However with the right techniques and products you can embrace your natural curls and create a stylish and trendy look In this article we will provide you with some useful tips and tricks on how to style naturally curly hair for guys in 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

Curly hair can be a challenge to style, especially for guys. However, with the right techniques and products, you can embrace your natural curls and create a stylish and trendy look. In this article, we will provide you with some useful tips and tricks on how to style naturally curly hair for guys in 2023.

Understanding Your Hair Type

Before you start styling your curly hair, it's important to understand your hair type. Curly hair can range from loose waves to tight coils, and each type requires different styling techniques and products. Determine the thickness, density, and curl pattern of your hair to choose the right products and styling methods.

Shampoo and Conditioner

Avoid using harsh shampoos that strip away the natural oils from your hair. Opt for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help to moisturize and define your curls, making them easier to style.

Leave-In Conditioner

Applying a leave-in conditioner to damp hair can help to control frizz and enhance the natural curl pattern. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair. This will provide moisture and make your curls more manageable.

Styling Products

Choose styling products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for gels, creams, or mousses that provide hold and definition without making your hair stiff or crunchy. Apply the product evenly through your hair, focusing on the ends to avoid weighing down your curls.

Diffusing or Air Drying

There are two main methods to dry your curly hair – diffusing or air drying. Diffusing involves using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to gently dry your curls while maintaining their shape. Air drying, on the other hand, allows your curls to dry naturally without using any heat. Experiment with both methods to see which one works best for your hair type.

Hairstyle Options

There are several hairstyles that work well for naturally curly hair. Consider a shorter haircut, such as a curly undercut or a tapered fade, to keep your curls in control and add some structure to your look. If you prefer longer hair, consider growing out your curls and embracing a more voluminous and free-flowing style.

Accessorize

Accessorizing your curly hair can add a touch of style and personality to your look. Consider using headbands, bandanas, or hair clips to keep your curls away from your face or to add a pop of color to your hairstyle. Experiment with different accessories to find what suits your style.

Nighttime Haircare

Protect your curls while you sleep by using a satin or silk pillowcase. These materials reduce friction and prevent your hair from becoming frizzy or tangled overnight. You can also consider using a satin or silk bonnet or scarf to protect your curls further.

Regular Trims

To maintain healthy and well-defined curls, it's important to get regular trims. Trimming your ends every 6-8 weeks will prevent split ends and promote hair growth. Find a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair to ensure they understand the unique needs of your curls.

Conclusion

Styling naturally curly hair for guys doesn't have to be a challenge. By understanding your hair type, using the right products, and experimenting with different styling techniques, you can embrace your curls and create a stylish and trendy look. Follow the tips and tricks provided in this article to achieve the best results for your curly hair in 2023.
