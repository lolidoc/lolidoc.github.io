Are you tired of struggling with your long wavy hair? Do you find it difficult to find the perfect haircut that suits your hair type? Well, you're in luck! In this blog post, we will discuss the best haircuts for long wavy hair in 2020 and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle.

Having long wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it gives you a beautiful and romantic look, it can also be challenging to manage and style. Finding the right haircut that enhances your natural waves and flatters your face shape is crucial to achieve the perfect look.

When it comes to haircuts for long wavy hair in 2020, the target is to find a style that embraces and enhances your natural waves. This year, we are seeing a trend towards longer layered cuts, shaggy styles, and beachy waves. These haircuts add movement and texture to your hair, making it look effortlessly chic and stylish.

In summary, the best haircuts for long wavy hair in 2020 are those that embrace and enhance your natural waves. Longer layered cuts, shaggy styles, and beachy waves are on-trend and can give you a chic and stylish look. Now, let's dive deeper into each of these styles.

Long Layered Cuts for Long Wavy Hair

Long layered cuts are perfect for those with long wavy hair as they add movement and dimension to your locks. This style involves cutting different lengths of layers throughout your hair, which helps to enhance your waves and create a more voluminous look. The layers also help to frame your face and add softness to your overall appearance.

When getting a long layered cut, make sure to communicate with your hairstylist about the length and placement of the layers. You can opt for shorter layers on the top to add volume, or longer layers towards the ends to create a more cascading effect. The key is to find the right balance that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Shaggy Styles for Long Wavy Hair

Shaggy styles are making a comeback in 2020 and are perfect for those with long wavy hair. This haircut involves adding layers and texture throughout your hair, giving it a tousled and effortless look. The shaggy style works particularly well with wavy hair as it enhances the natural waves and adds movement.

To achieve a shaggy look, ask your hairstylist to add choppy layers and texture to your hair. You can also opt for a curtain bang or side-swept bangs to frame your face and add a touch of edginess to your overall look. Don't be afraid to embrace the messy and undone vibe of the shaggy style, as that's what makes it so trendy and cool.

Beachy Waves for Long Wavy Hair

Beachy waves are a timeless and classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. This effortless and carefree look is perfect for those with long wavy hair as it enhances the natural waves and gives you a relaxed and beachy vibe.

To achieve beachy waves, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves throughout your hair. Alternatively, you can also try braiding your damp hair overnight and unraveling the braids in the morning for natural-looking waves. Finish off the look with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture and hold to your waves.

Tips for Styling Long Wavy Hair

Now that you know about the best haircuts for long wavy hair in 2020, here are some tips to help you style and maintain your waves:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage and frizz.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to define and enhance your natural waves.

3. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can disrupt the wave pattern and cause frizz.

4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently and enhance your waves.

5. Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and keep your waves intact overnight.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I get a short haircut if I have long wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have long wavy hair. However, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a short style. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best short haircut that suits your features and enhances your waves.

Q: How often should I trim my long wavy hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your long wavy hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair. Regular trims also help to keep your waves looking fresh and bouncy.

Q: Can I color my long wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can color your long wavy hair. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and enhances your waves. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the best hair color for your long wavy hair.

Q: How can I protect my long wavy hair from heat damage?

A: To protect your long wavy hair from heat damage, use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Set the temperature of your styling tools to a lower setting and avoid excessive heat exposure. It's also important to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Conclusion of Haircuts for Long Wavy Hair 2020

In conclusion, finding the perfect haircut for long wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right style and maintenance, you can achieve a beautiful and effortless look. Whether you opt for long layered cuts, shaggy styles, or beachy waves, make sure to embrace and enhance your natural waves. Remember to follow the tips for styling long wavy hair to keep your waves looking their best. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can rock your long wavy hair in 2020 and beyond.
