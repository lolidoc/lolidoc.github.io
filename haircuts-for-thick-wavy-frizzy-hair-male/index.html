2. Textured Crop

A textured crop is a trendy haircut that works well for thick, wavy, frizzy hair male. It involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back while leaving some length on top. This style adds definition to your waves and reduces volume.

3. Undercut

An undercut is a versatile haircut that can work well for thick, wavy, frizzy hair male. It involves shaving or cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. This style adds contrast and helps to control frizz.
