Hairstyles for Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

Introduction

Having thick wavy hair can be a blessing for men who know how to style it. With the right haircut and products, you can rock a variety of trendy hairstyles. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair in 2023.

The Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It involves keeping the hair on the sides and back short while leaving the top long and voluminous. To style, apply a pomade or wax to your damp hair and comb it upward and backward, creating a sleek and voluminous look.

The Messy Textured Crop

If you prefer a more casual and low-maintenance look, the messy textured crop is a great option. This hairstyle works well with thick wavy hair as it adds texture and volume. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a short to medium-length crop with layered top. Apply a texturizing spray or paste to add definition and create a messy, effortless style.

The Undercut

The undercut is a versatile hairstyle that works well with thick wavy hair. It involves keeping the sides and back short while leaving the top long. This creates a stylish contrast and allows you to style your wavy hair on top in various ways. You can slick it back, create a side part, or leave it messy for a more relaxed look.

The Long Flow

If you're a fan of longer hair, embrace your thick wavy locks with a long flow hairstyle. This style is all about letting your hair grow out and flow freely. To maintain this look, make sure to regularly trim your ends to avoid split ends and use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair healthy and frizz-free.

Product Recommendations

1. Pomade

A good pomade can help you achieve a sleek and polished look for hairstyles like the classic pompadour. Look for a water-based pomade that provides a strong hold without making your hair look greasy.

2. Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray is essential for creating messy and textured hairstyles. It adds grip and definition to your hair, making it easier to style and giving it a natural, lived-in look.

3. Moisturizing Conditioner

Thick wavy hair tends to be dry and prone to frizz. A moisturizing conditioner can help hydrate your hair, reduce frizz, and enhance your natural waves. Look for a conditioner that is specifically formulated for thick and wavy hair.

Conclusion

With the right haircut and products, guys with thick wavy hair can rock a variety of stylish hairstyles. Whether you prefer a classic look like the pompadour or a more relaxed style like the long flow, embrace your natural texture and experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your personality and lifestyle.
