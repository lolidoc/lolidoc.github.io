What is argan oil?

Argan oil is a natural oil that is derived from the kernels of the argan tree. It is native to Morocco and has been used for centuries for its various health and beauty benefits. Argan oil is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and fatty acids, which contribute to its nourishing and moisturizing properties. It is commonly used in hair and skincare products due to its ability to promote hair growth, hydrate the skin, and reduce the signs of aging.

History and Myth of Argan Oil

Argan oil has a long history of use in Morocco, where it is known as "liquid gold." It has been used for centuries by the Berber people for its various health and beauty benefits. According to legend, the benefits of argan oil were discovered by goats. It is said that the goats would climb the argan trees and eat the fruit, and the oil would then be extracted from their droppings. While this may be just a myth, it is true that argan oil has been used for centuries for its numerous benefits.

The Hidden Secret of Argan Oil

The hidden secret of argan oil lies in its unique composition. Argan oil is rich in antioxidants, which help protect the hair and scalp from environmental damage.
