Question and Answer

Q: Can I still dye my grey hair?

A: Yes, you can still dye your grey hair if you prefer a different color. However, it's important to choose the right products and techniques to ensure the best results and minimize damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my grey hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your grey hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I style my grey hair with heat tools?

A: Yes, you can style your grey hair with heat tools, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid excessive heat to prevent damage.

Q: How can I make my grey hair shine?

A: To make your grey hair shine, use a shine-enhancing hair serum or spray and avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip away natural oils.
