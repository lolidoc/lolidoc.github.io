What is a Round Brush?

A round brush is a styling tool that is used to create volume, shape, and smoothness in the hair. It typically has a cylindrical barrel and bristles that are evenly spaced around the barrel. The size of the barrel can vary, with smaller barrels being ideal for shorter hair lengths.

Round brushes are commonly used in conjunction with a blow dryer. The heat from the dryer combined with the brushing action of the round brush helps to shape and style the hair. The bristles of the brush grip the hair, allowing for tension and control during the styling process.
