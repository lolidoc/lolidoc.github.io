Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins Over 60

Table of Contents

Introduction

As we age, our bodies go through various changes, and one of the most common concerns for women over 60 is dealing with a fat face and double chin. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help to minimize these features and enhance your overall appearance. In this article, we will explore different hairstyles that are perfect for those with fat faces and double chins over 60.

The Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is a versatile option that can be tailored to suit your face shape and personal style. For women with fat faces and double chins, a chin-length bob with layers can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, drawing attention away from the chin area.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, opt for long layers that frame your face. This style helps to elongate the face and draw attention away from your double chin. Avoid blunt cuts and instead, ask your stylist to add soft, face-framing layers that will create a slimming effect.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for women with fat faces and double chins. They help to create diagonal lines that soften the face and draw attention away from the chin area. Opt for longer bangs that graze the eyebrows for a flattering look.

Updos and Ponytails

Updos and ponytails are not only stylish but can also help to create a slimmer appearance. Pulling your hair back and away from your face highlights your features and takes attention away from your double chin. Experiment with different updo styles, such as a messy bun or a sleek ponytail, to find the look that suits you best.

Short Pixie Cut

If you're ready to embrace a shorter hairstyle, consider a pixie cut. This bold and chic style accentuates your facial features and draws attention upwards. The short length also helps to minimize the appearance of a double chin. Consult with your stylist to find the right pixie cut that complements your face shape.

Layered Pixie Cut

If you prefer a pixie cut but want to add some volume and movement to your hair, opt for a layered pixie cut. The layers add texture and dimension to your hair, creating a fuller look that can help balance out a round face and double chin. This style is low-maintenance and perfect for those who want a stylish yet effortless look.

Accessorize with Headbands or Scarves

Another way to divert attention from your double chin is by accessorizing with headbands or scarves. These accessories draw attention to the top of your head and away from your chin. Choose headbands or scarves in vibrant colors or patterns to add a pop of style to your overall look.

Consult with a Professional Stylist

When considering a new hairstyle, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist. They can assess your face shape, hair type, and personal style to recommend the best hairstyle for you. Don't be afraid to bring in pictures or discuss any concerns you have about your fat face and double chin.

Confidence is Key

Ultimately, the most important thing is to embrace your natural beauty and feel confident in your own skin. While hairstyles can help to enhance your appearance, true beauty comes from within. Embrace your age and rock any hairstyle with confidence, knowing that you are beautiful just the way you are.

Conclusion

Having a fat face and double chin is a common concern for women over 60, but with the right hairstyle, you can minimize these features and enhance your overall look. Whether you opt for a bob cut, long layers, or a pixie cut, there are plenty of options to choose from. Remember to consult with a professional stylist and choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Embrace your natural beauty and rock any hairstyle with pride!
