A: The frequency of hair color touch-ups depends on the individual. However, most hair colors require touch-ups every 4-6 weeks to maintain their vibrancy.

A: While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it is recommended to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can assess your hair and recommend the best products and techniques for achieving the desired result.
