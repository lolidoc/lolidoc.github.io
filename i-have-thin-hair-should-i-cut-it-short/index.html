Understanding Thin Hair and Its Challenges

Thin hair refers to hair that has a smaller diameter and less volume compared to thick or normal hair. This type of hair can pose challenges when it comes to styling and creating volume. People with thin hair often struggle to achieve a full and voluminous look, which is why cutting it short is often considered as a solution.
