Best Mousse for Fine Short Hair: Tips and Tricks

When using mousse on fine short hair, there are a few tips and tricks that can help you achieve the best results:

- Start with a small amount of mousse and add more if needed. It's easier to add more product than to remove excess product from the hair.

- Apply the mousse to damp hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This helps to create volume and hold throughout the hair.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the mousse evenly throughout the hair. This ensures that the product is evenly distributed and provides maximum volume and hold.

- Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create additional volume and body. Focus on lifting the roots to create lift and height in the hair.
