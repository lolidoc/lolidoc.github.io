How to Style Short Hairstyles for Thin Fine Hair 2021

Styling short hairstyles for thin, fine hair in 2021 is all about adding volume and texture. Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls for added texture.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.
