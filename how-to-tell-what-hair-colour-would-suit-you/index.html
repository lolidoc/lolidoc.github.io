How to Tell What Hair Colour Would Suit You

Introduction

Choosing a hair colour that suits you can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. Your hair colour can dramatically change your appearance, so it's essential to make the right choice. In this article, we will provide you with tips and insights on how to determine what hair colour would suit you best.

Understanding Your Skin Tone

One of the key factors in determining the right hair colour for you is your skin tone. Skin tones can be categorized into warm, cool, or neutral. A quick way to identify your skin tone is by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones, and if they appear blue, you have cool undertones. For those with both green and blue veins, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm undertones, hair colours with golden, honey, or caramel shades would complement your complexion. Shades like golden blonde, copper, or warm brown would suit you best. Avoid cool tones like platinum blonde or ash brown, as they may wash you out.

Cool Skin Tones

Cool undertones go well with hair colours that have ashy or cool undertones. Shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, or burgundy would look great on you. Avoid warm tones like golden or copper, as they may clash with your complexion.

Neutral Skin Tones

Lucky you! With a neutral skin tone, you can pull off a wide range of hair colours. You have the freedom to experiment with warm and cool tones. Consider trying shades like strawberry blonde, chocolate brown, or even a vibrant red.

Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining the most flattering hair colour for you. Certain hair colours can make your eyes pop while others may make them appear dull. If you have blue eyes, shades like platinum blonde or light brown can enhance their brightness. For green or hazel eyes, warm tones like copper or golden brown can bring out the flecks of color. Brown eyes can complement a wide range of hair colours, from dark brown to light blonde.

Consult with a Professional

If you still find it challenging to determine the perfect hair colour for yourself, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They have the expertise and knowledge to assess your features and recommend the most suitable hair colour based on your skin tone, eye color, and personal preferences.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can make a significant difference in your overall appearance. By considering your skin tone, eye color, and seeking professional advice, you can find a hair colour that suits you perfectly. Remember, it's all about feeling confident and comfortable in your own skin.
