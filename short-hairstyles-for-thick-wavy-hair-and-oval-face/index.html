How to Style Short Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair and Oval Face

Styling short hairstyles for thick wavy hair and oval face is relatively simple. Here are a few steps to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Start with clean, dry hair: Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Make sure your hair is completely dry before styling.

2. Apply a styling product: Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair to help enhance your natural waves and add definition. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a diffuser: Attach a diffuser to your hairdryer and use it to dry your hair. This will help enhance your natural waves and add volume to your hair.

4. Finish with a hairspray: Once your hair is dry, finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
