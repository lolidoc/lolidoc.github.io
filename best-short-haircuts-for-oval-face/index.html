Best Short Haircuts for Oval Face

Another great option for oval faces is the bob haircut. This classic hairstyle is timeless and versatile, making it a popular choice for women of all ages. If you're looking for something more daring, consider an undercut hairstyle. This edgy and modern haircut features shaved or closely cropped sides with longer hair on top. For those who prefer a softer and more feminine look, a short layered haircut is a great option. This haircut features layers of varying lengths that add movement and texture to your hair. It can be styled with soft curls or waves for a romantic and playful look. The short layered haircut is versatile and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for women with oval faces.

What is an Oval Face Shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions and gently rounded contours. It is considered to be the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off a wide range of hairstyles and looks. Oval faces are longer than they are wide, with a slightly narrower forehead and jawline. The cheekbones are often the widest part of the face.

Oval faces are well-suited to a variety of haircuts and styles, as the balanced proportions allow for flexibility. This face shape can pull off both short and long hairstyles, making it easy to switch up your look whenever you want. Oval faces are also versatile when it comes to hair texture, as they can accommodate both straight and curly hair.
