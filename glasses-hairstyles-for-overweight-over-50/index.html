Glasses Hairstyles for Overweight Over 50 - Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear bangs with glasses if I'm overweight and over 50?

A: Absolutely! Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and draw attention to your eyes. Opt for softer, side-swept bangs that complement your face shape and glasses frames.

Q: Are there any hairstyles to avoid if I'm overweight and over 50?

A: While there are no hard and fast rules, it's generally best to avoid hairstyles that are too severe or angular, as these can emphasize facial features and make you appear older. Instead, opt for softer, more voluminous styles that enhance your natural beauty.

Q: Can I wear my hair in an updo with glasses?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear your hair in an updo with glasses. Consider styles that frame your face and allow your glasses to sit comfortably on your ears. Avoid styles that pull your hair too tightly, as this can create tension and discomfort.

Q: How can I make my glasses hairstyles last longer?

A: To make your glasses hairstyles last longer, use products that provide hold and texture. Consider using a hairspray or texturizing spray to set your style in place. Additionally, avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause it to become flat or lose its shape.
