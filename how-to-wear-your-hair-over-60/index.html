Question and Answer

Q: Can I still wear long hair over 60?

A: Absolutely! While shorter styles are often recommended for women over 60, there is no age limit on long hair. If you feel confident and beautiful with long hair, go for it!

Q: How can I add volume to my thinning hair?

A: Adding layers and using volumizing products can help create the illusion of fuller hair. Additionally, blow-drying your hair upside down can add lift and volume at the roots.

Q: Should I dye my gray hair or embrace it?

A: The choice is entirely up to you! Some women choose to embrace their natural gray hair as a sign of wisdom and aging gracefully, while others prefer to cover it up with hair dye. Consider what makes you feel most confident and beautiful.

Q: What if I have a round face?

A: If you have a round face, opt for hairstyles that create height and elongate your face. Styles with volume at the crown or side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face shape.
