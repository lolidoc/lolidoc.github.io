How to Style Short Pixie Hairstyles for Fine Hair over 50

Styling short pixie hairstyles for fine hair over 50 is simple and easy. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your locks. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of texturizing product to add volume and hold.

2. Use a round brush or your fingers to style your hair.

If you prefer a sleek and polished look, use a round brush to blow dry your hair. For a messy and textured look, simply use your fingers to tousle and style your hair.

3. Finish with a texturizing spray or hairspray.

To add extra volume and hold to your hairstyle, mist your hair with a texturizing spray or hairspray. This will help your hair stay in place throughout the day and maintain its shape.
