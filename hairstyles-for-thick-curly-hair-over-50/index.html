How to Style Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Styling hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50 can be simple and effortless. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the desired look:

  1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or gel to damp hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting, scrunching your curls as you go.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose curls or add more texture.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place.
