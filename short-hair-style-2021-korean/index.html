Short Hair Style 2021 Korean: How to Style

Styling short hair style 2021 Korean is relatively easy and requires minimal effort. Here are some tips to help you achieve the desired look:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or a styling cream, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume and shape. Blow dry your hair in the desired direction, whether it's straight back or to the side.
  4. If you want a sleek and polished look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. For a more textured and tousled look, use a curling iron or a wand to create loose waves.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray or a styling spray to set the style and add shine.

Remember to experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you. Don't be afraid to get creative and try new looks.
