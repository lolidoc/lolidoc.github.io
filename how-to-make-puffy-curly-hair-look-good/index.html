How To Make Puffy Curly Hair Look Good

Written by Vannesa Jul 05, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having puffy curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can make it look amazing. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks to help you achieve beautiful, well-defined curls that will make heads turn. Whether you have naturally curly hair or want to create curls with styling tools, we've got you covered.

1. Start with the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Choosing the right shampoo and conditioner is crucial for managing puffy curly hair. Look for products that are specifically formulated for curly hair, as they help to enhance and define your curls. Avoid products that contain sulfates, as they can strip the hair of its natural oils and lead to frizz.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize and detangle your curls. This will make your hair more manageable and prevent frizz. Use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to distribute the product evenly through your hair.

3. Use a Diffuser When Blow-Drying

If you prefer to blow-dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment to minimize frizz and enhance your curls. The diffuser helps to distribute the airflow evenly, reducing heat damage and maintaining the shape of your curls. Start with low heat and speed settings, and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser for best results.

4. Embrace the Power of Hair Gel

Hair gel is a great product to help tame puffy curly hair. Apply a small amount of gel to your damp hair, focusing on the ends and areas prone to frizz. Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation and let it air dry or use a diffuser to set the style.

5. Avoid Brushing or Combing Dry Hair

Brushing or combing dry hair can lead to frizz and disrupt the natural curl pattern. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet or damp. This will help to preserve the shape of your curls and prevent them from becoming puffy.

6. Sleep on Silk or Satin Pillowcases

Cotton pillowcases can create friction and cause your curls to become frizzy overnight. Switch to silk or satin pillowcases, as they are smoother and gentler on your hair. Alternatively, you can wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf before going to bed.

7. Refresh Your Curls with a Water-Based Spray

If your curls start to lose their definition throughout the day, refresh them with a water-based spray. Simply mist your hair lightly and scrunch it to revive the curls. Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

8. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don't be afraid to try different hairstyles to switch up your look. From updos to braids and ponytails, there are plenty of styles that can help you manage puffy curly hair while looking stylish. Explore tutorials online or consult with a hairstylist for inspiration.

9. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Excessive heat can damage your curls and make them appear puffy. Whenever you use heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, apply a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Opt for lower heat settings and use these tools sparingly.

10. Embrace Natural Ingredients

Consider incorporating natural ingredients into your hair care routine. For example, coconut oil can help moisturize and define your curls, while aloe vera gel can provide hold without weighing your hair down. Experiment with different natural products to find what works best for your hair.

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks, you can make your puffy curly hair look good and feel confident in your natural curls. Remember, it's all about finding the right products and techniques that work for you. Embrace your curls, and let them shine!
