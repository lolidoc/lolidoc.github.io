Many African American women struggle to find a hairstylist who understands their unique hair texture and can create the desired look. This often leads to frustration and disappointment. By learning how to cut your own hair, you can take control of your style and achieve the look you've always wanted.

To cut your own hair short African American style, you will need a few essential tools. These include a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. Additionally, having a handheld mirror will be helpful to see the back of your head while cutting.

Now, let's get into the step-by-step process of cutting your own hair short African American style. First, start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a clean cut. Once your hair is clean and damp, detangle it using a wide-tooth comb.

Next, part your hair into small sections using the hair clips. Start with the back sections and work your way towards the front. This will help you maintain control and ensure an even cut. Take one section at a time and comb it straight down.

Using your comb as a guide, determine the desired length you want to cut. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers at the desired length and use the scissors to trim the ends. Remember to cut small amounts at a time to avoid any mistakes or unevenness.

Continue this process for each section of your hair, making sure to check the length and symmetry as you go. Once you have finished cutting, comb through your hair to ensure there are no tangles or uneven sections. Style your hair as desired and voila! You have successfully cut your own hair short African American style.
