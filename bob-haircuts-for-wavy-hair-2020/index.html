FAQs About Bob Haircuts for Wavy Hair 2020

1. Can I get a bob haircut if I have tight curls?

Yes, you can still get a bob haircut if you have tight curls. Just make sure to choose a bob style that works with your curl pattern and consult with a hairstylist who is experienced with curly hair.

2. Will a bob haircut make my hair look shorter?

A bob haircut can make your hair look slightly shorter, especially if you opt for a shorter length. However, it can also make your hair look thicker and more voluminous, so it's a trade-off.

3. How often do I need to get a bob haircut?

How often you need to get a bob haircut will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it. On average, most people get their hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain their desired length and shape.

4. Can I still style my bob haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can still style your bob haircut in different ways. You can curl it, straighten it, or add accessories to change up your look. The key is to find a style that works with your natural texture and enhances your waves.
