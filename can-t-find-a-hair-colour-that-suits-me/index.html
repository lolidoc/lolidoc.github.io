Understanding Your Undertone and Skin Tone

To understand your undertone, you can look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have a cool undertone. If your veins appear green, you have a warm undertone. If your veins appear blue-green, you have a neutral undertone.

When determining your skin tone, consider factors such as how your skin reacts to the sun. Fair skin burns easily and rarely tans, while medium skin tans gradually and may experience some burning. Dark skin rarely burns and tans easily.
