Understanding the Rectangle Face Shape

A rectangle face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a straight hairline, and equal width across the forehead, cheeks, and jaw. It is often longer than it is wide, with angular features. The goal when choosing a haircut for a rectangle face shape is to soften these angular features and create balance.
