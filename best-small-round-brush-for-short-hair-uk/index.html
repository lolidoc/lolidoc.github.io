Common Questions About the Best Small Round Brush for Short Hair in the UK

Q: Can I use a small round brush on long hair?

A: While small round brushes are primarily designed for short hair, they can also be used on longer hair lengths. However, for longer hair, you may need a larger barrel size to achieve the desired volume and control.

Q: How often should I clean my small round brush?

A: It is recommended to clean your small round brush at least once a month to remove any build-up of styling products or oils. You can simply remove any hair from the brush and soak it in warm, soapy water for a few minutes before rinsing and air-drying.

Q: Can I use a small round brush on wet hair?

A: It is not recommended to use a small round brush on wet hair, as wet hair is more prone to breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or a brush specifically designed for wet hair to detangle and style.

Q: Can I use a small round brush with a hairdryer?

A: Yes, small round brushes are often used with a hairdryer to achieve volume and shape. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the dryer at a safe distance from your hair to avoid heat damage.
