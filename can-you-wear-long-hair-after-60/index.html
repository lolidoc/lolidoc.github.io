Tips for Wearing Long Hair After 60

1. Consider your face shape: Choose a long hairstyle that flatters your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, opt for layers or face-framing pieces to create the illusion of length. If you have a square face, soft layers or side-swept bangs can soften your features.

2. Embrace your natural texture: Work with your hair's natural texture instead of fighting against it. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are styling techniques and products that can enhance your natural texture and make your long hair look its best.

3. Take care of your hair: As we age, our hair can become more brittle and prone to breakage. Use gentle shampoos and conditioners that nourish and hydrate your hair. Regular trims can also help to prevent split ends and keep your long hair looking healthy and vibrant.
