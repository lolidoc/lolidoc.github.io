Short Curly Hairstyles for 65 Year-Old Woman: Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style and maintain your short curly hair:

Short Curly Hairstyles for 65 Year-Old Woman: How to Style

Styling short curly hair can be quick and easy. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your short curly hair:

1. Start with clean, dry hair: Wash and condition your hair with products specifically designed for curly hair.

2. Apply styling products: Apply a lightweight mousse or curl-enhancing cream to your damp hair to define your curls and add moisture.

3. Use a diffuser attachment: Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and use it on low heat to gently dry your hair while enhancing your curls.

4. Define your curls: Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate and define your curls. You can also use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need extra definition.

5. Finish with a hairspray: Spray a flexible hold hairspray all over your hair to set your curls and reduce frizz.
