What is the Best Swimsuit Style for Over 60?

The best swimsuit style for women over 60 is one that provides ample coverage, support, and comfort. Here are some key features to look for when shopping for a swimsuit:

Look for swimsuits with built-in cups or underwire for extra support and lift. This will help enhance your bust and provide a more flattering shape.

2. Tummy Control

If you're concerned about your midsection, opt for swimsuits with tummy control panels. These panels help smooth out any lumps or bulges, giving you a more streamlined silhouette.

3. Adjustable Straps

Adjustable straps are essential for ensuring a proper fit. They allow you to customize the level of support and comfort, making your swimsuit feel tailored to your body.

4. Ruching and Draping

Swimsuits with ruching and draping are great for camouflaging any problem areas and creating a slimming effect. Look for styles that feature these details around the waist or hips.
