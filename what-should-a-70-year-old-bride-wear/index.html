What Should A 70 Year Old Bride Wear

Introduction

Getting married is a joyous occasion at any age, and for a 70-year-old bride, it can be even more special. As a mature bride, you have years of wisdom and experience behind you, and your wedding day is an opportunity to celebrate that. When it comes to choosing your wedding attire, there are no hard and fast rules. The most important thing is to feel comfortable, confident, and beautiful on your big day. In this article, we will explore some ideas and tips on what a 70-year-old bride can wear to look and feel her best.

Consider Your Personal Style

When choosing your wedding dress, it's essential to consider your personal style. Just because you're a mature bride doesn't mean you have to conform to traditional expectations. If you love vibrant colors, don't be afraid to wear a colorful wedding gown. If you prefer a more understated look, opt for a classic white or ivory dress. Focus on finding a style that reflects your personality and makes you feel like the best version of yourself.

Comfort is Key

On your wedding day, comfort should be a top priority. You'll be spending hours in your dress, so make sure it fits well and allows you to move freely. Consider lightweight fabrics that won't weigh you down or make you feel too hot. Look for dresses with stretch or give, as they can provide a more comfortable fit. Don't forget about your shoes too! Opt for low-heeled or flat shoes that will keep you comfortable throughout the day.

Focus on Flattering Silhouettes

Choosing a dress with a flattering silhouette is important at any age. A-line or empire waist dresses can be particularly flattering for mature brides as they accentuate the waist and create a feminine shape. Avoid overly tight or revealing dresses if they don't make you feel comfortable. Instead, opt for styles that highlight your best features and make you feel confident.

Consider Sleeves or Coverage

If you prefer to have some coverage on your arms or shoulders, consider choosing a dress with sleeves or adding a bolero or jacket. There are many elegant and stylish options available that can provide the coverage you desire without compromising on style. Lace sleeves, three-quarter length sleeves, or even a cape can add a touch of sophistication to your wedding look.

Accessorize Thoughtfully

Accessories can elevate your wedding look and add a personal touch. Opt for accessories that complement your dress and overall style. Consider wearing a statement necklace or a pair of statement earrings to add some glamour. A delicate veil or a fascinator can also make a beautiful addition to your bridal ensemble. Don't forget to choose comfortable and stylish undergarments that provide support and ensure a smooth silhouette.

Consider Alternatives to Traditional Dresses

If wearing a traditional wedding dress doesn't resonate with you, don't be afraid to explore alternative options. Many mature brides opt for chic pant suits or elegant jumpsuits that offer comfort and style. These options can be just as beautiful and sophisticated as a traditional gown. Explore different styles and find the one that makes you feel like the radiant bride you are.

Consult with a Professional

When in doubt, seek the advice of a professional. Bridal consultants or stylists can help you navigate the world of wedding fashion and find the perfect dress for your special day. They have experience working with brides of all ages and can offer valuable insights and recommendations. Don't be afraid to ask for help and trust their expertise.

Conclusion

As a 70-year-old bride, your wedding day is a celebration of love, life, and the experiences that have shaped you. When choosing your wedding attire, focus on what makes you feel comfortable, confident, and beautiful. Consider your personal style, prioritize comfort, and choose a flattering silhouette. Don't be afraid to explore alternative options and consult with professionals for guidance. Remember, it's your day, and you deserve to feel like a radiant bride at any age.
