Styling Very Short Natural Black Hair In 2023

Short natural black hair is beautiful versatile and easy to maintain Whether you are just starting your natural hair journey or have been rocking your natural hair for years there are countless styling options available to you In this article we will explore some trendy and creative ways to style very short natural black hair in 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

Short natural black hair is beautiful, versatile, and easy to maintain. Whether you are just starting your natural hair journey or have been rocking your natural hair for years, there are countless styling options available to you. In this article, we will explore some trendy and creative ways to style very short natural black hair in 2023.

1. The Tapered Cut

The tapered cut is a popular choice for short natural hair. This style involves shaving the sides and back of the hair while leaving the top slightly longer. It creates a stylish and edgy look that requires minimal daily maintenance. You can enhance the style with a line-up or add some color to make it even more unique.

2. Finger Coils

Finger coils are a great way to add definition and texture to your short natural hair. This style involves twisting small sections of hair around your finger to create coils. It works well on all hair types and can last for several days with the right maintenance. To keep your coils looking fresh, moisturize your hair regularly and protect them while sleeping.

3. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a classic hairstyle that can be achieved on very short natural hair. This style involves dividing your hair into small sections and twisting them tightly into small knots. Bantu knots can be worn as a cute and trendy look or unraveled for beautiful curls. To ensure your knots stay in place, use bobby pins or small hair bands.

4. Frohawk

The frohawk is a bold and eye-catching style that combines the elegance of an updo with the fierceness of an afro. To achieve this style, create two or three rows of small cornrows on the sides of your head, leaving the center hair free. Fluff up the center hair to create a voluminous and stylish frohawk. You can also add accessories like hairpins or headbands to enhance the overall look.

5. Twist-Out

A twist-out is a versatile style that can be done on very short natural hair. This style involves twisting small sections of hair and allowing them to set overnight. In the morning, unravel the twists for beautiful and defined curls. To maintain your twist-out, moisturize your hair regularly and protect it while sleeping with a satin bonnet or pillowcase.

6. Mohawk

A mohawk is a bold and edgy style that can be achieved on very short natural hair. This style involves shaving the sides of your head and leaving the center hair longer. You can enhance the look by adding twists, braids, or even color to the center hair. The mohawk allows you to express your individuality and make a statement.

7. Head Wraps

Head wraps are a stylish and practical way to accessorize your short natural hair. They come in various colors, patterns, and fabrics, allowing you to create different looks for different occasions. Head wraps also protect your hair from harsh weather conditions and help retain moisture. Simply tie the head wrap around your head, leaving the ends loose or tucked in.

8. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a chic and low-maintenance style for very short natural hair. This style involves cutting your hair short all around, giving you a fresh and youthful look. You can add texture and dimension to the pixie cut by using styling products or adding highlights. With a pixie cut, you can easily transition from a casual day look to an elegant evening look.

9. Afro Puffs

Afro puffs are a cute and versatile style for short natural hair. This style involves gathering your hair into two or more puffs on top of your head. You can create different sizes and heights of puffs to change up your look. Afro puffs are great for everyday wear or special occasions. Add some hair accessories like bows or clips to make your puffs even more stylish.

10. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a bold and daring choice for very short natural hair. This style involves cutting your hair very short all around, giving you a sleek and minimalist look. The buzz cut requires minimal maintenance and can be a liberating experience. Embrace your natural beauty and rock this powerful and confident style.

Conclusion

Styling very short natural black hair in 2023 opens up a world of possibilities. Whether you choose a tapered cut, finger coils, Bantu knots, a frohawk, a twist-out, a mohawk, head wraps, a pixie cut, afro puffs, or a buzz cut, remember to embrace your unique beauty and have fun experimenting with different styles. Short natural hair is a statement of confidence and individuality, and these styling options allow you to express yourself authentically.
