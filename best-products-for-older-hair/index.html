How to Best Products for Older Hair

To get the most out of your best products for older hair, follow these steps:

2. Apply a nourishing conditioner

After shampooing, apply a generous amount of conditioner to your hair. Focus on the mid-lengths and ends, where the hair tends to be drier. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse thoroughly.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, treat your hair to a deep conditioning treatment. Apply a hair mask or treatment to your hair, then cover it with a shower cap or towel. Leave the treatment on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse thoroughly.
