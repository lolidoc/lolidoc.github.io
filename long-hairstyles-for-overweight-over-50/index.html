Tips for Long Hairstyles for Overweight Women over 50

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling long hairstyles for overweight women over 50:

1. Use Volumizing Products:

To add volume and lift to your hair, use volumizing products such as mousse or root-lifting sprays. Apply these products to the roots of your hair and blow-dry with a round brush for added volume.

2. Embrace Layers:

Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, making it appear fuller and more voluminous. Ask your stylist for long layers that frame your face and enhance your features.

3. Experiment with Curls or Waves:

Curls or waves can add instant volume and texture to your hair. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose curls or waves, and then tousle with your fingers for a more natural look.

4. Consider a Side Part:

A side part can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Experiment with different parting styles to see which one flatters your face shape the most.
