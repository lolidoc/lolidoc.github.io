Question and Answer

Q: Can short hairstyles flatter a round chubby face?

A: Yes, short hairstyles can flatter a round chubby face. However, it's essential to choose a style that adds height and volume on top to create the illusion of length.

Q: Are there any specific hairstyles that round-faced women should avoid?

A: Round-faced women should avoid hairstyles that add width to their face shape, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs. These styles can make the face appear rounder.

Q: How can I determine my face shape?

A: To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Step back and observe the shape you've traced. Common face shapes include oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Q: Can bangs flatter a round chubby face?

A: Bangs can flatter a round chubby face if they are swept to the side or are soft and wispy. Avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across, as they can make the face shape appear rounder.
