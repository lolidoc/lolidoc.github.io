Fun Facts About Grey Hair

Did you know that grey hair is caused by a decrease in melanin production? Melanin is the pigment that gives your hair its color. As you age, your body produces less melanin, leading to the appearance of grey or white hair.

Another interesting fact is that genetics play a significant role in when and how quickly your hair turns grey. If your parents or grandparents had grey hair at an early age, there's a higher chance that you will too.

Grey hair can also be influenced by lifestyle factors such as stress, smoking, and certain medical conditions. Managing stress levels and adopting a healthy lifestyle can help slow down the greying process.
