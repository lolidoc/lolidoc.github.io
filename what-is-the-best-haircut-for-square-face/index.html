Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear short hair with a square face?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear short hair with a square face. Just make sure to choose a style that adds softness and movement to your features, such as a textured pixie cut or a bob with layers.

Q: Are bangs suitable for a square face?

A: Bangs can be a great option for square faces, as they help to balance out your strong jawline and draw attention to your eyes. Side-swept bangs are especially flattering for this face shape.

Q: How do I know if I have a square face?

A: Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline and equal width and length. If your forehead, cheeks, and jawline are all about the same width, you likely have a square face.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a square face. However, adding some texture or waves can help to soften your features and create a more balanced look.
