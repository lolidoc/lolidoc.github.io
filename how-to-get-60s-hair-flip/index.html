Welcome to our blog post about how to get the iconic 60s hair flip! If you're looking to recreate the glamorous hairstyles of the past, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll share tips, tricks, and techniques to help you achieve the perfect 60s hair flip look.

Many people struggle with creating the voluminous and bouncy hair flip that was so popular in the 60s. Achieving this style can be a challenge, especially if you have fine or straight hair. But fear not, with the right tools and techniques, anyone can rock the 60s hair flip!

To get started, you'll need a few key items: a curling iron, a teasing comb, hairspray, and some bobby pins. These tools will help you create volume, curls, and hold for your hair flip. Now let's dive into the step-by-step process of achieving the perfect 60s hair flip.

To begin, start by curling your hair with a curling iron. This will create the foundation for your hair flip. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the barrel of the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 1: Curl your hair

Once your hair is fully curled, use a teasing comb to backcomb the roots of your hair. This will create added volume and texture, making it easier to achieve the desired hair flip. Gently tease the roots of your hair by combing downward towards the scalp.

Step 2: Backcomb the roots

Next, take a section of hair from the front of your head and twist it away from your face. Secure the twisted section with a bobby pin at the back of your head. Repeat this process on the other side of your head, making sure the twists are symmetrical.

Step 3: Twist and secure the front sections

Now it's time to create the actual hair flip. Take the remaining hair at the back of your head and gently brush it back towards the crown of your head. Use your fingers or a comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles. Once the hair is smooth, use a bobby pin to secure it in place.

Step 4: Create the hair flip

Finally, finish off your 60s hair flip by spraying it with a generous amount of hairspray. This will help hold the style in place and give it a polished look. Use your fingers to gently tousle and shape the hair flip to your liking.

Step 5: Set with hairspray

And there you have it - the perfect 60s hair flip! With a little practice and the right techniques, you can achieve this iconic hairstyle. Now go rock that retro look and turn heads wherever you go!

In conclusion, achieving the 60s hair flip is all about creating volume, curls, and a perfect hair flip shape. By following the steps outlined in this article, you'll be able to recreate this iconic hairstyle and channel your inner vintage glamour. So grab your tools and get ready to rock the 60s hair flip!
