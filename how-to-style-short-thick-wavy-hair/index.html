Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your short thick wavy hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss different styling techniques and tips specifically tailored for short thick wavy hair. Whether you're looking for a casual everyday look or a glamorous style for a special occasion, we've got you covered.

Having short thick wavy hair can be both a blessing and a challenge. While your hair has natural volume and texture, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Many people with short thick wavy hair struggle with frizz, unruly curls, and finding a hairstyle that flatters their face shape. But with the right techniques and products, you can embrace your natural waves and create stunning hairstyles that suit your personal style.

When it comes to styling short thick wavy hair, the key is to enhance your natural texture while keeping it under control. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your waves hydrated and reduce frizz. Once your hair is towel-dried, apply a curl-enhancing mousse or cream to define your waves and add hold. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently comb through your hair and distribute the product evenly.

Now that you have the basics down, let's dive into some specific styling techniques for short thick wavy hair:

1. Beachy Waves

To achieve effortless beachy waves, start by dividing your hair into sections. Take a curling wand or a flat iron and wrap each section of hair around the barrel, leaving the ends out for a more relaxed look. Hold for a few seconds and then release. Once all sections are curled, run your fingers through your hair to break up the waves and create a more natural look. Finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a versatile and easy option for short thick wavy hair. Simply take the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or some bobby pins. Leave some face-framing pieces out for a softer look. This style is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a great way to tame your waves while still looking chic. Start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Twist the ponytail around the base to create a bun and secure with bobby pins. Gently pull out some pieces from the bun to create a messy, undone look. Finish with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

4. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for short thick wavy hair. This short hairstyle allows your natural waves to shine while keeping your hair easy to manage. Talk to your hairstylist to find the perfect pixie cut that suits your face shape and personal style.

Conclusion

Styling short thick wavy hair doesn't have to be a challenge. By embracing your natural texture and using the right techniques and products, you can create stunning hairstyles that suit your personal style. Whether you're going for beachy waves, a half-up half-down look, a messy bun, or a pixie cut, the key is to enhance your natural waves while keeping your hair under control. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
