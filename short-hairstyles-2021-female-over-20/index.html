Recommendation of Short Hairstyles 2021 Female Over 20

If you're considering a short hairstyle for 2021, here are some recommendations to inspire your next look:

1. Sleek Bob

A sleek bob is a classic and timeless choice that suits women of all ages. This hairstyle is characterized by its clean lines and polished finish. It can be worn straight or with a slight wave for added texture. The sleek bob is versatile and can be dressed up or down for any occasion.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring choice that exudes confidence. This hairstyle is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. It can be styled in various ways, such as spiked up for a edgy look or swept to the side for a softer vibe. The pixie cut is perfect for those who want to make a statement and stand out from the crowd.

3. Shag

The shag is a trendy and modern hairstyle that is making a comeback in 2021. This cut features choppy layers and a textured finish, giving it a relaxed and effortless look. The shag can be worn with or without bangs, depending on your preference. It is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type.

4. Undercut

For those who want to add an element of edge to their look, an undercut is a great option. This hairstyle involves shaving or closely trimming the hair on one side or the back of the head, while leaving the rest of the hair longer. The undercut can be hidden when the hair is down or showcased with an updo or half-up style. It adds a cool and edgy vibe to any short hairstyle.

Tips for Short Hairstyles 2021 Female Over 20

If you're considering a short hairstyle for 2021, here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consult with a Hairstylist

Before making the chop, schedule a consultation with a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts. They can assess your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle to recommend the best short hairstyle for you.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Short hairstyles can work for all hair types, but it's important to consider your natural texture. If you have curly or wavy hair, a shorter length may require more styling and maintenance to achieve your desired look.

3. Experiment with Styling Products

Invest in quality styling products that are designed for short hair. This can include texturizing sprays, pomades, and hair waxes. These products can help you achieve the desired shape, texture, and hold for your short hairstyle.

4. Embrace Regular Trims

To maintain the shape and style of your short haircut, regular trims are essential. Aim to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your short hairstyle looking fresh and healthy.
