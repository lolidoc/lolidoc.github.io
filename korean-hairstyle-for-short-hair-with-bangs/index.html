Korean Hairstyle for Short Hair with Bangs: How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the Korean hairstyle for short hair with bangs, follow these simple steps:

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products suited for your hair type.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

3. Use a round brush and blow dry your hair, focusing on adding volume and smoothing out any frizz.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and style to your bangs. You can opt for a straight or wavy look, depending on your preference.

5. Finish off the look with a hairspray to hold everything in place.
