How to Blunt Cut Long Hair Yourself - 2023

Introduction

Blunt cut hairstyles have become increasingly popular in recent years. Not only do they give a sleek and sophisticated look, but they are also relatively easy to achieve at home. In this tutorial, we will guide you step by step on how to blunt cut long hair yourself in the comfort of your own home.

What You Will Need

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools and materials ready:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A handheld mirror
  • A towel or cape to protect your clothes

Step 1: Preparation

Start by washing and drying your hair. It's important to work with clean and dry hair to ensure accurate cutting. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use clips to separate your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair down the middle, creating two equal sections. Then, clip the upper section of your hair on top of your head to keep it out of the way while you work on the bottom section.

Step 3: Cut the First Section

Release the bottom section of your hair and comb it straight down. Take a small subsection of your hair and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Slide your fingers down to the desired length and make a straight cut. Repeat this process for the rest of the bottom section.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After cutting the first section, release the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Compare the length of the top section with the cut length of the bottom section. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure an even and symmetrical look.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Once you are satisfied with the length, remove the clips and shake out your hair to see the final result. If you notice any uneven sections, you can make minor adjustments by following the same cutting technique.

Step 6: Styling

Blunt cut long hair looks great when styled straight or with loose waves. You can use a straightener or a curling iron to achieve your desired look. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray for added hold and shine.

Step 7: Maintenance

To maintain your blunt cut long hair, schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help prevent split ends and maintain the overall shape of your haircut.

Conclusion

By following these simple steps, you can achieve a beautiful blunt cut on your long hair without the need to visit a salon. Remember to take your time and be cautious while cutting, as it's always better to start with less and trim more if needed. With practice, you'll become more confident in cutting your own hair and can enjoy the benefits of a stylish blunt cut from the comfort of your own home.
