Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the best medium length hair options that flatter round faces. Whether you want to add volume, slim down your face, or enhance your natural features, we have got you covered.

Having a round face can sometimes be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. Many people with round faces struggle with wanting to create the illusion of length and slimness. They often find it difficult to find a hairstyle that complements their face shape without making it look rounder.

But fear not! There are plenty of medium length hairstyles that are perfect for round faces. These hairstyles can help to elongate your face and create the appearance of more defined cheekbones. So, let's dive in and discover the best medium length hair options for round faces.

Best Medium Length Hair for Round Faces: Flattering and Stylish

When it comes to finding the best medium length hair for round faces, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, look for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head. This will help to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Another important factor to consider is the length of the hair. Medium length hairstyles are ideal for round faces because they hit the sweet spot between short and long. They provide enough length to create a slimming effect without overwhelming the face.

Additionally, hairstyles with layers can be incredibly flattering for round faces. Layers create movement and texture, which can help to break up the roundness of the face. They also add volume and body to the hair, giving it a more dynamic and interesting look.

Now that we have covered the basics, let's explore some specific medium length hairstyles that are perfect for round faces.

The Bob: A Classic Choice

The bob is a timeless hairstyle that looks great on almost everyone, including those with round faces. For a round face, opt for a medium length bob that falls just below the chin. This length will help to elongate your face while still framing your features.

Add some layers to your bob to create movement and texture. This will give your hair more body and prevent it from looking flat. You can also try adding some side-swept bangs to further slim down your face.

The Long Pixie: Edgy and Chic

The long pixie is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that works well for round faces. This hairstyle features short sides and a longer top, creating a bold and stylish look. The longer top adds height and volume to the crown of your head, giving the illusion of a longer face shape.

Ask your stylist to add some layers to your long pixie to give it more movement and texture. This will prevent your hair from looking flat and add a touch of femininity to the overall look.

The Shag: Effortlessly Cool

The shag is a versatile and effortlessly cool hairstyle that is perfect for round faces. This hairstyle features choppy layers and a lot of texture, creating a messy and undone look. The shag works well with medium length hair, as it adds movement and body to your locks.

When opting for a shag hairstyle, make sure to keep the layers longer at the front to frame your face. This will help to elongate your features and create the appearance of a more oval face shape.

The Lob: A Modern Classic

The lob, or long bob, is a modern and chic hairstyle that is universally flattering. This hairstyle falls just above the shoulders and is slightly longer in the front, creating a soft and feminine look.

For a round face, opt for a lob that is slightly longer in the front and shorter at the back. This will help to create the illusion of length and slimness. Adding some layers to your lob will also give it more movement and prevent it from looking heavy.

Conclusion

Finding the perfect medium length hair for a round face can be a challenge, but with the right knowledge and inspiration, it is definitely achievable. Whether you opt for a bob, a long pixie, a shag, or a lob, the key is to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of your head while still framing your features.

Experiment with different lengths, textures, and layers to find the hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember, the best medium length hair for round faces is the one that makes you feel like the best version of yourself!

So go ahead, embrace your round face and rock that medium length hair with pride!
