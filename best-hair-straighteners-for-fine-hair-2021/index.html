Tips for Using Hair Straighteners for Fine Hair

When using a hair straightener for fine hair, it's important to take extra care to prevent damage. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  1. Use a heat protectant spray before straightening your hair.
  2. Start with the lowest heat setting and gradually increase if needed.
  3. Section your hair and straighten small sections at a time.
  4. Avoid going over the same section of hair multiple times.
