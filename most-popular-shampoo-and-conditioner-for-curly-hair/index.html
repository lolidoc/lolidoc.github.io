Tips for Using the Most Popular Shampoo and Conditioner for Curly Hair

To get the most out of the most popular shampoo and conditioner for curly hair, follow these tips:

1. Use a generous amount of conditioner

Curly hair tends to be drier, so don't be afraid to use a generous amount of conditioner to provide the necessary hydration and moisture.

2. Detangle gently

Before rinsing out the conditioner, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently detangle your curls, starting from the ends and working your way up.

3. Scrunch, don't rub

When drying your hair, avoid rubbing it with a towel as this can cause frizz. Instead, gently scrunch your hair with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to remove excess water.

4. Style with care

After washing and conditioning, apply a curl-enhancing product or gel to define your curls. Use your fingers or a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting to dry your hair and enhance your curls' shape.
