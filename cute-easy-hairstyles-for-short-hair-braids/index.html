Cute Easy Hairstyles For Short Hair Braids

Written by Alexa Dec 27, 2022 · 4 min read

Short hair can be just as versatile and stylish as long hair especially when it comes to braided hairstyles If you have short hair and are looking for some cute and easy hairstyles we ve got you covered In this article we will share some trendy and simple braided hairstyles that are perfect for short hair in 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction

Short hair can be just as versatile and stylish as long hair, especially when it comes to braided hairstyles. If you have short hair and are looking for some cute and easy hairstyles, we've got you covered! In this article, we will share some trendy and simple braided hairstyles that are perfect for short hair in 2023.

The Classic French Braid

The French braid is a timeless hairstyle that works well for short hair too. Start by dividing your hair into three sections at the top of your head. Cross the right section over the middle, then the left section over the new middle section. Continue this pattern, adding small sections of hair to each side as you go. Secure the braid with a small elastic and you're good to go!

The Fishtail Braid

If you're looking for a more intricate braided hairstyle, the fishtail braid is a great option. Start by dividing your hair into two sections. Take a small piece of hair from the outer edge of the left section and cross it over to join the right section. Repeat this process on the other side, crossing a small piece of hair from the outer edge of the right section to the left. Continue alternating sides until you reach the end of your hair and secure with an elastic.

The Dutch Braid

The Dutch braid is similar to the French braid, but instead of crossing the sections over the middle, you cross them under. This creates a braid that appears to be raised from the scalp. Start by dividing your hair into three sections at the top of your head. Cross the right section under the middle, then the left section under the new middle section. Continue this pattern, adding small sections of hair to each side as you go. Secure the braid with a small elastic.

The Halo Braid

The halo braid is a beautiful and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for special occasions. Start by dividing your hair into two sections, one on each side of your head. Braid each section into a regular three-strand braid and secure with elastics. Then, take one braid and wrap it around the top of your head, pinning it in place with bobby pins. Repeat with the other braid, crossing it over the first braid. Tuck the ends of the braids under each other and secure with more bobby pins.

The Braided Updo

If you're looking for a stylish updo for your short hair, a braided updo is a great option. Start by creating a small French braid at the front of your hair, starting from one side and working towards the other. Once you reach the back of your head, secure the braid with an elastic. Then, gather the rest of your hair into a low ponytail and twist it up into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and you have a chic braided updo for short hair!

Accessorize with Ribbons or Headbands

Another cute and easy way to style your short hair with braids is to accessorize with ribbons or headbands. Start by braiding a small section of your hair and securing it with an elastic. Then, tie a ribbon around the base of the braid or place a headband over the top of your head, positioning it just above the braid. This simple addition can instantly elevate your hairstyle and make it look more playful and stylish.

Conclusion

Short hair doesn't have to limit your hairstyle options. With these cute and easy braided hairstyles, you can rock a new look every day. Whether you prefer a classic French braid, an intricate fishtail braid, or an elegant braided updo, there's a style for everyone. Experiment with different braiding techniques and don't forget to accessorize to add your personal touch. Have fun and embrace the versatility of your short hair!
