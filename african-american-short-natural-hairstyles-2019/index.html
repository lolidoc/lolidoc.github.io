African American Short Natural Hairstyles 2019

Introduction

African American women have been embracing their natural hair texture more and more in recent years. Short natural hairstyles have become increasingly popular among African American women, as they offer versatility, low maintenance, and a chance to showcase their unique beauty. In this article, we will explore some of the hottest African American short natural hairstyles in 2019.

The Tapered Cut

The tapered cut is a classic short natural hairstyle that never goes out of style. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving more length on top. This style can be customized to suit your face shape and preference, whether you want a dramatic look or a more subtle one. It's a great option for those who want to showcase their natural curls or coils.

The Afro

The afro is a bold and iconic hairstyle that celebrates natural hair in all its glory. It involves growing out your hair to create a voluminous and round shape. With the right care and products, you can achieve a full and healthy-looking afro. This style is perfect for those who want to make a statement and embrace their natural texture.

Braided Protective Styles

Braided protective styles are not only trendy but also help to protect your natural hair from damage. These styles involve braiding the hair close to the scalp in various patterns and designs. They can be worn for several weeks, allowing your hair to grow and rest. From box braids to cornrows, there are endless options to choose from.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a chic and sophisticated short hairstyle that works well on African American women. This style involves cutting the hair short all over, creating a sleek and polished look. It's a low maintenance option that can be easily styled with the right products. The pixie cut is perfect for those who want a short and edgy hairstyle.

Twist Outs

Twist outs are a popular styling option for short natural hair. This technique involves twisting small sections of hair and allowing them to dry before unraveling them. The result is defined and bouncy curls that can last for several days. Twist outs are a great way to enhance your natural texture and add volume to your short hair.

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that is gaining popularity among African American women. This style involves shaving the hair very short or even completely bald. It's a statement-making look that exudes confidence and individuality. The buzz cut requires minimal maintenance and is a great option for those who want to embrace a low maintenance and bold style.

The Bob

The bob is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that suits women of all ages and hair types. For African American women with short natural hair, the bob can be customized to suit their texture and face shape. Whether you prefer a sleek and straight bob or a curly and voluminous one, this style can be easily achieved with the right tools and products.

The Mohawk

The Mohawk is a bold and unconventional hairstyle that allows you to showcase your creativity. This style involves shaving the hair on the sides and leaving a strip of hair in the middle, which can be styled in various ways. From braided Mohawks to curly Mohawks, there are endless options to explore. The Mohawk is perfect for those who want to make a statement and stand out from the crowd.

The Finger Coils

Finger coils are a popular styling option for short natural hair, especially for those with tight curls or coils. This technique involves coiling small sections of hair around your finger, creating defined and spiral curls. Finger coils can last for several days and can be easily refreshed with water and a leave-in conditioner. This style is perfect for those who want to enhance their natural texture and create a polished look.

The Natural Updo

If you're looking for a more formal and elegant hairstyle, the natural updo is a great option. This style involves gathering the hair at the crown of the head and creating a stylish updo. From braided updos to twisted updos, there are countless ways to rock this hairstyle. The natural updo is perfect for special occasions or when you want to elevate your look.
