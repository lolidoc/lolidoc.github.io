Having long, thick, wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have voluminous locks, it can also be challenging to find hairstyles that are quick and easy to do. Many hairstyles require a lot of time and effort, which can be frustrating for those with busy schedules.

Cute Easy Hairstyles for Long Thick Wavy Hair

1. Braided Half-Updo: This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or even a special occasion. Simply take two sections of hair from the front and create two braids. Then, secure the braids at the back of your head with bobby pins. Leave the rest of your hair down for a soft and romantic look.

2. Messy Bun: The messy bun is a classic and timeless hairstyle that looks great on everyone. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around the base. Secure with bobby pins, leaving a few strands loose for a relaxed and effortless vibe.

3. Beach Waves: Embrace your natural waves with this low-maintenance hairstyle. Apply a texturizing spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat. The result is effortless and beachy waves that are perfect for any occasion.

4. Side Braid: This hairstyle is simple yet elegant. Gather all your hair to one side and create a loose braid. Secure with a hair tie or ribbon for a feminine touch. This hairstyle is great for a day at the office or a night out with friends.

Tips for Cute Easy Hairstyles for Long Thick Wavy Hair

1. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. This will help prevent breakage and minimize frizz.

How to Style Cute Easy Hairstyles for Long Thick Wavy Hair
