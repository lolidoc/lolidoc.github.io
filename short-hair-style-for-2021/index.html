Short Hair Style for 2021: Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you rock your short hair style in 2021:

1. Embrace your natural texture: Short hair is a great way to showcase your natural texture and add volume to your hair. Embrace your curls, waves, or straight hair and work with what you've got.

2. Use the right products: Invest in quality hair products that are designed for short hair. This can include texturizing sprays, volumizing mousses, and styling creams. These products can help you achieve your desired look and make your short hair style last longer.

3. Experiment with accessories: Accessories can be a fun and easy way to change up your short hair style. Try adding a headband, hair clips, or a stylish hat to add a pop of color or texture to your look.

4. Maintain regular trims: Short hair styles require regular trims to keep them looking fresh and stylish. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your short hair style in shape.
