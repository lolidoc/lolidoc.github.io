The History and Myth of Woman's Short Haircut

Short haircuts have a long and fascinating history. In ancient times, short hair was often associated with power and strength. Women would cut their hair short to signify their independence and assertiveness. In more recent history, short haircuts became popular during the 1920s and 1960s, reflecting the changing roles of women in society.
