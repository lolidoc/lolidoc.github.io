Understanding Your Undertones

Before diving into the quiz, it's essential to understand your undertones. Undertones refer to the subtle hues beneath the surface of your skin that can greatly impact how different hair colors look on you. Whether you have warm, cool, or neutral undertones can help guide you towards the most flattering hair color.

Warm Undertones

If you have warm undertones, your skin has a golden or peachy hue. Hair colors like rich browns, warm blondes, and fiery reds will complement your complexion best. Think caramel highlights or auburn shades for a vibrant and radiant look.

Cool Undertones

Cool undertones are characterized by pink or blue hues in your skin. If this sounds like you, consider hair colors like platinum blondes, ash browns, and cool-toned reds. These colors will create a striking contrast and enhance your natural features.

Neutral Undertones

Individuals with neutral undertones have a balance of warm and cool tones in their skin. This means you have the flexibility to experiment with various hair colors. From warm chocolates to cool burgundies, the options are endless.

Warm Undertones

Cool Undertones

For those with cool undertones, consider trying icy platinum, ash blonde, or a rich burgundy. These colors will bring out the coolness in your complexion and make a bold statement.

Neutral Undertones

If you have neutral undertones, lucky you! You can experiment with a wide range of colors, from warm caramel highlights to cool chocolate browns. The choice is yours, and the possibilities are endless.
