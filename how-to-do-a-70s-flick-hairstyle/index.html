How To Do A 70S Flick Hairstyle In 2023

Introduction

The 70s flick hairstyle is a timeless and iconic look that has made a comeback in recent years. This hairstyle, characterized by its voluminous and playful flicks, can add a touch of retro glamour to any modern outfit. In this tutorial, we will guide you through the steps to achieve the perfect 70s flick hairstyle in 2023.

Preparation

Before you begin styling your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra body and texture. Towel-dry your hair gently, avoiding any rough movements that may cause frizz.

Step 1: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to shield it from the damaging effects of heat styling tools. This will help to maintain the health and integrity of your hair while achieving the desired look.

Step 2: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, focusing on the roots to create maximum volume. Lift the roots as you blow dry to add height and body. Once your hair is dry, use a wide-toothed comb to gently tease the roots for extra volume.

Creating the Flicks

Now that your hair is prepped and ready, it's time to create the signature flicks of the 70s hairstyle.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections, starting from the bottom. Use hair clips or ties to hold each section in place while you work on the others. This will make it easier to achieve the flicks evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Curling Iron

Take a curling iron with a medium-sized barrel and wrap a section of hair around it, starting from the mid-lengths. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process for each section of hair, alternating the direction of the curls to create a natural-looking flick.

Step 5: Brush Out

Once all sections are curled, use a paddle brush or wide-toothed comb to gently brush out the curls. This will blend them together and create soft, bouncy flicks.

Finishing Touches

To complete the 70s flick hairstyle, there are a few finishing touches you can add.

Step 6: Hair Spray

Mist your hair with a flexible hold hairspray to set the flicks in place and prevent any frizz. This will ensure that your hairstyle lasts throughout the day.

Step 7: Add Accessories

For an extra touch of 70s glam, you can add accessories such as headbands, hair scarves, or flower clips. These will complement the flicks and enhance the retro vibe of your hairstyle.

Conclusion

The 70s flick hairstyle is a fun and versatile look that can be easily achieved in 2023. By following these steps and adding your own personal flair, you can rock this iconic hairstyle and turn heads wherever you go.
