Q: What styling products should I use for thin curly hair?

A: For thin curly hair, it's best to use lightweight styling products that won't weigh your curls down. Mousse, curl-enhancing creams, and lightweight gels are great options to define your curls without making them appear flat.

Q: How often should I trim my thin curly hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your thin curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its health and prevent split ends. Regular trims can also help add volume and movement to your hair.
