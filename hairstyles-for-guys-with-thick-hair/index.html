Hairstyles For Guys With Thick Hair Stay Stylish In 2023

Written by Exel Jan 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Having thick hair is a blessing as it provides endless styling options and versatility Whether you prefer a classic or trendy look there are numerous hairstyles that can complement your thick locks and enhance your overall appearance In this article we will explore some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick hair in 2023

Table of Contents

Introduction: Embrace Your Thick Hair

Having thick hair is a blessing, as it provides endless styling options and versatility. Whether you prefer a classic or trendy look, there are numerous hairstyles that can complement your thick locks and enhance your overall appearance. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for guys with thick hair in 2023.

1. The Modern Pompadour

The modern pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that continues to be popular in 2023. This style features short sides with longer hair on top, which is swept back and styled with a strong hold pomade. The height and volume of the pompadour add a touch of sophistication to your look, making it perfect for both formal and casual occasions.

2. The Textured Crop

If you prefer a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut, the textured crop is an excellent choice. This haircut involves keeping the hair on top short and textured, while the sides are tapered for a clean and neat appearance. The textured crop works well with thick hair as it adds definition and volume to your locks.

3. The Undercut

The undercut is a versatile hairstyle that suits guys with thick hair. This haircut involves keeping the sides and back short while leaving the hair on top longer. You can style the top hair in various ways, such as slicking it back, creating a pompadour, or even sporting a messy look. The contrast between the short sides and the voluminous top creates a bold and edgy appearance.

4. The Quiff

The quiff is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. This look involves keeping the hair on top longer and styled upward and backward to create a voluminous front. The quiff works exceptionally well with thick hair, as it adds height and texture to your locks. To achieve this look, use a strong-hold product and a hairdryer for added volume.

5. The Slicked-Back Style

If you prefer a polished and sophisticated look, the slicked-back style is an ideal choice. This hairstyle involves combing back your thick hair using a high-shine pomade or gel. The slicked-back style works well for both formal and casual occasions, adding a touch of elegance to your overall appearance.

6. The Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that allows you to experiment with your thick hair. This look involves keeping the hair on the sides shorter while leaving the hair on top longer. Style the top hair upward and slightly backward to create a faux hawk effect. This edgy hairstyle is perfect for guys who want to make a bold statement.

7. The Buzz Cut

If you prefer a low-maintenance and practical hairstyle, the buzz cut is a great option. This style involves cutting your hair very short all over for a clean and minimalistic look. The buzz cut works well with thick hair, as it showcases your natural texture and thickness.

8. The Medium Length Waves

If you want to embrace the natural texture of your thick hair, consider growing it out to a medium length. This allows your hair to form natural waves, creating a relaxed and effortless look. Use a lightweight styling product to enhance the waves and keep your hair looking fresh and tousled.

9. The Side Part

The side part is a classic and sophisticated hairstyle that suits guys with thick hair. This look involves creating a deep side part and combing your hair to one side for a polished appearance. The side part works well for both formal and casual occasions, adding a touch of elegance to your overall look.

10. The Long and Layered

If you're willing to embrace longer hair, the long and layered style is a great choice. This look involves growing your hair out and adding layers to enhance movement and texture. The long and layered style works well with thick hair, as it allows you to showcase your natural thickness and create a relaxed and effortless look.

In conclusion, guys with thick hair have a wide range of hairstyles to choose from in 2023. Whether you prefer a classic, trendy, or low-maintenance look, there is a hairstyle that can complement your thick locks and enhance your overall style. Experiment with different styles, consult with a professional hairstylist, and find the perfect hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle. Embrace your thick hair and stay stylish throughout the year!
