Short Hair Styles 2021 for Fat Faces: How to Choose the Right Style

Choosing the right short hair style for a fat face requires considering various factors, such as face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect style:

1. Determine your face shape: Before choosing a haircut, determine your face shape. Common face shapes include round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Different styles suit different face shapes, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape.

2. Consider your hair texture: Your hair texture plays a significant role in determining the best short hair style for you. If you have thick hair, you can try a pixie cut or a layered bob. If you have thin hair, a sleek bob or a lob with layers can add volume and movement to your hair.

3. Reflect your personal style: Your personal style should be reflected in your haircut. If you prefer a bold and edgy look, you can opt for a pixie cut with shaved sides. If you prefer a more feminine and romantic look, a layered bob with soft curls can be a great choice.

4. Consult with a professional: When in doubt, consult with a professional hairstylist. They have the expertise and experience to guide you in choosing the right short hair style for your fat face. They can also provide valuable tips and advice on how to maintain and style your new haircut.
