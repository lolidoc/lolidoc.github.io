Are you tired of struggling to achieve the perfect space buns with your short, thick hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we will walk you through step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the coveted space buns hairstyle with ease.

Many individuals with short, thick hair often face challenges when attempting to create space buns. The lack of length and the density of the hair can make it difficult to achieve the desired look. However, with the right technique and a few tips and tricks, you can rock space buns effortlessly.

To create space buns with short, thick hair, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prep and Brush

Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a smooth finish.

Step 2: Part your hair

Using a comb, create a middle parting in your hair from the front to the back of your head. This will divide your hair into two equal sections.

Step 3: Secure each section

Take one section of hair and begin twisting it tightly from the base all the way to the ends. Once you've reached the end, coil the twisted hair into a bun shape and secure it with bobby pins. Repeat this step with the other section of hair.

Step 4: Finishing touches

Once both buns are secured, use your fingers to gently loosen the hair around the buns to create a fuller and more voluminous look. You can also pull out a few face-framing strands of hair to soften the style.

In conclusion, creating space buns with short, thick hair is entirely possible with the right technique. By following these steps and using a few styling tricks, you can achieve the space buns hairstyle effortlessly.

History and Myth of Space Buns

The space buns hairstyle has gained popularity in recent years, but its origins can be traced back to various cultures throughout history. In ancient Egypt, for example, women would wear a similar hairstyle called the "double bun." This style was often adorned with beads and other decorative accessories.

In modern times, the space buns hairstyle has become a staple in pop culture. It has been worn by celebrities, such as Princess Leia in Star Wars and Miley Cyrus during her Bangerz era. The hairstyle is often associated with a fun and playful aesthetic.

The Hidden Secret of Space Buns

The secret to achieving perfect space buns lies in the preparation and technique used. Here are some hidden secrets to help you master the space buns hairstyle:

1. Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add grip and volume to your hair before styling. This will help the buns hold their shape and prevent them from slipping out.

2. Tease your hair at the roots before creating the buns. This will add extra volume and make your buns appear fuller.

3. Don't be afraid to play around with the placement of your buns. You can position them high on your head for a more playful look or lower for a sleeker, more sophisticated style.

Recommendations for Space Buns with Short Thick Hair

If you have short, thick hair and want to achieve the perfect space buns, here are some recommendations to consider:

1. Invest in high-quality bobby pins that can hold your hair securely in place. Look for ones that match your hair color to create a seamless finish.

3. Consider adding extensions or using a hairpiece to add length and volume to your hair. This can make it easier to create fuller and more voluminous space buns.

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect space buns with short, thick hair:

Common Questions About Space Buns with Short Thick Hair

Q: Can I achieve space buns with shoulder-length hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve space buns with shoulder-length hair. Use hair extensions or a hair donut to add volume and length to your buns.

Q: How long does it take to create space buns?

A: The time it takes to create space buns depends on your hair length and thickness. On average, it can take anywhere from 10-30 minutes.

Q: Can I sleep with space buns?

A: Yes, you can sleep with space buns to preserve the hairstyle overnight. Make sure to secure the buns with bobby pins and use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools to create space buns?

A: While heat styling tools can be used to curl or straighten your hair before creating space buns, it's best to avoid applying direct heat to the buns themselves to prevent damage.
