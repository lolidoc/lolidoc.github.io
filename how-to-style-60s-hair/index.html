Are you a fan of the iconic 60s look? Do you want to recreate the fabulous hairstyles of that era? Well, you're in luck! In this blog post, we will explore how to style 60s hair and achieve that timeless, retro look.

The 60s was a decade known for its bold fashion and hairstyles. Women in this era embraced voluminous and glamorous hair, often styled into bouffants, beehives, and sleek updos. However, achieving these styles can be challenging, especially if you're not familiar with the techniques and products used back then.

If you're wondering how to style 60s hair, we've got you covered. Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve that vintage look:

1. Start with the Right Tools

To achieve authentic 60s hairstyles, you'll need the right tools. Invest in a good teasing comb, hot rollers, and large barrel curling iron. These tools will help you create the volume and curls necessary for those iconic looks.

2. Tease for Volume

One of the key elements of 60s hair is volume. To achieve this, tease your hair at the crown using a teasing comb. Start by sectioning off the top layer of your hair and gently backcomb each section at the roots. This will create the height and volume required for many 60s hairstyles.

3. Experiment with Updos

Updos were a popular choice for women in the 60s. From sleek chignons to beehives, there are plenty of options to choose from. To create an updo, gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with bobby pins. You can also add accessories like headbands or scarves for an extra retro touch.

4. Embrace Big, Bouncy Curls

Curls were a staple of 60s hairstyles. To achieve those big, bouncy curls, use hot rollers or a large barrel curling iron. Wrap small sections of hair around the roller or curling iron and hold for a few seconds. Once your hair has cooled down, gently brush out the curls for a softer, more natural look.

5. Finish with Hairspray

To ensure your 60s hairstyle stays in place, finish off with a generous amount of hairspray. Choose a strong hold hairspray to keep your hair looking fabulous all day long. Don't be afraid to use a little extra to achieve that perfect 60s hold.

Conclusion of how to style 60s hair

Styling your hair in a 60s-inspired look can be a fun and creative way to change up your hairstyle. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve that retro, glamorous look. Whether you're attending a themed party or simply want to add a touch of vintage charm to your everyday style, embracing the hairstyles of the 60s is a great choice. So go ahead, grab your teasing comb and hairspray, and start recreating those fabulous 60s hairstyles!
