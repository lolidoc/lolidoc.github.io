How to Cut Hair Boy Short: A Step-by-Step Guide

When it comes to cutting hair boy short, it's important to have a step-by-step guide to follow. Here is a detailed process to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

1. Start by wetting your boy's hair to make it easier to work with. Use a spray bottle or simply wet the hair under the faucet.

2. Comb the wet hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to section off the hair and ensure an even cut.

3. Use a comb to section off the hair into smaller sections. Start from the back and work your way towards the front.

4. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Use the scissors to trim the hair to the desired length. Remember to start with small amounts and trim gradually.

5. Continue cutting the hair in small sections, moving from the back to the front. Be sure to constantly check the length and style using a mirror.

6. Once you've finished cutting all the sections, comb through the hair to remove any loose hairs or clippings.

7. Style the hair as desired using a comb, gel, or other styling products. You can experiment with different styles to find the one that suits your boy's face shape and features.

8. Finally, clean up any stray hairs or uneven patches by using the scissors to make small corrections.
