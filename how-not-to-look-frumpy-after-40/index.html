When I turned 40, I was determined to maintain a stylish and fashionable look. I didn't want to become one of those women who let themselves go after a certain age. This helped me create a more streamlined wardrobe that consisted of timeless pieces that could be mixed and matched.

Next, I invested in a few key pieces that flattered my body and made me feel confident. This included a well-fitted blazer, a pair of dark wash jeans, and a versatile little black dress. These pieces became the foundation of my wardrobe, and I built around them with accessories and statement pieces.

I also learned the importance of dressing for my shape. As I got older, my body changed, and I had to adjust my style accordingly. I found that A-line dresses and skirts were incredibly flattering on my hourglass figure, while high-waisted pants and tops accentuated my waistline.

Another important aspect of not looking frumpy after 40 is taking care of your skin and hair. As we age, our skin loses elasticity and our hair becomes thinner and more brittle.
