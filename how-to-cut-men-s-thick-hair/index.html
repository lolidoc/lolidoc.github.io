How To Cut Men s Thick Hair A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by Alexa May 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse for men While it provides volume and texture it can also be challenging to manage and style However with the right techniques and tools cutting men s thick hair can be a breeze In this article we will provide a comprehensive guide on how to cut men s thick hair in 2023 ensuring a stylish and well groomed look

Table of Contents

Introduction

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. While it provides volume and texture, it can also be challenging to manage and style. However, with the right techniques and tools, cutting men's thick hair can be a breeze. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide on how to cut men's thick hair in 2023, ensuring a stylish and well-groomed look.

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting, it's essential to gather the necessary tools. For cutting men's thick hair, you'll need:

1. Clippers

Invest in a high-quality pair of clippers with various guard sizes. This will allow you to achieve different lengths and textures.

2. Scissors

A good pair of barber scissors will be needed to trim and shape the hair precisely.

3. Comb

Get a wide-toothed comb for detangling and parting the hair.

4. Mirror

A handheld mirror or a wall-mounted mirror will help you see the back of the head while cutting.

5. Hair Products

Have some styling products like pomade, wax, or gel on hand to finish the haircut and style it.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before starting the haircut, ensure that the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair tends to shrink and may result in an inaccurate cut.

2. Determine the Desired Length

Consult with the person whose hair you are cutting to determine the desired length and style. This will help you decide on the appropriate guard size for the clippers.

3. Start with the Sides and Back

Using the clippers with the chosen guard size, start cutting the sides and back of the head first. Work your way up, going against the hair's growth for a more even cut.

4. Blend the Top and Sides

Once the sides and back are trimmed, remove the guard from the clippers and use them to blend the top with the sides. Be careful not to take off too much hair in this step.

5. Use Scissors for Texture and Detailing

Switch to the scissors to add texture and detail to the haircut. Point-cutting and texturizing techniques will help remove bulk and create a more natural look.

6. Trim the Bangs and Hairline

Trim the bangs and hairline to ensure a clean and polished look. Use scissors or clippers with a smaller guard size for precision.

7. Check for Symmetry

Regularly check the haircut from different angles to ensure symmetry. Make any necessary adjustments to create a balanced look.

8. Style the Hair

Apply the desired styling product to achieve the desired look. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for the person's hair type and style.

9. Clean Up

After the haircut, use a brush or comb to remove any loose hair. Offer a neck trim or a hot towel service to give the person a complete grooming experience.

10. Maintenance and Aftercare

Advise the person on how to maintain and care for their haircut at home. Recommend a suitable shampoo, conditioner, and any additional hair care products to keep their thick hair healthy and manageable.

Conclusion

Cutting men's thick hair requires the right tools, techniques, and attention to detail. By following this comprehensive guide, you can confidently tackle thick haircuts in 2023. Remember to communicate with the person whose hair you are cutting and adapt the techniques to their individual needs. With practice and experience, you'll become a pro at cutting men's thick hair and ensuring a stylish and trendy look every time.
