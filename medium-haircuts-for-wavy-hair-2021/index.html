Having medium hair with natural waves can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have the versatility of being able to wear your hair straight or curly. When it comes to medium haircuts for wavy hair, there are a few pain points that people often face. One common struggle is finding a haircut that doesn't make the hair look too bulky or frizzy. Another pain point is finding a style that is easy to maintain and doesn't require too much styling every day.

If you're looking for a medium haircut that suits your wavy hair, consider opting for layers. Layers help to add movement and texture to your hair, making it easier to style and manage. They also help to reduce bulkiness and prevent your waves from looking weighed down.

In summary, the best medium haircuts for wavy hair in 2021 are those that embrace the natural texture of your hair and enhance your waves. Layered cuts are a great option as they add movement and reduce bulkiness. Now, let's dive deeper into the world of medium haircuts for wavy hair in 2021.

Medium Haircuts for Wavy Hair 2021: Enhancing Your Natural Waves

When it comes to medium haircuts for wavy hair in 2021, the goal is to enhance your natural waves and create a flattering shape. One popular option is the long bob, also known as the "lob". This haircut hits just above the shoulders and adds movement and texture to your hair. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or curly.

Another trendy option for medium haircuts for wavy hair in 2021 is the shag haircut. This haircut features short layers throughout the hair, creating a messy and textured look. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks stylish and on-trend.

For those who prefer a more polished look, the collarbone-length haircut is a great choice. This haircut hits just below the collarbone and is perfect for enhancing your waves and creating a sleek and sophisticated look. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or with loose waves.
