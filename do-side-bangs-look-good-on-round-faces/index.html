What Are Side Bangs?

Side bangs, also known as side-swept bangs, are a type of hairstyle where the bangs are styled to one side of the face. They can be cut at various lengths and angles, depending on your preference and face shape. Side bangs can be a versatile option for many different face shapes, including round faces.

History and Myth of Side Bangs

Side bangs have been a popular hairstyle for many years. They were first made popular in the 1960s and 1970s by celebrities like Brigitte Bardot and Farrah Fawcett. Since then, they have remained a trendy and stylish choice for many people.

There is a myth that side bangs are not suitable for round faces. However, this is simply not true. With the right cut and styling, side bangs can enhance your features and flatter your face shape.
