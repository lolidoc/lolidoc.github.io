What is thin hair?

Thin hair refers to hair that has a smaller diameter or fewer hair strands per square inch compared to thicker hair. It can be genetic or caused by factors such as aging, hormonal changes, or damage from styling and heat tools. Thin hair tends to lack volume and body, making it difficult to create certain hairstyles and looks.
