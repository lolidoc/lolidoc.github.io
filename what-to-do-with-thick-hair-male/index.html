What to Do with Thick Hair Male: More Tips

When it comes to managing thick hair, here are some more tips:

1. Don't overwash your hair. Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

2. Use a conditioner. Conditioner helps to moisturize and soften the hair, making it more manageable. Look for a conditioner that is specifically designed for thick or coarse hair.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Excessive heat can damage the hair and make it more prone to frizz and breakage. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

4. Consider getting a professional hair treatment. There are various hair treatments available that can help to tame and manage thick hair. Speak to a hairstylist to find out which treatment is best for you.

What to Do with Thick Hair Male: How To

When it comes to managing and styling thick hair, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips.
  3. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the ends to add texture and definition.
  4. Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair as desired.
