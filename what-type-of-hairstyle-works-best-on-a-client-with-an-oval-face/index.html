Do you have an oval face and are unsure of what type of hairstyle would suit you best? Well, look no further! In this article, we will explore the different types of hairstyles that work best on clients with an oval face shape.

Having an oval face shape is considered a blessing in the world of hairstyling. This face shape is versatile and can pull off various hairstyles with ease. However, it can sometimes be overwhelming to choose the perfect hairstyle that enhances your features and complements your face shape.

The best type of hairstyle for a client with an oval face is one that highlights their natural features and balances out the proportions of their face. The oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin and the cheekbones being the widest part of the face.

To enhance the natural beauty of an oval face, it is recommended to go for hairstyles that frame the face and accentuate the cheekbones. Some popular hairstyles that work well on clients with an oval face include layered cuts, long bobs, side-swept bangs, and soft waves. These styles add dimension and movement to the hair, while also drawing attention to the client's best features.

Layered Cuts:

Layered cuts are a great option for clients with an oval face as they add texture and volume to the hair. By adding layers, the hair appears fuller and creates a sense of movement. This style works well with both long and short hair, providing versatility and options for different occasions.

Long Bobs:

Long bobs, also known as "lobs," are another excellent choice for clients with an oval face shape. This hairstyle falls just above the shoulders and can be customized to suit individual preferences. Lobs frame the face beautifully and draw attention to the cheekbones, creating a flattering and stylish look.

Side-Swept Bangs:

Side-swept bangs are a popular choice for clients with an oval face as they add softness and dimension to the hair. Bangs that are swept to the side create a diagonal line that helps to balance out the proportions of the face. This style works well with both short and long hair, giving clients the option to experiment and find their perfect look.

Soft Waves:

Soft waves are a timeless hairstyle that works well on clients with an oval face shape. This hairstyle adds movement and texture to the hair, creating a romantic and feminine look. Soft waves can be achieved using various styling tools, such as curling irons or rollers, and can be worn for both casual and formal occasions.

Conclusion:

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a client with an oval face shape, the options are endless. Layered cuts, long bobs, side-swept bangs, and soft waves are just a few of the many hairstyles that work well on this face shape. The key is to find a style that enhances the client's natural features and balances out the proportions of their face. By considering these factors, clients with an oval face can confidently rock any hairstyle they choose!

Question and Answer

Q: Can clients with an oval face shape pull off short hairstyles?

A: Absolutely! Clients with an oval face shape can pull off short hairstyles with ease. Short hairstyles, such as pixie cuts or bob cuts, can highlight the client's facial features and add a touch of sophistication to their overall look.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that clients with an oval face shape should avoid?

A: While clients with an oval face shape have the freedom to experiment with various hairstyles, it is best to avoid hairstyles that add excessive volume to the sides of the face. These hairstyles can make the face appear wider and disrupt the balanced proportions of an oval face shape.

Q: Can clients with an oval face shape wear their hair in a middle part?

A: Absolutely! Clients with an oval face shape can wear their hair in a middle part as it helps to create symmetry and balance. However, it is always fun to experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for each individual.

Q: Can clients with an oval face shape wear a blunt fringe?

A: Yes, clients with an oval face shape can pull off a blunt fringe. However, it is important to consider the client's forehead size and the width of their face when choosing a blunt fringe. A softer, side-swept fringe might be a more flattering option for some clients.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, clients with an oval face shape have the advantage of being able to pull off a wide range of hairstyles. Layered cuts, long bobs, side-swept bangs, and soft waves are just a few examples of hairstyles that work well on this face shape. The key is to choose a style that enhances the client's natural features and balances out the proportions of their face. With the right hairstyle, clients with an oval face shape can confidently embrace their beauty and rock any look they desire.
