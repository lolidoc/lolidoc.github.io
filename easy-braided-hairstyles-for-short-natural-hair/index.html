Introduction

Short natural hair is not a limitation when it comes to creating beautiful and intricate braided hairstyles. In fact, with the right techniques and styling products, you can achieve stunning looks that are both stylish and easy to maintain. Whether you're looking for a casual everyday hairstyle or something more glamorous for a special occasion, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore some trendy and easy braided hairstyles for short natural hair in 2023.

The Classic Box Braids

1. Mini Box Braids

Mini box braids are perfect for short natural hair. This style involves creating tiny, thin braids that fall just above the shoulders. They can be left loose or styled into an updo for a more sophisticated look. To achieve this style, start by sectioning your hair and braiding each section tightly from root to tip. Use a lightweight styling gel or edge control to keep the braids neat and in place.

2. Jumbo Box Braids

If you prefer a bolder and more statement-making look, jumbo box braids are a great option. These braids are thicker and longer, reaching mid-back or even waist-length. Jumbo box braids can be achieved by using synthetic hair extensions or by adding volume to your natural hair. This style is versatile and can be worn in various ways, such as a high ponytail or a half-up, half-down style.

The Chic Cornrows

1. Side Cornrows

Side cornrows are a trendy and stylish option for short natural hair. This style involves braiding small sections of hair close to the scalp, starting from one side and moving towards the other. You can create a single row of cornrows or multiple rows, depending on the desired look. Side cornrows can be left as is or styled into a chic updo or ponytail.

2. Zigzag Cornrows

If you're looking for a more unique and edgy look, zigzag cornrows are a great choice. This style involves creating cornrows in a zigzag pattern across the scalp. This adds visual interest and dimension to your hairstyle. Zigzag cornrows can be worn as a standalone style or combined with other braiding techniques, such as box braids or twists, for a more intricate look.

The Stylish Twists

1. Mini Twists

Mini twists are a popular choice for short natural hair as they are easy to create and maintain. This style involves twisting small sections of hair from root to tip, creating a spiral-like effect. Mini twists can be left as is or styled into an updo or ponytail. They can also be accessorized with beads or hair jewelry for added flair.

2. Chunky Twists

If you prefer a more voluminous and textured look, chunky twists are a great option. This style involves twisting larger sections of hair, resulting in thicker and more defined twists. Chunky twists can be worn loose or styled into an updo, bun, or ponytail. They provide a great foundation for various hairstyles and can be accessorized with headbands or scarves.

Conclusion

Short natural hair doesn't have to limit your hairstyle options. With the right techniques and creativity, you can achieve beautiful and trendy braided hairstyles. Whether you opt for classic box braids, chic cornrows, or stylish twists, there are endless possibilities to explore. Experiment with different styles, accessories, and hair products to find what works best for you. Embrace your natural hair and rock those braids with confidence!
