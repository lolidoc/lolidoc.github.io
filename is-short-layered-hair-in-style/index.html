How to Style Short Layered Hair

Styling short layered hair is relatively easy and requires minimal effort. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your short layered hair:

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture. 2. Towel-dry your hair gently to remove excess water. 3. Apply a small amount of volumizing mousse or spray to your roots to add lift and texture. 4. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to style your hair, focusing on adding volume to the roots and creating movement with the layers. 5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand or flat iron to add waves or curls to the ends of your hair for added texture. 6. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to lock in your style and add shine.

Question and Answer

Q: Will short layered hair suit my face shape?
A: Short layered hair can be customized to suit any face shape. Your hairstylist can help you determine the best length and layers for your face shape.

Q: How often do I need to trim my short layered hair?
A: It's recommended to trim your short layered hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent any split ends.

Q: Can I still curl my short layered hair?
A: Yes, you can definitely curl your short layered hair. Use a curling wand or flat iron to create different textures and waves.

Q: Will short layered hair make my hair look thinner?
A: No, short layered hair can actually create the illusion of thickness and volume. The layers add texture and movement to your hair, making it appear fuller.
