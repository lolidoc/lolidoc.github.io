How to Style Short Hairstyles for Naturally Wavy Hair Over 50

Styling short hairstyles for naturally wavy hair over 50 is relatively easy. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair to define your waves and reduce frizz.

2. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your waves and add volume to your hair.

3. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define your waves. Curl random sections of hair to create a tousled and textured look.

4. Finish off your hairstyle with a texturizing spray or light-hold hairspray to add texture and hold.
