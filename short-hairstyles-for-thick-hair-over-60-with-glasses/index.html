How to Style Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair Over 60 with Glasses

Styling short hairstyles for thick hair over 60 with glasses is easy and effortless. Here's how you can do it:

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will add texture and volume to your hair.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a mousse or volumizing spray, to add extra volume and hold.

3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to lift the roots and add volume to your hair. Focus on the crown area to create height.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or a straightener to add texture and style to your hair. Create loose waves or curls for a more glamorous look.

5. Finish off with a hairspray to set the style and add hold.
