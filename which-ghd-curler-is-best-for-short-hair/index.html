How to Use the ghd Curve Creative Curl Wand

Using the ghd Curve Creative Curl Wand is easy. Follow these steps:

1. Start by plugging in the curl wand and turning it on. Allow it to heat up for a few seconds.

2. Section your hair and clip up the top layers to keep them out of the way.

3. Take a small section of hair and hold the wand with the tip facing downward.

4. Wrap the hair around the wand, starting at the base and leaving the ends out for a natural look.

5. Hold for a few seconds and then release the curl.

6. Repeat this process for the rest of your hair, working in small sections.

7. Once all the curls are done, allow them to cool for a few minutes.

8. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently separate and tousle the curls for a more relaxed look.
