How To Tell If A Hair Color Suits You

Written by Jhonny May 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's important to find a shade that suits your skin tone and enhances your natural features. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks to help you determine if a hair color suits you.

Understanding Your Skin Undertone

The first step in finding a hair color that suits you is understanding your skin undertone. There are three main undertones: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool undertones have pink or blue undertones, warm undertones have yellow or peach undertones, and neutral undertones have a mix of both. Determining your skin undertone will guide you in choosing the right hair color.

Testing Your Undertone

To determine your undertone, you can try the vein test. Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't determine the color, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color suits you. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop and enhance their natural beauty. For example, if you have blue eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde or copper can complement your eyes.

Complementary Colors

Understanding the color wheel can help you choose a hair color that complements your eye color. Colors opposite each other on the color wheel are considered complementary. For example, if you have green eyes, red or copper hair colors can make your eyes stand out.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can provide clues as to which shades will suit you best. If you have warm undertones and a natural hair color with red or golden hues, opting for a hair color in the same range can enhance your natural beauty.

Gradual Color Changes

If you're unsure about making a drastic change, you can opt for gradual color changes. For example, if you have dark brown hair, you can start by adding subtle highlights or lowlights in a shade that complements your skin tone.

Seek Professional Advice

If you're still unsure about which hair color suits you, it's always a good idea to seek professional advice. Hair stylists are trained to analyze your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color to recommend the best hair color that suits you.

Consultation with a Colorist

Schedule a consultation with a professional colorist who can assess your features and provide personalized recommendations. They have a deep understanding of different hair colors and can guide you in making the right choice.

Try Virtual Hair Color Tools

If you want to get an idea of how a certain hair color will look on you before making a commitment, try using virtual hair color tools. These tools allow you to upload a photo and try different hair colors virtually, giving you a glimpse of how they will suit you.

Online Hair Color Simulators

There are numerous online hair color simulators available that can help you visualize different shades on your hair. Experiment with various colors to see which ones flatter your skin tone and make you feel confident.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color is a personal decision, and it's important to consider factors such as skin undertone, eye color, and natural hair color. By understanding these factors and seeking professional advice when needed, you can find a hair color that suits you perfectly and enhances your overall appearance.
