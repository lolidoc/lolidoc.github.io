How to Style Wavy Hair for Round Faces

When it comes to styling wavy hair for round faces, there are many options to choose from. Here are some ideas to get you started:

1. Loose waves: Create loose, natural-looking waves by using a curling wand or curling iron. Wrap sections of hair around the barrel, leaving the ends out for a more relaxed look.

2. Beachy waves: Achieve a beachy, textured look by spritzing sea salt spray onto damp hair and scrunching it with your hands. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to enhance the waves.

3. Defined curls: If you prefer more defined curls, use a curling wand or curling iron to create tight curls. After curling, gently separate the curls with your fingers for a more natural look.

4. Half-up, half-down: Create a chic and stylish look by pulling back the top half of your wavy hair and securing it with a clip or hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down to showcase your waves.
