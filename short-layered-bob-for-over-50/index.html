What is a Short Layered Bob?

A short layered bob is a haircut that features shorter hair at the back of the head and gradually longer hair towards the front. This creates layers and adds movement and texture to the hair. The length of the bob can vary, but it is typically cut above the shoulders. This hairstyle is popular among women of all ages, but it is particularly flattering for women over 50 who want to add volume and body to their hair.

Tips for Styling a Short Layered Bob

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.

2. Apply a lightweight mousse or volumizing spray to damp hair before blow-drying. This will add even more volume and hold to your style.

3. Use a round brush while blow-drying to create volume at the roots and smooth out the ends.

4. For a sleek and polished look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair after blow-drying.

5. To add texture and movement, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.
