Question and Answer

Q: How do I determine my face shape?

A: To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine the outline of your face in the mirror. There are several common face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has different hairstyles that flatter it best.

Q: What if I have thin or fine hair?

A: If you have thin or fine hair, there are several hairstyles that can add volume and texture. Layered cuts, pixie cuts, and bob cuts are all great options for adding the appearance of thickness to thin hair.

Q: Can I change my hairstyle if I don't like it?

A: Absolutely! Hair grows back, and hairstyles can always be changed. If you try a new hairstyle and it doesn't suit your personality or preferences, you can always switch it up or grow it out.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on your hair type and desired style. On average, getting a trim every 6-8 weeks can help maintain the health and shape of your hairstyle.
