The Best Round Brush for Thick Frizzy Hair: Tips and Tricks

When using a round brush on thick, frizzy hair, there are a few tips and tricks that can help you achieve the best results:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair: It's best to use a round brush on damp hair, as it will be more pliable and easier to style.

2. Use a heat protectant: Before applying heat to your hair, always use a heat protectant spray or serum to minimize damage.

3. Section your hair: Working in sections can make it easier to style your hair with a round brush. Start at the bottom and work your way up, focusing on one section at a time.

4. Use the right technique: To achieve smooth, frizz-free hair, hold the brush horizontally and wrap a section of hair around the barrel. Use a hairdryer to apply heat, moving the brush through the hair as you go.
