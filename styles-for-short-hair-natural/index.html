Styles for Short Hair Natural: Maintenance Tips

Maintaining short natural hair is relatively simple, but there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Moisturize regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin bonnet or using a satin pillowcase.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

By following these tips, you can keep your short natural hair healthy and vibrant.
