How to Cut Thinning Hair on Top

When it comes to cutting thinning hair on top, there are a few key considerations to keep in mind. Here are some tips and techniques to help you achieve the best results:

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. This will make it easier to cut and style.

2. Use a comb to section off the top portion of your hair, leaving the sides and back untouched.

3. Decide on the length you want for your hair and use hair clippers or scissors to trim the hair on top. If you're using clippers, start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to achieve the desired length. If you're using scissors, remember to cut small sections at a time and blend them together for a natural look.

4. Once you've cut the hair on top, use a comb to blend the cut hair with the rest of your hair. This will create a seamless and natural-looking result.

5. Style your hair using a product that adds volume and texture. This will help create the illusion of fuller hair.
