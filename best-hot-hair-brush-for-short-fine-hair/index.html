Question and Answer

Q: Can I use a hot hair brush on wet hair?

A: It is not recommended to use a hot hair brush on wet hair, as this can cause damage. It is best to use the hot hair brush on dry hair.

Q: How often should I clean my hot hair brush?

A: It is a good idea to clean your hot hair brush regularly to remove any build-up. Once a month is a good rule of thumb, but you may need to clean it more often if you use a lot of styling products.

Q: Can I use a hot hair brush on thick hair?

A: While hot hair brushes are suitable for most hair types, including thick hair, it may take longer to achieve your desired results. You may need to section your hair and work in smaller sections to ensure that the hot air is evenly distributed.

Q: Can I use a hot hair brush if I have color-treated hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hot hair brush if you have color-treated hair. However, it is important to use a heat protectant spray and to avoid using excessive heat to prevent damage to your color.
