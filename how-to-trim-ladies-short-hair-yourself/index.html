How to Trim Ladies Short Hair Yourself

When it comes to trimming ladies short hair yourself, it's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the length and shape you want to achieve. Begin by sectioning off your hair into manageable sections, using hair clips or elastics to keep them separate.

Next, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold your scissors at an angle, pointing towards the floor, and trim off the desired amount. Be sure to only cut a little at a time, as it's easier to take more off than to add it back on.

Continue working your way around your head, taking small sections and trimming them to your desired length. Remember to check your progress in the mirror frequently to ensure an even cut. Once you have finished trimming all the sections, give your hair a final comb through and style as desired.
