Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are all the rage this year. From the classic bob to edgy pixie cuts, shorter styles are versatile, low maintenance, and can be customized to suit your unique features. One popular short haircut for 2020 is the textured pixie cut. This style adds volume and movement to your hair, creating a playful and youthful look. Another trendy option is the asymmetrical bob, which features a longer length in the front and shorter layers in the back. This style is edgy and modern, perfect for those who want to make a statement.

If you're not ready to commit to a drastic chop, there are also medium length hairstyles that are on-trend for 2020. The lob, or long bob, is a popular choice. It falls just above the shoulders and can be worn straight, wavy, or curled. This versatile style is flattering for all face shapes and can be customized to suit your personal style. Another option is the shag haircut, which features layered, textured ends. This style adds movement and volume to your hair, giving it a laid-back, bohemian vibe.

Long Flowing Waves

For those who prefer longer hair, flowing waves are a popular choice for 2020. This style is effortlessly chic and can be worn for any occasion. To achieve this look, start by curling your hair with a large barrel curling iron or wand. Then, use your fingers to gently tousle the curls and create a natural, lived-in effect. Finish with a spritz of texturizing spray for added hold and volume. If you have naturally straight hair, you can use a flat iron to create loose waves. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
