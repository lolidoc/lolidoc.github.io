How To Control Long Thick Hair For Guys

Introduction

Having long thick hair as a guy can be both a blessing and a challenge. While it allows for a variety of styling options, it can also be difficult to manage and control. If you're struggling to find ways to keep your long thick hair under control, this article is here to help. We'll provide you with tips and techniques to ensure your locks always look their best.

1. Regular Trims

One of the most important steps in controlling long thick hair is to get regular trims. This will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and healthy. Aim to visit a professional stylist every 6-8 weeks to maintain your desired length and shape.

2. Proper Shampooing and Conditioning

Using the right shampoo and conditioner is crucial for managing long thick hair. Look for products specifically designed for your hair type, such as those with moisturizing and strengthening properties. Avoid overwashing your hair, as this can strip away natural oils and leave it dry and frizzy.

3. Towel Drying Techniques

When drying your hair, avoid vigorously rubbing it with a towel, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water and pat your hair dry. If you're in a hurry, opt for a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt, as these are gentler on the hair.

4. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Long thick hair can easily get tangled, so it's essential to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your locks. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots, being gentle to avoid any unnecessary hair breakage.

5. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can work wonders for taming long thick hair. Apply a small amount of product from mid-length to ends to keep your hair moisturized and manageable throughout the day. Look for leave-in conditioners with lightweight formulas to avoid weighing your hair down.

6. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it harder to control. Try to limit the use of hot tools like straighteners and curling irons. If you must use them, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray beforehand to minimize damage.

7. Opt for Updos

Updos are a great way to keep long thick hair under control, especially on busy days. Experiment with different hairstyles like buns, ponytails, or braids. These not only keep your hair in place but also add a stylish touch to your overall look.

8. Embrace Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can be your best friend when it comes to managing long thick hair. Use headbands, hair clips, or bobby pins to keep your hair off your face and prevent it from getting in the way. Choose accessories that complement your style and add a touch of flair.

9. Don't Skip Regular Conditioning Treatments

In addition to using a regular conditioner, treat your long thick hair to deep conditioning treatments every few weeks. This will help nourish and hydrate your locks, making them more manageable and less prone to frizz and breakage.

10. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Instead of constantly fighting against your hair's natural texture, embrace it. Long thick hair often has a beautiful natural wave or curl pattern. Work with your hair's natural texture by using styling products designed to enhance and define your curls or waves.

Conclusion

Controlling long thick hair for guys might require some extra effort, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look. Remember to regularly trim your hair, use proper shampoo and conditioning, and be gentle when drying and combing. Embrace different hairstyles, accessories, and your hair's natural texture to showcase your unique style. With these tips, you'll be able to rock your long thick hair with confidence.
