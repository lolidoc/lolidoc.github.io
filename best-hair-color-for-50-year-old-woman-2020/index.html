Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose a high-quality hair dye. If you're unsure, it's best to consult with a professional hairstylist.

Q: Will dyeing my hair damage it?

A: Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, especially if you're going lighter or using bleach. However, using high-quality products and following a proper hair care routine can help to minimize damage and keep your hair healthy.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: How often you need to touch up your roots will depend on your hair growth rate and the color you choose. On average, most people need to touch up their roots every 4-6 weeks.
