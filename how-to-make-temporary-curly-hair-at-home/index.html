How To Make Temporary Curly Hair At Home

Written by Vannesa Jun 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Having curly hair can add a touch of elegance and glamour to your overall look However not everyone is blessed with natural curls If you re looking to experiment with temporary curly hair you re in luck In this article we ll guide you through some easy and effective methods to achieve temporary curls at home

Table of Contents

Introduction

Having curly hair can add a touch of elegance and glamour to your overall look. However, not everyone is blessed with natural curls. If you're looking to experiment with temporary curly hair, you're in luck! In this article, we'll guide you through some easy and effective methods to achieve temporary curls at home.

1. Braiding Method

One of the simplest ways to create temporary curls is by using the braiding method. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's slightly damp. Then, divide your hair into multiple sections and braid each section tightly. Leave the braids in overnight and undo them in the morning to reveal beautiful, natural-looking curls.

2. Twist and Pin Method

If you're short on time and want quick curls, the twist and pin method is perfect for you. Begin by dividing your hair into small sections. Take each section, twist it tightly, and secure it to your scalp using bobby pins. Leave the twists in for a few hours or overnight, and when you remove the pins, you'll have gorgeous curls.

3. Heatless Curling Rods

If you prefer heatless methods, consider using curling rods. Start by applying a styling mousse or a curl-enhancing product to damp hair. Then, take small sections of your hair and wrap them around the curling rods. Leave the rods in for a few hours or overnight and gently remove them to reveal bouncy curls.

4. Sock Curls

Did you know that you can achieve beautiful curls using just a sock? Cut the toe portion of a clean sock and roll it up into a donut shape. Dampen your hair and divide it into sections. Take each section, twist it, and wrap it around the sock donut. Leave it in overnight and unravel in the morning for soft, voluminous curls.

5. Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a great tool for creating temporary curls. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and divide it into sections. Take a flexi rod and wrap a section of your hair around it from the ends to the roots. Bend the rod to secure it in place. Leave the rods in for a few hours or overnight and remove them to reveal gorgeous, bouncy curls.

6. Paper Towel Method

If you're looking for a DIY alternative to curlers, try using paper towels. Cut a paper towel into long strips and roll them up tightly. Dampen your hair and divide it into sections. Wrap each section around a paper towel strip and secure it with a hair tie. Leave it in overnight and unwrap in the morning to achieve natural-looking curls.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to make temporary curly hair at home, you can experiment with different styles and looks. Remember to be gentle with your hair and use styling products that provide heat protection to avoid any damage. With these methods, you can enjoy beautiful curls whenever you want, without the need for a permanent hairstyle change.
