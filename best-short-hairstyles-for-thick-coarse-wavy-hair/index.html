Best Short Hairstyles For Thick Coarse Wavy Hair In 2023

Introduction

Thick coarse wavy hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right short hairstyle, you can embrace your natural texture and create a stunning look. In this article, we will explore the best short hairstyles for those with thick coarse wavy hair in 2023. Whether you prefer a low-maintenance style or something more edgy and trendy, we have got you covered!

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well with thick coarse wavy hair. This cut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. It is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and stylish. To enhance your natural waves, apply some texturizing spray and scrunch your hair for a tousled look.

2. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers is another excellent option for thick coarse wavy hair. This style adds movement and dimension to your hair, making it easier to manage. The layers can help to reduce bulk and create a more flattering shape. To style, use a curl-enhancing mousse and scrunch your hair while blow-drying to enhance your natural waves.

3. Shaggy Lob

The shaggy lob, also known as the long bob, is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that works well with thick coarse wavy hair. This hairstyle features layers throughout the hair, creating a textured and effortless look. To style, use a sea salt spray to enhance your waves and add some tousled texture.

4. Textured Crop

If you are looking for a bold and edgy style, consider a textured crop. This short hairstyle is cut close to the head and features choppy layers for added texture and movement. To style, apply a styling gel or wax and tousle your hair for a messy and textured look.

5. Curly Pixie

If you have naturally tight and coily waves, a curly pixie cut can be a great option. This short hairstyle embraces your natural texture and creates a stylish and playful look. To enhance your curls, use a curl-defining cream and scrunch your hair while it air-dries.

6. Side-Swept Bangs

For those who want to add some versatility to their short hairstyle, consider adding side-swept bangs. This can soften the look and frame your face beautifully. To style, blow-dry your bangs with a round brush for a smooth and polished finish.

7. Mohawk Fade

If you want to make a bold statement, a mohawk fade can be a great choice. This style features shaved sides and back, with longer hair on top styled into a mohawk. To enhance your waves, apply some texturizing paste and tousle your hair for a messy and textured look.

8. Undercut with Design

If you are feeling adventurous, consider an undercut with a design. This style features shaved sides or back with a unique design carved into the hair. The longer hair on top can be styled into waves or curls for added texture and dimension.

9. Afro Puff

If you have natural afro-textured hair, embrace your curls with an afro puff. This style is simple yet stylish and perfect for showcasing your natural texture. Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail and fluff out your curls for a voluminous look.

10. Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that works well with thick coarse wavy hair. This style features shorter sides and longer hair in the middle styled into a faux hawk shape. To style, apply some pomade or wax and tousle your hair for a textured and messy look.

In conclusion, there are plenty of stylish and trendy short hairstyles for those with thick coarse wavy hair in 2023. From classic pixie cuts to edgy mohawk fades, there is something to suit every personal style. Embrace your natural texture and experiment with different styles to find the perfect short hairstyle for you.
