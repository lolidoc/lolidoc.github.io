Questions and Answers

Q: Are hair extensions safe for older women?

A: Yes, hair extensions are safe for older women. As long as they are applied and maintained properly, hair extensions pose no risk to your natural hair.

Q: How long do hair extensions last?

A: The lifespan of hair extensions can vary depending on the type of extensions and how well they are cared for. On average, hair extensions can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months.

Q: Can I style my hair extensions like my natural hair?

A: Yes, you can style your hair extensions just like your natural hair. They can be washed, blow-dried, curled, and straightened. However, it's important to use heat protectant products and avoid excessive heat to prevent damage.

Q: Can I swim with hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can swim with hair extensions. However, it's important to rinse your hair immediately after swimming in chlorinated or saltwater and avoid exposing your extensions to excessive amounts of chlorine or salt.
